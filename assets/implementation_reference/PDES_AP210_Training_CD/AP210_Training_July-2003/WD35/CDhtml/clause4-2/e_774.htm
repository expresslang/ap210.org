4.2.774 Restraint_condition

NOTE: The text description for this Application Object has not been fully updated to agree with the EXPRESS.
A Restraint_condition is a type of Managed_design_object. A Restraint_condition is the specification of a requirement on the restraining conditions applied either to a Datum_feature or a Physical_feature_or_part_template during the verification of one or more Geometric_tolerance or Dimensional_characteristic. The Restraint_condition may be a Tolerance_specific_restraint_condition. If the Restraint_condition is not a Tolerance_specific_restraint_condition, the Restraint_condition is a requirement for all instances of Geometric_tolerance and Dimensional_characteristic with the exception of those instances of Geometric_tolerance that have a Pre_defined_geometric_tolerance_qualifier applied to them that have a description of "FREE_STATE", and those instances of Dimensional_characteristic that have a Pre_defined_dimension_qualifier applied to them that have a description of "FREE_STATE".

The data associated with this application object are the following:

  • applied_to
  • restraint_description

4.2.774.1 applied_to

The applied_to specifies either a Physical_feature_or_part_template or a Datum_feature_usage_in_datum_relationship. If the applied to specifies a Physical_feature_or_part_template, it is on that Physical_feature_or_part_template that the restraint requirement is applied. If a Datum_feature_usage_in_datum_relationship is specified as the applied_to, the Restraint_condition specifies a restraint requirement on the relationship between the Datum_feature and the Datum specified by the Datum_feature_usage_in_datum_relationship.

4.2.774.2 restraint_description

The restraint_description specifies the Ee_requirement_occurrence that describes how the restraint shall be applied.

EXAMPLE: The Restraint_condition might specify the torque to apply to bolts to secure Datum_feature"A" to inspection equipment that simulates Datum"A".
