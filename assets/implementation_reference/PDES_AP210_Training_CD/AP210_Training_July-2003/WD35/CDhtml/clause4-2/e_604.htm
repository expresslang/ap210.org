4.2.634 Partially_plated_cutout

NOTE: The text description for this Application Object has not been fully updated to agree with the EXPRESS.
A Partially_plated_cutout is a type of Cutout that is not a completely plated cutout for reason of manufacturing capabilities or design purposes.

The data associated with this application object are the following:

  • plated_segment
  • unplated_segment

4.2.634.1 plated_segment

A plated_segment is an inverse relationship that specifies that the existence of the Partially_plated_cutout is dependent on the existence of the Plated_cutout_edge_segment that specifies the Partially_plated_cutout as a composed_cutout. There shall be one or more Plated_cutout_edge_segment in the role of plated_segment for the Partially_plated_cutout. The plated_segment specifies the segment that is plated.

4.2.634.2 unplated_segment

An unplated_segment is an inverse relationship that specifies that the existence of the Partially_plated_cutout is dependent on the existence of the Cutout_edge_segment that specifies the Partially_plated_cutout as a composed_cutout. There shall be one or more Cutout_edge_segment in the role of unplated_segment for the Partially_plated_cutout. The unplated_segment specifies the segment that is unplated.

Formal constraints:

WR1: A Plated_cutout_segment shall not be in the role of unplated_segment.

ENTITY partially_plated_cutout
  SUBTYPE OF (cutout);
INVERSE
  plated_segment   : SET [1:?] OF cutout_edge_segment FOR composed_cutout;
  unplated_segment : SET [1:?] OF cutout_edge_segment FOR composed_cutout;
END_ENTITY; -- partially_plated_cutout
