4.2.915 Tolerance_zone_boundary

NOTE: The text description for this Application Object has not been fully updated to agree with the EXPRESS.
A Tolerance_zone_boundary is a type of Non_feature_shape_element that is a boundary of a Tolerance_zone. A Tolerance_zone_boundary is one of a Conical_tolerance_zone_boundary, a Tolerance_zone_circular_or_cylindrical_or_spherical_boundary, or a Tolerance_zone_opposing_boundary_set.

EXAMPLE: A pair of opposing planes may form a Tolerance_zone_boundary.

The data associated with this application object are the following:

  • boundary_zone_definition
  • bounded_zone

4.2.915.1 boundary_zone_definition

The boundary_zone_definition is an inverse relationship that specifies that the existence of the Tolerance_zone_boundary is dependent on the existence of the Tolerance_zone_boundary_definition that specifies the Tolerance_zone_boundary as its defined_zone_boundary.

4.2.915.2 bounded_zone

The bounded_zone is an inverse relationship that specifies that the existence of the Tolerance_zone_boundary is dependent on the existence of the Tolerance_zone that specifies the Tolerance_zone_boundary as its zone_boundary.

ENTITY tolerance_zone_boundary
  ABSTRACT SUPERTYPE OF (ONEOF (
      tolerance_zone_circular_or_cylindrical_or_spherical_boundary,
      tolerance_zone_opposing_boundary_set,
      conical_tolerance_zone_boundary))
  SUBTYPE OF (non_feature_shape_element);
  INVERSE
    bounded_zone             : tolerance_zone FOR zone_boundary;
    boundary_zone_definition : tolerance_zone_boundary_definition FOR
