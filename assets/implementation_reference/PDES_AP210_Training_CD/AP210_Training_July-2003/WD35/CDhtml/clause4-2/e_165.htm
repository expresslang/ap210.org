4.2.166 Composite_surface_profile_refinement_tolerance

NOTE: The text description for this Application Object has not been fully updated to agree with the EXPRESS.
A Composite_surface_profile_refinement_tolerance is a type of Profile_of_any_surface_tolerance that is used in conjunction with a Composite_surface_profile_locating_tolerance and possibly two other Composite_surface_profile_refinement_tolerance to apply a more restrictive specification on the form, orientation, and possibly size of a Physical_feature_or_part_template than on the location of the Physical_feature_or_part_template. If the Composite_surface_profile_refinement_tolerance is also a Profile_of_any_surface_tolerance_with_referenced_datum_system the defined Tolerance_zone is oriented but not located relative to the Datum_system the definition of which is specified as the datum_system_definition.

NOTE: The attribute datum_system_definition is declared in the Geometric_tolerance_with_referenced_datum_system entity of which this entity is a subtype.

The data associated with this application object are the following:

  • refined_control

4.2.166.1 refined_control

The refined_control specifies either a Composite_surface_profile_locating_tolerance or another Composite_surface_profile_refinement_tolerance to which this Composite_surface_profile_refinement_tolerance assists in specifying design requirements by specifying a further refinement of the form, orientation, and possibly size of the Physical_feature_or_part_template specified as the toleranced_shape_element.

NOTE: The attribute toleranced_shape_element is declared in the Geometric_tolerance entity of which this entity is a subtype.

Formal constraints:

WR1: The Physical_feature_or_part_template specified as the toleranced_shape_element by this Composite_surface_profile_refinement_tolerance shall be the same instance of Physical_feature_or_part_template as specified as the toleranced_shape_element by the Profile_of_any_surface_tolerance that is specified as the refined_control.

WR2: If this Composite_surface_profile_refinement_tolerance is also a Profile_of_any_surface_tolerance_without_referenced_datum_system, it shall not be specified as a refined_control by another Composite_surface_profile_refinement_tolerance.

WR3: If this Composite_surface_profile_refinement_tolerance specifies a Composite_surface_profile_refinement_tolerance as the refined_control, this Composite_surface_profile_refinement_tolerance shall either be a Profile_of_any_surface_tolerance_without_referenced_datum_system or have less Datum in its Datum_system than the Datum_system of the Composite_surface_profile_refinement_tolerance specified as the refined_control.

WR4: If this Composite_surface_profile_refinement_tolerance specifies a Composite_surface_profile_locating_tolerance as the refined_control, this Composite_surface_profile_refinement_tolerance shall either be a Profile_of_any_surface_tolerance_without_referenced_datum_system or have less than or an equal number of Datum in its Datum_system than the Datum_system of the Composite_surface_profile_locating_tolerance specified as the refined_control.

WR5: If this Composite_surface_profile_refinement_tolerance is a Profile_of_any_surface_tolerance_with_referenced_datum_system, the Datum in its Datum_system shall be in the Datum_system of the Profile_of_any_surface_tolerance specified as the refined_control and shall be assigned the same datum precedence.

WR6: If this Composite_surface_profile_refinement_tolerance specifies a Profile_of_any_surface_tolerance that specifies a Datum_system_defining_conditions_without_material_conditions as the datum_system_definition, the Profile_of_any_surface_tolerance specified as the refined_control shall also specify a Datum_system_defining_conditions_without_material_conditions as its datum_system_definition.

WR7: If this Composite_surface_profile_refinement_tolerance specifies a Profile_of_any_surface_tolerance that specifies a Datum_system_defining_conditions_with_material_conditions as the datum_system_definition; the Profile_of_any_surface_tolerance specified as the refined_control shall also specify a Datum_system_defining_conditions_with_material_conditions as its datum_system_definition, and each Datum_feature of the Datum_system of this Composite_surface_profile_refinement_tolerance shall be assigned the same material condition property as is assigned to it in the Datum_system of the Profile_of_any_surface_tolerance specified as the refined_control.

WR8: The size of the Tolerance_zone_boundary of this Composite_surface_profile_refinement_tolerance shall be less than the size of the Tolerance_zone_boundary of the Profile_of_any_surface_tolerance specified as the refined_control.

WR9: The Tolerance_zone_boundary of this Composite_surface_profile_refinement_tolerance shall be based on the same True_profile as the Tolerance_zone_boundary of the Profile_of_any_surface_tolerance specified as the refined_control.

WR10: The Composite_surface_profile_refinement_tolerance shall be specified as a refined_control by at most one Composite_surface_profile_refinement_tolerance.
