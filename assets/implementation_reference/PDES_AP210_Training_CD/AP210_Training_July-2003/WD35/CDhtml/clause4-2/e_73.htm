4.2.74 Bonded_conductive_base_blind_via

NOTE: The text description for this Application Object has not been fully updated to agree with the EXPRESS.
A Bonded_conductive_base_blind_via is a type of Blind_via in which the material that composes the base of the Via is conductive, and in which the base material has been implemented by conductively bonding the Land at the base of the Via to a Stratum_feature of an adjacent conducting Stratum.

The data associated with this application object are the following:

  • features_join

4.2.74.1 features_join

The features_join specifies the Stratum_feature_conductive_join that joins the Land on the interior facing surface of the Stratum that is one end of the extent of the Blind_via to the Land or other Stratum_feature on the interior facing surface of the Stratum immediately adjacent to the first Stratum. The surfaces identified need be adjacent only through the extent of the intersection of these two joined features.

Formal constraints:

WR1: The Land at the base of the Bonded_conducted_base_blind_via shall be bonded to the Land on the adjacent Stratum.

NOTE: A conductive bonding agent may be used to form the bond identified by features_join.
