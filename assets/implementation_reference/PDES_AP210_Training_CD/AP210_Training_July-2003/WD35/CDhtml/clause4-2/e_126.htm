4.2.126 Component_make_from_relationship

NOTE: The text description for this Application Object has not been fully updated to agree with the EXPRESS.
A Component_make_from_relationship is a type of Managed_design_object. A Component_make_from_relationship is the association between one Assembly_composition_relationship and another Assembly_composition_relationship, where one Assembly_composition_relationship results from full or partial reuse of the other. A Component_make_from_relationship may be a Laminate_group_component_make_from_relationship.

NOTE: The main use of this concept is design reuse where the modification consists of the fact that the Assembly_composition_relationship is in a different assembly from the original design, or where the original design is a reference design.

The data associated with this application object are the following:

  • resultant_component
  • reusable_component

4.2.126.1 resultant_component

The resultant_component specifies the Assembly_composition_relationship for the Assembly_component that results from the reuse of the reusable_component in the Component_make_from_relationship.

4.2.126.2 reusable_component

The reusable_component specifies the Assembly_composition_relationship for the Assembly_component that is reused to create the resultant_component in the Component_make_from_relationship.

Formal constraints:

WR1: The reusable_component shall not be an ancestor, either directly or indirectly of the resultant_component. The inverse shall also be true.

WR2: The reusable_component shall not be the resultant_component.
