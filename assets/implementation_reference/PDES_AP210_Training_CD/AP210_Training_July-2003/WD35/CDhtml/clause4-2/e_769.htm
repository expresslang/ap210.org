4.2.769 Requirement_allocation

NOTE: The text description for this Application Object has not been fully updated to agree with the EXPRESS.
A Requirement_allocation is a type of Managed_design_object. A Requirement_allocation is the allocation of requirement occurrences to the items in the product that meet the requirements.

The data associated with this application object are the following:

  • allocated_requirement
  • target_product_object

4.2.769.1 allocated_requirement

The allocated_requirement specifies the Ee_requirement_occurrence that is being allocated in the Requirement_allocation.

4.2.769.2 target_product_object

The target_product_object specifies either an Ee_product, an Ee_product_configuration, an Ee_product_definition, an Ee_product_version, a Part_feature, a Physical_connectivity_definition, or a Physical_connectivity_element, or one of an Assembly_component, a Component_external_reference, a Component_feature, a Component_feature_external_reference, an Ee_requirement_occurrence, a Stratum, or one of an Inter_stratum_feature, a Laminate_component, a Stratum_feature, or a Stratum_surface as the target for the Requirement_allocation. There shall be one or more Ee_product, Ee_product_configuration, Ee_product_definition, Ee_product_version, Part_feature, Physical_connectivity_definition, Assembly_component, Component_external_reference, Component_feature, Component_feature_external_reference, Ee_requirement_occurrence, Stratum, Inter_stratum_feature, Laminate_component, Stratum_feature, or Stratum_surface for the Requirement_allocation.
