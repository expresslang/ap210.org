4.2.251 Default_passage_based_land_physical_template

NOTE: The text description for this Application Object has not been fully updated to agree with the EXPRESS.
A Default_passage_based_land_physical_template is a type of Default_land_physical_template. A Default_passage_based_land_physical_template is either a Default_component_termination_passage_based_land_physical_template, a Default_unsupported_passage_based_land_physical_template, or a Default_via_based_land_physical_template. The shape of a Land based on this template is dependent on the shape of the passage with which it is associated.

EXAMPLE: Figure dpblpt illustrates the relationship between the minimum_annular_ring and the size category of finished hole for which the minimum_annular_ring is defined. The category information is specified in the member of Passage_technology associated with this Default_passage_based_land_physical_template.

NOTE: The usual domain technical reference is ANSI/IPC D-275.

The data associated with this application object are the following:

  • minimum_annular_ring
  • minimum_fabrication_allowance
  • of_passage_technology

4.2.251.1 minimum_annular_ring

The minimum_annular_ring specifies the Length_data_element of the smallest dimension between each point on the inner bound of a passage and the nearest point on the outer bound of a Land containing the passage for the Default_passage_based_land_physical_template.

4.2.251.2 minimum_fabrication_allowance

The minimum_fabrication_allowance specifies the Length_data_element for the smallest dimension of the total of the manufacturing process tolerance effects for the Default_passage_based_land_physical_template.

4.2.251.3 of_passage_technology

The of_passage_technology specifies the role of the Passage_technology for the Default_passage_based_land_physical_template.

4.2.251.4 of_stratum_extent

The of_stratum_extent specifies the role of the Inter_stratum_extent for the Default_passage_based_land_physical_template. The of_stratum_extent specfies the actual subset of the passage specified by of_passage_technology to which this Default_passage_based_land_physical_template applies.

Formal constraints:

UR1: The combination of the attribute of_passage_technology and the attribute of_stratum_technology inherited from the supertype Default_land_physical_template shall be unique in a population of Default_passage_based_land_physical_template.

WR1: The stratum extent specified by of_stratum_extent shall be a subset of the stratum extent specified by the of_passage_technology.
