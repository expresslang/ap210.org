4.2.608 Part_device_terminal_model_port_assignment

NOTE: The text description for this Application Object has not been fully updated to agree with the EXPRESS.
A Part_device_terminal_model_port_assignment is a type of Managed_design_object. A Part_device_terminal_model_port_assignment is the relationship between the Analytical_model_port and the Part_device_terminal, for the purpose of supporting behavioural simulation of bond wire and lead frame effects in a full package simulation. A simulation model provides an explicitly defined functional block that acts as the interface between the Part_device_terminals that are the access mechanisms of the Part_device and the Packaged_part_terminals that are the access mechanisms of the Packaged_part. This is appropriate where there is no detailed physical information available. The purpose, fidelity, and accuracy of the simulation model are not predefined in this part of ISO 10303.

NOTES:

  1. The Device_terminal_map is the mechanism provided in this part of ISO 10303 for defining the "internal" surface of the Package_terminal as used in a Packaged_part since the design of a Packaged_part is outside the scope of this part of ISO 10303.
  2. Various manufacturers provide simulation models for the combination of leadframe and IC bond whose internal connection may be referenced by this object and whose external connection may be assigned to the Package_terminal by the Part_feature_model_port_assignment object.
  3. This part of ISO 10303 does not specify the physical details of the connection from the Package_terminal to the Part_device_terminal since that is outside the scope of the Packaged_part Application Object.
  4. A single IC package design definition (e.g. Ball-grid design definition) may be exchanged with this part of ISO 10303 by considering the package to be an Interconnect_module.

The data associated with this application object are the following:

  • internal_connection
  • internal_port

4.2.608.1 internal_connection

The internal_connection specifies the role of the Part_device_terminal for the Part_device_terminal_model_port_assignment.

4.2.608.2 internal_port

The internal_port specifies the role of the Analytical_model_port for the Part_device_terminal_model_port_assignment.

Formal constraints:

UR1: The combination of the internal_connection and the internal_port shall be unique within a population of Part_device_terminal_model_port_assignment.
