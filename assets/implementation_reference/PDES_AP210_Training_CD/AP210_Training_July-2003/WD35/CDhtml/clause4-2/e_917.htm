4.2.917 Tolerance_zone_boundary_member

NOTE: The text description for this Application Object has not been fully updated to agree with the EXPRESS.
A Tolerance_zone_boundary_member is a type of Non_feature_shape_element that is one of the two explicitly identified members of a Tolerance_zone_explicit_opposing_boundary_set.

The data associated with this application object are the following:

  • explicit_opposing_boundary_set

4.2.917.1 explicit_opposing_boundary_set

The explicit_opposing_boundary_set is an inverse relationship that specifies that the existence of the Tolerance_zone_boundary_member is dependent on the existence of the Tolerance_zone_explicit_opposing_boundary_set that specifies the Tolerance_zone_boundary_member as one of its boundary_members.

EXAMPLE: Tolerance_zone_boundary_members may be used to specify an unequally disposed profile tolerance (i.e., either a Profile_of_any_line_tolerance or Profile_of_any_surface_tolerance).

Formal constraints:

WR1: The Tolerance_zone_boundary_member shall not be specified as the offset_tolerance_zone_boundary_member by more than one Tolerance_zone_boundary_member_offset.

ENTITY tolerance_zone_boundary_member
  SUBTYPE OF (non_feature_shape_element);
  INVERSE
    explicit_opposing_boundary_set : 
	tolerance_zone_explicit_opposing_boundary_set FOR boundary_members;
END_ENTITY; -- tolerance_zone_boundary_member
