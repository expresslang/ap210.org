4.2.567 Operational_requirement_occurrence_relationship

NOTE: The text description for this Application Object has not been fully updated to agree with the EXPRESS.
An Operational_requirement_occurrence_relationship is a type of Managed_design_object. An Operational_requirement_occurrence_relationship is the association between one Ee_requirement_occurrence and another, with a defined type of relationship. An Operational_requirement_occurrence_relationship is an incompletely modeled concept and shall be combined with members of Requirement_composition to form a complete requirement.

The data associated with this application object are the following:

  • related_requirement_occurrence
  • relating_requirement_occurrence
  • relationship_type

4.2.567.1 related_requirement_occurrence

The related_requirement_occurrence specifies the first Ee_requirement_occurrence for the Operational_requirement_occurrence_relationship. The first Ee_requirement_occurrence is the dependent item if the relationship is a dependency relationship.

4.2.567.2 relating_requirement_occurrence

The relating_requirement_occurrence specifies the second Ee_requirement_occurrence for the Operational_requirement_occurrence_relationship. The second Ee_requirement_occurrence is the independent item if the relationship is a dependency relationship.

4.2.567.3 relationship_type

The relationship_type specifies one of the following:
  • an AND_OPERATION
  • an OR_OPERATION
  • a DERIVED_FROM relationship
The mathematical operations AND, OR shall be implemented for this relationship_type. DERIVED_FROM only asserts a dependency relationship. The AND_OPERATION is provided so that changes in relationship_type from OR_OPERATION to AND_OPERATION do not cause a structural change to the populated data. The implicit relationship between two member of Ee_requirement_occurrence that help to compose the same Requirement_group_occurrence is an AND relationship.

Formal constraints:

WR1: Each member of Operational_requirement_occurrence_relationship shall specify members of Ee_requirement_occurrence that are also specified by a member of Requirement_composition that specifies a member of Requirement_group_occurrence as the composition or result of the operation.
