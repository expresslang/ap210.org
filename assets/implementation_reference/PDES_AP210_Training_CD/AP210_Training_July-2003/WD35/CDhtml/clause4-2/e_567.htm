4.2.566 Open_shell_based_surface

NOTE: The text description for this Application Object has not been fully updated to agree with the EXPRESS.
A Open_shell_based_surface is a type of Feature_shape_definition that represents a shape with a simple or complex surface using a set of connected faces. The shape shall be manifold and shall not be closed. The shape shall not be used in the composition of a closed volume. The shape shall be a single shell if it is the shape of a Stratum_feature, Stratum_feature_template_component, Stratum_feature_template, or the shape of a Design_intent_modification. The shape shall be one or more shell if it is the shape of a Stratum. The shape shall be a single shell if it is the shape of a Stratum_average_surface or the shape of a Stratum_surface. An Open_shell_based_surface guarantees material continuity as the material traverses corners in a three-dimensional model.
