4.2.115 Component_based_annotation_symbol_placement_relationship

NOTE: The text description for this Application Object has not been fully updated to agree with the EXPRESS.
A Component_based_annotation_symbol_placement_relationship is a type of Component_feature_to_layout_feature_relationship. A Component_based_annotation_symbol_placement_relationship is the information that identifies a Special_symbol_laminate_component and is to be placed in the context of the design. This information overrides any such information that may be provided in the library.

NOTE: This information is used to provide a means to reposition occurrences of polarization etch symbols, such as are used for polarized capacitors and diodes. The placement information is provided by the Next_higher_assembly_relationship for the occurrence of the etch symbol.

The data associated with this application object are the following:

  • related_stratum_concept

4.2.115.1 related_stratum_concept

The related_stratum_concept specifies the role of the Special_symbol_laminate_component for the Component_based_annotation_symbol_placement_relationship.
