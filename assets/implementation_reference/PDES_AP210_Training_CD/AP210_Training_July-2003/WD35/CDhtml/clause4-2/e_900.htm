4.2.900 Thermal_component

NOTE: The text description for this Application Object has not been fully updated to agree with the EXPRESS.
A Thermal_component is a type of Assembly_component that is included in the design in order to satisfy a heat flow requirement.

NOTE: An Assembly_component utilized as a Thermal_component may be another type of Assembly_component as well.

EXAMPLE: Examples of Thermal_component are: a heatsink, a thermal via, an active thermal cooler (such as a fan), or a thermoelectric cooler.

NOTE: 
*)
ENTITY thermal_component
  SUBTYPE OF (assembly_component);
END_ENTITY; -- thermal_component
