4.2.562 Non_functional_land

NOTE: The text description for this Application Object has not been fully updated to agree with the EXPRESS.
A Non_functional_land is a type of Stratum_feature_template_component whose implementing Stratum_feature is not also being composed by other instances of Laminate_component. A Non_functional_land may be one of a Component_termination_passage_and_contact_size_dependent_non_functional_land, a Component_termination_passage_dependent_non_functional_land, a Contact_size_dependent_non_functional_land, an Unsupported_passage_dependent_non_functional_land, a Via_and_contact_size_dependent_non_functional_land, or a Via_dependent_non_functional_land.

The data associated with this application object are the following:

  • access_mechanism
  • alternate_land_definition
  • usage_definition

4.2.562.1 access_mechanism

The access_mechanism is an inverse relationship that specifies that the existence of the Non_functional_land is dependent on the existence of the Non_functional_land_join_terminal that specifies the Non_functional_land as its associated_component. There shall be one or more Non_functional_land_join_terminal for a Non_functional_land. The access_mechanism is only provided for support of plated passages within the lateral geometric boundary of the Non_functional_land and.

4.2.562.2 alternate_land_definition

The alternate_land_definition specifies one role of the Land_physical_template for the Non_functional_land. The alternate_land_definition need not be specified for a particular Non_functional_land. An alternate_land_definition overrides the usage_definition if it is provided.

4.2.562.3 usage_definition

The usage_definition specifies one role of the Land_physical_template for the Non_functional_land.

Formal constraints:

WR1: There shall be no direct connection to a Conductive_interconnect_element from a member of Non_functional_land.

WR2: The stratum_feature specified by SELF\stratum_feature_template_component.stratum_feature_implementation shall not be in the role of stratum_feature_implementation for any other stratum_feature_template_component.

      component_termination_passage_dependent_non_functional_land,

  SUBTYPE OF (stratum_feature_template_component);
    alternate_land_definition                : OPTIONAL land_physical_template;
    SELF\assembly_component.usage_definition : land_physical_template;
  INVERSE
    access_mechanism           : SET [1:?] OF
                                 non_functional_land_join_terminal FOR 
                                 associated_component;
