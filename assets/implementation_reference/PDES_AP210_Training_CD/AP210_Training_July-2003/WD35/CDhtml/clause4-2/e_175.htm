4.2.175 Conductive_interconnect_element_with_user_defined_single_transition

NOTE: The text description for this Application Object has not been fully updated to agree with the EXPRESS.
A Conductive_interconnect_element_with_user_defined_single_transition is a type of Conductive_interconnect_element. The Conductive_interconnect_element_with_user_defined_single_transition supports the specification of custom start and end shape templates and separately specified left_curve_shape_representation and right_curve_shape_representation (which are either Bounding_curve or Trimmed_line).

NOTES:

  1. For examples of the curves that can be used, see Bounding_curve.
  2. The electrical properties of the Conductive_interconnect_element_with_user_defined_single_transition may be assigned using a requirement allocation structure.

EXAMPLE: Figure bc1 illustrates a detail of a layout drawing that shows a transition on a trace. The segment "l" is supported in this part of ISO 10303 as a member of Conductive_interconnect_element_with_user_defined_single_transition.

The data associated with this application object are the following:

  • end_definition
  • end_terminus
  • left_curve_shape_representation
  • right_curve_shape_representation
  • start_definition
  • start_terminus

4.2.175.1 end_definition

The end_definition specifies one role of the Trace_template for the Conductive_interconnect_element_with_user_defined_single_transition.

4.2.175.2 end_terminus

The end_terminus specifies one role of the Layer_connection_point for the Conductive_interconnect_element_with_user_defined_single_transition.

4.2.175.3 left_curve_shape_representation

The left_curve_shape_representation specifies one role of either a Bounding_curve or Trimmed_line for the Conductive_interconnect_element_with_user_defined_single_transition.

4.2.175.4 right_curve_shape_representation

The right_curve_shape_representation specifies one role of either a Bounding_curve or Trimmed_line for the Conductive_interconnect_element_with_user_defined_single_transition.

4.2.175.5 start_definition

The start_definition specifies one role of the Trace_template for the Conductive_interconnect_element_with_user_defined_single_transition.

4.2.175.6 start_terminus

The start_terminus specifies one role of the Layer_connection_point for the Conductive_interconnect_element_with_user_defined_single_transition.
