4.2.95 Change_design_object_management_relationship

NOTE: The text description for this Application Object has not been fully updated to agree with the EXPRESS.
A Change_design_object_management_relationship is a type of Design_object_management_relationship that provides information about design changes, including the swapping changes among components, gates, or terminals. A Change_design_object_management_relationship may be a Component_swap_specification, a Gate_path_swap_specification, or a Terminal_swap_specification.

The data associated with this application object are the following:

  • current_design_object
  • previous_design_object

4.2.95.1 current_design_object

The current_design_object specifies the Managed_design_object, after the design change, for the Change_design_object_management_relationship. The current_design_object is in the scope of the current_design.

4.2.95.2 previous_design_object

The previous_design_object specifies the Managed_design_object, before the design change, for the Change_design_object_management_relationship. The previous_design_object is in the scope of the previous_design.

Formal constraints:

UR1: The combination of the current_design_object and the current_design inherited from Design_object_management_relationship shall be unique within a population of Change_design_object_management_relationships.

UR2: The combination of the previous_design_object and the previous_design inherited from Design_object_management_relationship shall be unique within a population of Change_design_object_management_relationships.

WR1: The current_design_object and the previous_design_object shall be of the same type of application object, as defined in this part of ISO 10303.

NOTE: A design change may affect only the attributes of the object and not its identifier.

ENTITY change_design_object_management_relationship
  SUPERTYPE OF (ONEOF (terminal_swap_specification,
      gate_path_swap_specification,
	component_swap_specification))
  SUBTYPE OF (design_object_management_relationship);
    current_design_object  : managed_design_object;
    previous_design_object : managed_design_object;
  UNIQUE
    ur1 : current_design_object, current_design;
    ur2 : previous_design_object, previous_design;
END_ENTITY; -- change_design_object_management_relationship
