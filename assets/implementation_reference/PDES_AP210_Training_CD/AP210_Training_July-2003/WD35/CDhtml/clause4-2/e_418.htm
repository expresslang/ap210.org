4.2.416 Geometric_alignment

NOTE: The text description for this Application Object has not been fully updated to agree with the EXPRESS.
A Geometric_alignment is a type of Derived_shape_element that is a linear or planar Shape_element that is established to contain two or more aligned Shape_element. The Shape_element from which the Geometric_alignment is established are associated with the Geometric_alignment by the Shape_element_deriving_relationship that are specified as the deriving_relationships.

NOTE: The attribute deriving_relationships is declared in the Derived_shape_element entity of which this entity is a subtype.

EXAMPLE: A Geometric_alignment that is derived from the Centre_axis of a circular hole pattern and a Centre_axis of one of the holes of the pattern is a plane to which a Dimensional_location might be applied.

Formal constraints:

WR1: The Geometric_alignment shall be derived from two or more Shape_element.

Informal constraints:

IP1: In the case of a planar Derived_shape_element, at least one of the aligned Shape_element shall be a linear Shape_element.

ENTITY geometric_alignment
  SUBTYPE OF (derived_shape_element);
END_ENTITY; -- geometric_alignment
