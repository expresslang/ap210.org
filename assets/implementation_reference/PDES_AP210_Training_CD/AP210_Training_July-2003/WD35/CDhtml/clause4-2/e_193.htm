4.2.193 Constraint_occurrence

NOTE: The text description for this Application Object has not been fully updated to agree with the EXPRESS.
A Constraint_occurrence is a type of Ee_requirement_occurrence that limits possible solutions to the requirements specification by identifying boundary conditions for any potential solution. A Constraint_occurrence may be one of Ee_product_definition_based_constraint_occurrence or of Rule_based_constraint_occurrence or may be a combination of the two.
