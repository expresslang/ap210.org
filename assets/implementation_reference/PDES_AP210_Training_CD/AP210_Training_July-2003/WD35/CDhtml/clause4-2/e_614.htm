4.2.610 Part_feature_make_from_relationship

NOTE: The text description for this Application Object has not been fully updated to agree with the EXPRESS.
A Part_feature_make_from_relationship is a type of Managed_design_object.

The data associated with this application object are the following:

  • associated_make_from
  • reusable_feature
  • resultant_feature

4.2.610.1 associated_make_from

The associated_make_from specifies an Engineering_make_from for the Part_feature_make_from_relationship.

4.2.610.2 reusable_feature

The reusable_feature specifies a Part_feature for the Part_feature_make_from_relationship.

4.2.610.3 resultant_feature

The resultant_feature specifies a Part_feature for the Part_feature_make_from_relationship.

Formal constraints:

UR1: The combination of reusable_feature and resultant_feature shall be unique within a population of Part_feature_make_from_relationship.

WR1: The reusable_feature shall not be the resultant_feature within a population of Part_feature_make_from_relationship.

WR2: The Physical_unit_usage_view referenced by the reusable_feature shall not be the Physical_unit_usage_view referenced by the resultant_feature within a population of Part_feature_make_from_relationship.

NOTE: 
*)
ENTITY part_feature_make_from_relationship
  SUBTYPE OF (managed_design_object);
    associated_make_from : engineering_make_from;
       reusable_feature  : part_feature;
       resultant_feature : part_feature;
UNIQUE
  ur1: reusable_feature, resultant_feature;
WHERE
  wr1: reusable_feature :<>: resultant_feature;
  wr2: reusable_feature.associated_definition :<>: 
          resultant_feature.associated_definition;
END_ENTITY; -- part_feature_make_from_relationship
