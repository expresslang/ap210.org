4.2.844 Straightness_tolerance

NOTE: The text description for this Application Object has not been fully updated to agree with the EXPRESS.
A Straightness_tolerance is a type of Geometric_tolerance_without_referenced_datum_system that specifies or partially specifies the design requirement on the straightness of either the Shape_element to which the Straightness_tolerance is applied or of constituent elements of the Shape_element to which the Straightness_tolerance is applied. When the design requirements are on the straightness of constituent elements of a non-axial Shape_element, the orientation of the Tolerance_zone_boundary is specified by a Viewing_plane to which the Tolerance_zone_boundary is parallel.

NOTE: Straightness tolerance is defined in terms of tolerance zone definitions in 14.1 of ISO 1101.

Formal constraints:

WR1: The Shape_element specified by the toleranced_shape_element shall be either a Physical_feature_or_part_template, a Centre_axis or a Centre_plane

NOTE: The attribute toleranced_shape_element is declared in the Geometric_tolerance entity of which this entity is a subtype.

WR2: If the toleranced_shape_element is a Centre_axis, the Straightness_tolerance shall specify a Tolerance_zone_definition as its tolerance_zone_or_boundary_definition; and that Tolerance_zone_definition shall specify a Tolerance_zone_boundary_definition_with_specified_size as its boundary_definition; and that Tolerance_zone_boundary_definition_with_specified_size shall specify a Diametrical_boundary_size_characteristic as its specified_boundary_size; otherwise, the Tolerance_zone_boundary_definition_with_specified_size shall specify an Opposing_boundary_set_size_characteristic as the specified_boundary_size.

WR3: If the toleranced_shape_element is a Physical_feature_or_part_template, the Tolerance_zone_definition that is specified as the tolerance_zone_or_boundary_definition shall be a Tolerance_zone_definition_with_specified_orientation that specifies a Viewing_plane_based_orientation as its zone_orientation; otherwise, the Tolerance_zone_definition shall not be a Tolerance_zone_definition_with_specified_orientation.

Informal constraints:

IP1: The form of the toleranced Shape_element shall be consistent with the form of a shape element for which the application of a straightness tolerance is sensible.
