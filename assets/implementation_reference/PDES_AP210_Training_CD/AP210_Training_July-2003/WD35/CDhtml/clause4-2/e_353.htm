4.2.353 Engineering_make_from

NOTE: The text description for this Application Object has not been fully updated to agree with the EXPRESS.
An Engineering_make_from is a type of Managed_design_object. An Engineering_make_from is the relationship between two products in which one product is used as the basis for the design of the other product.

EXAMPLE: Engineering_make_from may be mask programmed processors, enterprise programmed digital devices, or field programmable or field reprogramable arrays or modems that are updateable by downloading new firmware from a central source.

NOTE: This facility is not limited to packaged parts but may be applied to any product.

EXAMPLE: A mask programmed processor would be a 'mask programmable processor' before programming and would be a 'Yaw rate processor' after programming.

The data associated with this application object are the following:

  • base_design
  • resultant_design

4.2.353.1 base_design

The base_design specifies the Ee_product_definition to be used as the basis for the design of another Ee_product for the Engineering_make_from.

4.2.353.2 resultant_design

The resultant_design specifies the Ee_product_definition that results from basing the design upon the design of another Ee_product for the Engineering_make_from.
