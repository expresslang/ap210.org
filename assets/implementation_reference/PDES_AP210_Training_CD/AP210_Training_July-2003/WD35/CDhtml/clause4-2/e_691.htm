4.2.691 Plus_minus_tolerance_characteristic

NOTE: The text description for this Application Object has not been fully updated to agree with the EXPRESS.
A Plus_minus_tolerance_characteristic is a type of Tolerance_characteristic. The Plus_minus_tolerance_characteristic includes a minimum, maximum, and nominal characteristic numeric parameter. All three have the same base unit, but may have different prefixes.

The data associated with this application object are the following:

  • component_characteristic

4.2.691.1 component_characteristic

The component_characteristic specifies the nominal, minimum, and maximum values of the Numeric_parameter for the Plus_minus_tolerance_characteristic. There shall be one nominal, one minimum and one maximum value for the Plus_minus_tolerance_characteristic.

Formal constraints:

WR1: All Data_element referenced by Numeric_parameter in the role of component_characteristic for Plus_minus_tolerance_characteristic shall have the same unit_of_measure.

WR2: The Numeric_parameter in the role of component_characteristic shall have a type_name of minimum, nominal, or maximum.

WR3: The Numeric_parameter in the role of component_characteristic that has a type_name of minmum shall have a property_value that is less than the property_value of the Numeric_parameter in the role of component_characteristic that has a type_name of nominal.

WR4: The Numeric_parameter in the role of component_characteristic that has a type_name of nominal shall have a property_value that is less than the property_value of the Numeric_parameter in the role of component_characteristic that has a type_name of maximum.
