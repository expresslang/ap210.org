4.2.309 Edge_based_2d_wireframe

NOTE: The text description for this Application Object has not been fully updated to agree with the EXPRESS.
An Edge_based_2d_wireframe is a type of Feature_shape_definition. An Edge_based_2d_wireframe is described by a two-dimensional graph of edges and vertices. The graph may be disconnected. Within the graph the edges do not intersect except at their boundaries, otherwise known as vertices. The geometry associated with a vertex shall be consistent with the geometry associated with any of the edges of which the vertex forms a boundary.
