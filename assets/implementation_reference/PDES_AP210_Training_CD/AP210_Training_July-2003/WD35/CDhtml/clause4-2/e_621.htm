4.2.617 Part_mating_feature

NOTE: The text description for this Application Object has not been fully updated to agree with the EXPRESS.
A Part_mating_feature is a type of Part_feature that is identified to facilitate tolerance specification when an occurrence of the associated part is mated to a complementary part. A Part_mating_feature is provided where there is no requirement to convey electrical functionality as would be provided by Part_terminal.

NOTE: The use of multi-pin connectors may require specification of mechanical mating areas in addition to electrical terminals.

EXAMPLE: The plastic housing on some connectors may contain Part_mating_features.

The data associated with this application object are the following:

  • mating_connection_area

4.2.617.1 mating_connection_area

The mating_connection_area specifies the role of the Connection_zone for the Part_mating_feature. The mating_connection_area provides a means to geometrically specify a surface area for critical mechanical mating properties.

NOTE: The Feature_shape_occurrence specified by the mating feature may be the same Feature_shape_occurrence that is specified by the associated Connection_zone. In this case the whole mating feature is the Connection_zone.
