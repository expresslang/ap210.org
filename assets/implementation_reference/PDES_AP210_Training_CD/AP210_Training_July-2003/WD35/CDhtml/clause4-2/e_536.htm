4.2.536 Material_shape_modification

NOTE: The text description for this Application Object has not been fully updated to agree with the EXPRESS.
A Material_shape_modification is a type of Design_intent_modification that indicates that the shape of material of the feature in the role of design_intent inherited from Design_intent_modification is to be modified to suit the design intent. The mathematical interpretation shall be dependent on the operator instance data in the geometrical representation used in the model. A Material_shape_modification shall be represented only by a geometric representation capable of supporting regularized Boolean operations.
