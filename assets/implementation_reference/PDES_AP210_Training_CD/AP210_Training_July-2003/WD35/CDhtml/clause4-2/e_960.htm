4.2.960 Volume_shape_intersection

NOTE: The text description for this Application Object has not been fully updated to agree with the EXPRESS.
A Volume_shape_intersection is a type of Managed_design_object. A Volume_shape_intersection may be one of Edge_segment_cross_section, or Pre_defined_perpendicular_datum_plane_symbol_3d_2d_relationship , or Pre_defined_perpendicular_datum_axis_symbol_3d_2d_relationship , or Pre_defined_parallel_datum_axis_symbol_3d_2d_relationship . A Volume_shape_intersection is the projection into two dimensions of a three-dimensional shape based on the intersection of a plane with the three-dimensional shape.

The data associated with this application object are the following:

  • orientation_intersection_plane
  • planar_shape_origin
  • projected_volume
  • surface_projection_result

4.2.960.1 orientation_intersection_plane

The orientation_intersection_plane specifies the role of the Axis_placement_3d for the Volume_shape_intersection. The Z axis of the Axis_placement_3d shall define the normal of the plane.

4.2.960.2 planar_shape_origin

The planar_shape_origin specifies the role of the Axis_placement_2d for the Volume_shape_intersection. The Axis_placement_2d shall define the two dimensional coordinate system.

4.2.960.3 projected_volume

The projected_volume specifies the role of the Bound_volume_shape for the Volume_shape_intersection.

4.2.960.4 surface_projection

The surface_intersection_result specifies the role of the Planar_projected_shape for the Volume_shape_intersection.
