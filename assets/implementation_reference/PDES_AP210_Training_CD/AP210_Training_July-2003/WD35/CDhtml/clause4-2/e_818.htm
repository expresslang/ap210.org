4.2.818 Shape_item

NOTE: The text description for this Application Object has not been fully updated to agree with the EXPRESS.
A Shape_item is a type of Managed_design_object. A Shape_item may be one of a Boolean_csg_result, a Cartesian_point, a Curve, a Primitive_2d, a Axis_placement, or additionally may be a Styled_text. The Shape_item shall not be a complex instance of Styled_text and Cartesian_point. The Shape_item shall not be a complex instance of Styled_text and Axis_placement.

NOTE: 
*)
ENTITY shape_item
  SUPERTYPE OF ((ONEOF (
     boolean_csg_result,
     cartesian_point,
     curve,
     axis_placement,
     primitive_2d)) ANDOR styled_text)
  SUBTYPE OF (managed_design_object);
DERIVE
    geometric_context : cartesian_coordinate_system := 
    associated_shape_definition[1].geometric_context;
INVERSE
  associated_shape_definition : SET [1:?] OF shape_definition
                                    for element;
WHERE
 WR1: NOT (('AP210_ARM.CARTESIAN_POINT' IN TYPEOF(SELF))
          AND
          ('AP210_ARM.STYLED_TEXT' IN TYPEOF(SELF)));
 WR2: NOT (('AP210_ARM.AXIS_PLACEMENT' IN TYPEOF(SELF))
          AND
          ('AP210_ARM.STYLED_TEXT' IN TYPEOF(SELF)));
END_ENTITY; -- shape_item
