4.2.811 Shape_element_boundary_relationship

NOTE: The text description for this Application Object has not been fully updated to agree with the EXPRESS.
A Shape_element_boundary_relationship is a type of Shape_element_relationship that specifies that a Shape_element is the boundary or a portion of the boundary of another Shape_element. The related_shape_element specifies the Shape_element that is the boundary or a portion of a boundary of the Shape_element specified as the relating_shape_element.

NOTES:

  1. The attribute related_shape_element is declared in the Shape_element_relationship entity of which this entity is a subtype.
  2. The attribute relating_shape_element is declared in the Shape_element_relationship entity of which this entity is a subtype.

EXAMPLE: A Shape_element_boundary_relationship may be used to specify that a Shape_element is one of the boundaries of a rectangular Datum_target area. The boundary may then be located relative to a Datum with a Dimensional_location.

Formal constraints:

WR1: The Shape_element specified as the relating_shape_element shall not be specified as a boundary of itself either directly or through intermediate Shape_element_boundary_relationship.
