4.2.237 Datum_system_defining_conditions

NOTE: The text description for this Application Object has not been fully updated to agree with the EXPRESS.
A Datum_system_defining_conditions is a type of Managed_design_object. A Datum_system_defining_conditions is either a Datum_system_defining_conditions_without_material_conditions or a Datum_system_defining_conditions_with_material_conditions. A Datum_system_defining_conditions is the specification of the conditions under which a Datum_system is established. The conditions include the order in which the Datum are established within the Datum_system and any material condition properties that are explicitly applied to Datum_feature that participate in the establishment of the Datum_system.

The data associated with this application object are the following:

  • assigned_datum_precedences
  • conditions_of

4.2.237.1 assigned_datum_precedences

The assigned_datum_precedences specify the Datum_precedence_assignment that define the order in which the Datum are established in the Datum_system specified as the conditions_of for the Datum_system_defining_conditions. There shall be one, two, or three Datum_precedence_assignment for the Datum_system_defining_conditions.

4.2.237.2 conditions_of

The conditions_of specifies the role of the Datum_system for which the Datum_system_defining_conditions are defined.

Formal constraints:

WR1: The conditions_of the Datum_system_defining_conditions shall be the same instance of Datum_system as specified by the established_datum_system attribute of the Datum_usage_in_datum_system_relationship that is specified by the assigned_to attribute of the Datum_precedence_assignment specified by the assigned_datum_precedences. That is, the Datum_system_defining_conditions and the specified Datum_precedence_assignments must be associated with the same Datum_system.

WR2: The name tertiary shall not exist in the names of the assigned_datum_precedences unless the name secondary exists in the names of the assigned_datum_precedences.

WR3: The name secondary shall not exist in the names of the assigned_datum_precedences unless the name primary exists in the names of the assigned_datum_precedences.

WR4: The datum_system_defining_conditions shall be referenced by at least one Geometric_tolerance_with_referenced_datum_system or Dimension_location_with_specified_datum_system.
