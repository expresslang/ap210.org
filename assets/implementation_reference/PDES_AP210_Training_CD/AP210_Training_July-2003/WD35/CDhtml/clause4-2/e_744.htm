4.2.744 Profile_of_any_line_tolerance

NOTE: The text description for this Application Object has not been fully updated to agree with the EXPRESS.
A Profile_of_any_line_tolerance is a type of Geometric_tolerance. A Profile_of_any_line_tolerance is either a Profile_of_any_line_tolerance_without_referenced_datum_system or a Profile_of_any_line_tolerance_with_referenced_datum_system. A Profile_of_any_line_tolerance may also be a Composite_linear_profile_refinement_tolerance. A Profile_of_any_line_tolerance participates in the specification of a design requirement on the form, and possibly on the orientation, size, and location of line elements of a surface. The Profile_of_any_line_tolerance is similar to the Profile_of_any_surface_tolerance in that the Tolerance_zone is based on the True_profile. However, with a Profile_of_any_line_tolerance, each line element of the surface must lie within its associated Tolerance_zone, where the line elements are the cross-sections of the toleranced surface that are parallel to the specified Viewing_plane. If the design requirements are such that the Tolerance_zone_boundary is unequally disposed about the True_profile, either unilaterally or bilateral, a Profile_boundary_definition_with_offsets shall be used to specify this requirement.

NOTE: Profile of any line tolerance is defined in terms of tolerance zone definitions in 14.5 of ISO 1101.

Formal constraints:

WR1: The Shape_element specified as the toleranced_shape_element shall be a Physical_feature_or_part_template.

NOTE: The attribute toleranced_shape_element is declared in the Geometric_tolerance entity of which this entity is a subtype.

WR2: The Profile_of_any_line_tolerance shall not be a Geometric_tolerance_with_applied_material_condition_property.

WR3: The Profile_of_any_line_tolerance shall specify as the tolerance_zone_or_boundary_definition a Tolerance_zone_definition that specifies a Profile_boundary_definition as its boundary_definition.

Informal constraints:

IP1: The form of the toleranced Shape_element shall be consistent with the form of a shape element for which the application of a profile of any line tolerance is sensible.
