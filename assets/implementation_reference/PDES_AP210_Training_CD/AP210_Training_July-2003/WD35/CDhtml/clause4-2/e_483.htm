4.2.483 Laminate_component

NOTE: The text description for this Application Object has not been fully updated to agree with the EXPRESS.
A Laminate_component is a type of Assembly_component. A Laminate_component may be one of a Conductive_interconnect_element, an Inter_stratum_feature, a Laminate_text_component, a Laminate_text_string_component, a Material_removal_laminate_component, a Probe_access_area, a Special_symbol_laminate_component or a Stratum_feature_template_component, or additionally may be a Fiducial, or additionally may be a Physical_laminate_component. All types of Laminate_component are realized as part of the manufacturing process that produces the interconnect.

The data associated with this application object are the following:

  • cad_layer
  • usage_definition

NOTE: Complex multi-layered components should be described by using the Assembly_group_component and Sub_assembly_component_relationship. These relationships will be created from occurrences of Stratum_feature_template_component or other Printed_component occurrences.

4.2.483.1 cad_layer

The cad_layer specifies the role of the External_definition for the Laminate_component. The cad_layer need not be specified for a Laminate_component.

4.2.483.2 usage_definition

The usage_definition specifies the role of the Part_template for the Laminate_component.
