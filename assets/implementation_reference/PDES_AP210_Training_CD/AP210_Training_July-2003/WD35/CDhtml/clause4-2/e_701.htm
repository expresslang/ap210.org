4.2.695 Position_tolerance

NOTE: The text description for this Application Object has not been fully updated to agree with the EXPRESS.
A Position_tolerance is a type of Geometric_tolerance that specifies or partially specifies the design requirement on the location of a Shape_element in relation to other Shape_elements, such as a Datum_system. A Position_tolerance is either a Position_tolerance_with_referenced_datum_system or a Position_tolerance_without_referenced_datum_system. A Position_tolerance may also be a Composite_feature_relating_position_tolerance.

NOTE: Position tolerance is defined in terms of tolerance zone definitions in 14.10 of ISO 1101.

Formal constraints:

WR1: The Shape_element specified as the toleranced_shape_element shall be a Centre_axis, Centre_plane, Centre_point, or Physical_feature_or_part_template.

WR2: If the toleranced_shape_element is a Centre_plane, the Position_tolerance shall specify a Tolerance_zone_definition as its tolerance_zone_or_boundary_definition; and that Tolerance_zone_definition shall specify a Tolerance_zone_boundary_definition_with_specified_size as its boundary_definition; and that Tolerance_zone_boundary_definition_with_specified_size shall specify an Opposing_boundary_set_size_characteristic as its specified_boundary_size.

WR3: If the toleranced_shape_element is a Centre_point the Position_tolerance shall specify a Tolerance_zone_definition as its tolerance_zone_or_boundary_definition.

Informal constraints:

IP1: The form of the toleranced Shape_element shall be consistent with the form of a shape element for which the application of a position tolerance is sensible.
