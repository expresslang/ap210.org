4.2.827 Single_boundary_dimension

NOTE: The text description for this Application Object has not been fully updated to agree with the EXPRESS.
A Single_boundary_dimension is a type of Dimensional_size that characterizes the size of a Shape_element that can be considered to have a single boundary or have the form of a curve. A Single_boundary_dimension may be a Curve_dimension.

EXAMPLE: The diameter of a cylindrical Shape_element would be characterized with a Single_boundary_dimension.

NOTE: 
*)
ENTITY single_boundary_dimension
  SUPERTYPE OF (curve_dimension)
  SUBTYPE OF (dimensional_size);
END_ENTITY; -- single_boundary_dimension
