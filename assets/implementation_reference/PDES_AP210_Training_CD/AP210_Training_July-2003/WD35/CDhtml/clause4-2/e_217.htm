4.2.217 Datum_axis_related_orientation

NOTE: The text description for this Application Object has not been fully updated to agree with the EXPRESS.
A Datum_axis_related_orientation is a type of Managed_design_object. A Datum_axis_related_orientation is a definition that characterizes the orientation of a Tolerance_zone in relation to a Datum_axis.

The data associated with this application object are the following:

  • orientation_type
  • orienting_relationship

4.2.217.1 orientation_type

The orientation_type specifies a label that indicates the manner in which the specified Tolerance_zone is oriented with respect to the specified Datum_axis. The orientation_type shall either be "centred_on_arc", or "centred_on_radial".

EXAMPLE: In the polar coordinate method of bidirectional positional tolerancing the bidirectional tolerance zone consists of two tolerance zones. One that is bounded by a pair of parallel planes that is centred along a radial from the datum axis and another that is bounded by a pair of concentric cylindrical arc boundaries that are centred along a cylindrical arc the origin of which is the datum axis. An orientation_type of "centred_on_radial" corresponds to the first tolerance zone and "centred_on_arc" corresponds to the second tolerance zone.

NOTE: The polar method of bidirectional positional tolerancing is discussed in 5.9.2 of ASME Y14.5M.

4.2.217.2 orienting_relationship

The orienting_relationship specifies the Datum_axis_and_tolerance_zone_orienting_relationship that associates the Tolerance_zone with the Datum_axis with which it is oriented. The specified Datum_axis_and_tolerance_zone_orienting_relationship is characterized by the Datum_axis_related_orientation.

Formal constraints:

WR1: The Datum_axis_related_orientation shall be specified as the zone_orientation by exactly one Tolerance_zone_definition_with_specified_orientation.
