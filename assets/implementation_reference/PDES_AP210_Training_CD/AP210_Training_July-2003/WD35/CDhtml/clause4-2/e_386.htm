4.2.386 Flatness_tolerance

NOTE: The text description for this Application Object has not been fully updated to agree with the EXPRESS.
A Flatness_tolerance is a type of Geometric_tolerance_without_referenced_datum_system that specifies or partially specifies the design requirement on the flatness of the Physical_feature_or_part_template to which the Flatness_tolerance is applied.

NOTE: Flatness tolerance is defined in terms of tolerance zone definitions in 14.2 of ISO 1101.

Formal constraints:

WR1: The Shape_element specified by the toleranced_shape_element shall be a Physical_feature_or_part_template.

NOTE: The attribute toleranced_shape_element is declared in the Geometric_tolerance entity of which this entity is a subtype.

WR2: The Flatness_tolerance shall not be a Geometric_tolerance_with_applied_material_condition_property.

WR3: The Flatness_tolerance shall specify a Tolerance_zone_definition as its tolerance_zone_or_boundary_definition; and that Tolerance_zone_definition shall specify a Tolerance_zone_boundary_definition_with_specified_size as its boundary_definition; and that Tolerance_zone_boundary_definition_with_specified_size shall specify an Opposing_boundary_set_size_characteristic as its specified_boundary_size.

WR4: If the Flatness_tolerance specifies a Tolerance_zone_definition_with_per_unit_size_specification as the tolerance_zone_or_boundary_definition, there shall be exactly two Length_data_element specified as the per_unit_size by the Tolerance_zone_per_unit_size_specification that is specified as the per_unit_size_specification by the Tolerance_zone_definition_with_per_unit_size_specification.

Informal constraints:

IP1: The form of the toleranced Shape_element shall be consistent with the form of a shape element for which the application of a flatness tolerance is sensible.
