4.2.834 Special_symbol_laminate_component

NOTE: The text description for this Application Object has not been fully updated to agree with the EXPRESS.
A Special_symbol_laminate_component is a type of Laminate_component such as an enterprise logo, trademark, or other enterprise specific symbol. A Special_symbol_laminate_component may be a Primary_stratum_indicator_symbol.

NOTES:

  1. A Special_symbol_laminate_component may or may not be limited to a member of a library of accepted symbols.
  2. A Special_symbol_laminate_component may or may not be scalable. A Special_symbol_laminate_component may or may not be associated with a physical net.
  3. These symbols may be stored in a library for legal reasons.

The data associated with this application object are the following:

  • stratum_feature_implementation
  • usage_definition

4.2.834.1 stratum_feature_implementation

The stratum_feature_implementation specifies the role of the Stratum_feature for the Special_symbol_laminate_component.

4.2.834.2 usage_definition

The usage_definition specifies the role of the Special_symbol_part_template for the Special_symbol_laminate_component.
