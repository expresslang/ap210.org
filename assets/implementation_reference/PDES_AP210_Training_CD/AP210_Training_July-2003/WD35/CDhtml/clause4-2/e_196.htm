4.2.196 Contract

A Contract is a type of Managed_design_object. A Contract is the identification of certain enterprise data related to a formal agreement between two business entities that is useful for managing product data.

NOTE: This part of ISO 10303 does not support the exchange of the contents of a contract.

The data associated with this application object are the following:

  • contractor
  • contract_approval
  • contract_date
  • contract_number

4.2.196.1 contractor

The contractor specifies either the Person_and_organization or the Organization performing work under the scope of the Contract. The contractor need not be specified for a particular Contract.

4.2.196.2 contract_approval

The contract_approval specifies the role of the Ee_approval for the Contract. The contract_approval is optional and need not be supplied.

4.2.196.3 contract_date

The contract_date specifies either the Date or the Date_and_time for the Contract. The contract_date is optional and need not be supplied.

4.2.196.4 contract_number

The contract_number specifies the role of the Ee_text for the identifier of the Contract.

Formal constraints:

UR1: The contract_number shall be unique within a population of Contracts.

NOTE: 
*)
ENTITY contract
  SUBTYPE OF (managed_design_object);
    contractor        : OPTIONAL person_organization;
    contract_number   : ee_text;
    contract_approval : OPTIONAL ee_approval;
    contract_date     : OPTIONAL date_or_date_and_time;
  UNIQUE
    ur1 : contract_number;
END_ENTITY; -- contract
