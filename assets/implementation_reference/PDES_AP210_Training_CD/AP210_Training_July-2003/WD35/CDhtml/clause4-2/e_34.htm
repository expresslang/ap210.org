4.2.34 Assembly_component

NOTE: The text description for this Application Object has not been fully updated to agree with the EXPRESS.
An Assembly_component is a type of Managed_design_object. An Assembly_component may be one of an Assembly_module_component, a Bare_die_component, an Interconnect_module_component, a Laminate_component, a Packaged_component, or a Printed_component, or additionally may be an Assembly_group_component, or additionally may be a Shield, or additionally may be a Thermal_component, or additionally may be a Routed_interconnect_component, or additionally may be an Interface_component. An Assembly_component is the occurrence of a Physical_unit or Part_template in an assembly. An Assembly_component may inclusage_definitione additional product data over that defined in Physical_unit or Part_template. An Assembly_component that is no other type is intended to be an instance of a Physical_unit and not of a Part_template.

EXAMPLE: Figure ac illustrates a detail of an assembly drawing that shows the Assembly_component in relation to a cross-sectional view of the Interconnect_module_component. Figure pusage_definitionv illustrates a complete assembly showing multiple Assembly_component.

NOTES:

  1. An Assembly_component may be assembled onto an Interconnect_module_component in the realization of an Assembly_module. An Assembly_component may be an occurrence of an Assembly_module_usage_view. An Assembly_component may be an occurrence of an Interconnect_module_usage_view. For the purposes of this model, the current interpretation is to treat an embedded component as a Packaged_component. The Interconnect_module_component_terminal that mates with the Packaged_component_join_terminal of the embedded component is not visible when the Assembly_module is assembled, but the Interconnect_module_component_terminal is still logically the interface to the Interconnect_module_component for the embedded component. The manufacturing view supports the fact that the embedded component is installed during part of the sequential manufacturing process for the Interconnect_module.
  2. An Assembly_component may be a floor-planning symbol if the definition is an Assembly_module or an Interconnect_module. If the Assembly_component is used in this manner, then the actual components will be inclusage_definitioned in the design in a later iteration that results from inclusage_definitioning the detailed components from the Assembly_module or Interconnect_module using a computer processor. The processor may choose to support traceability by using the Component_make_from_relationship to specify what component of the Assembly_module or Interconnect_module corresponds to the component in the design.
  3. Parameters may be assigned to Assembly_component by the Planned_characteristic object through the Assembly_requirements_allocation.

The data associated with this application object are the following:

  • assembly
  • component_2d_shape
  • component_3d_shape
  • usage_definition

4.2.34.1 assembly

The assembly is an inverse relationship that specifies that the existence of the Assembly_component is dependent on the existence of the Assembly_composition_relationship that specifies the Assembly_component as its component. There shall be one or more Assembly_composition_relationship for an Assembly_component.

NOTE: This cardinality supports multiple versions of the design, with the same member of Assembly_component.

4.2.34.2 component_2d_shape

The component_2d_shape is an inverse relationship that specifies that there shall be no more than one Assembly_component_2d_shape that specifies the Assembly_component as its shape_characterized_component. The component_2d_shape need not be supplied for a particular Assembly_component.

4.2.34.3 component_3d_shape

The component_3d_shape is an inverse relationship that specifies that there shall be no more than one Assembly_component_3d_shape that specifies the Assembly_component as its shape_characterized_component. The component_3d_shape need not be supplied for a particular Assembly_component.

4.2.34.4 usage_definition

The usage_definition specifies either a Part_template or a Physical_unit for the Assembly_component. The usage_definition need not be supplied for an Assembly_component. The usage_definition is optional because some types of Assembly_component are defined through another mechanism. The types of Assembly_component for which usage_definition is required inclusage_definitione the explicit statement of that requirement in their definition.

Formal constraints:

WR1: If the usage_definition exists, then only a Footprint_occurrence shall reference a Footprint_definition and only a Padstack_occurrence shall reference a Padstack_definition.

WR2: All members of assembly shall be for the same product.

WR3: No more than one member of assembly shall be for a version of the product.

WR4: An Assembly_component shall not be both a Laminate_component and an Interface_component.

ENTITY assembly_component
  ABSTRACT SUPERTYPE OF (((ONEOF (
      physical_component,
      printed_component,
      laminate_component) 
      ANDOR shield) 
      ANDOR thermal_component)
      ANDOR assembly_group_component)
  SUBTYPE OF (managed_design_object);
    usage_definition : OPTIONAL physical_unit_or_part_template_or_fp_or_pd;
  INVERSE
    assembly                   : SET [1:?] OF assembly_composition_relationship FOR 
                                 component;
    component_2d_shape         : SET [0:1] OF assembly_component_2d_shape FOR 
                                 shape_characterized_component;
    component_3d_shape         : SET [0:1] OF assembly_component_3d_shape FOR 
                                 shape_characterized_component;
    component_non_planar_shape : SET [0:1] OF assembly_component_non_planar_shape FOR 
                                 shape_characterized_component;
WHERE
  WR1: NOT(EXISTS(usage_definition)) OR
       (
       (('AP210_ARM.FOOTPRINT_OCCURRENCE' IN TYPEOF(SELF)) AND 
        ('AP210_ARM.FOOTPRINT_DEFINITION' IN TYPEOF(usage_definition))) OR
        (NOT ('AP210_ARM.FOOTPRINT_OCCURRENCE' IN TYPEOF(SELF)) 
         AND NOT('AP210_ARM.FOOTPRINT_DEFINITION' IN TYPEOF(usage_definition))) OR
        (('AP210_ARM.PADSTACK_OCCURRENCE' IN TYPEOF(SELF)) AND 
          ('AP210_ARM.PADSTACK_DEFINITION' IN TYPEOF(usage_definition))) OR
        (NOT ('AP210_ARM.PADSTACK_OCCURRENCE' IN TYPEOF(SELF)) AND 
          NOT('AP210_ARM.PADSTACK_DEFINITION' IN TYPEOF(usage_definition)))
       );
END_ENTITY; -- assembly_component
