4.2.260 Derived_shape_element

NOTE: The text description for this Application Object has not been fully updated to agree with the EXPRESS.
A Derived_shape_element is a type of Non_feature_shape_element that is derived from a set of one or more other Shape_element. A Derived_shape_element is one of an Apex, a Centre_of_symmetry, a Geometric_alignment, a Geometric_intersection, a Linear_extension, or a Tangent_shape_element.

The data associated with this application object are the following:

  • deriving_relationships

4.2.260.1 deriving_relationships

The deriving_relationships is an inverse relationship that specifies that the existence of the Derived_shape_element is dependent on the existence of the Shape_element_deriving_relationship that specifies the Derived_shape_element as its relating_shape_element. There shall be one or more Shape_element_deriving_relationship specified as the deriving_relationships for a Derived_shape_element.

EXAMPLE: The Centre_axis of a cylindrical hole is a Derived_shape_element. The Centre_axis cannot exist unless the hole exists.

Formal constraints:

WR1: A Derived_shape_element shall not be derived from itself, either directly or through intermediate Derived_shape_elements.

ENTITY derived_shape_element
  ABSTRACT SUPERTYPE OF (ONEOF (apex,centre_of_symmetry,linear_extension,
      geometric_intersection,geometric_alignment,tangent_shape_element))
  SUBTYPE OF (non_feature_shape_element);
  INVERSE
    deriving_relationships : SET [1:?] OF
                             shape_element_deriving_relationship FOR 
                             relating_shape_element;
END_ENTITY; -- derived_shape_element
