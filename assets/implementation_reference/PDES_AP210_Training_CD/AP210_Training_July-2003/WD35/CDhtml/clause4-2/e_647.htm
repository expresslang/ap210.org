4.2.647 Person_and_organization

A Person_and_organization is a type of Managed_design_object. A Person_and_organization is the aggregation of a Person and an Organization.

The data associated with this application object are the following:

  • assigned_organization
  • assigned_person
  • person_id_in_organization

4.2.647.1 assigned_organization

The assigned_organization specifies the role of the Organization for the Person_and_organization.

4.2.647.2 assigned_person

The assigned_person specifies the role of the Person for the Person_and_organization.

4.2.647.3 person_id_in_organization

The person_id_in_organization specifies the identifier for the Person within the context of the Organization for the Person_and_organization. The person_in_organization is optional and need not be supplied.

Formal constraints:

UR1: The combination of the person_id_in_organization and the assigned_organization shall be unique within a population of Person_and_organizations.

UR2: The combination of the assign_person and the assigned_organization shall be unique within a population of Person_and_organizations.

ENTITY person_and_organization
  SUBTYPE OF (managed_design_object);
    assigned_person       : person;
    assigned_organization : organization;
    person_id_in_organization : OPTIONAL ee_name;
  UNIQUE
    ur1 : assigned_person, assigned_organization;
    ur2 : person_id_in_organization, assigned_organization;
END_ENTITY; -- person_and_organization
