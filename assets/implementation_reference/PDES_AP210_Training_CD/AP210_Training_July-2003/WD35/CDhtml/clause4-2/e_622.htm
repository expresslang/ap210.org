4.2.618 Part_mounting_feature

NOTE: The text description for this Application Object has not been fully updated to agree with the EXPRESS.
A Part_mounting_feature is a type of Part_feature that is identified to facilitate tolerance specification when an occurrence of the associated part is used in an assembly.

NOTE: The use of high density surface mount parts may require specification of mechanical mounting areas in addition to electrical terminals.

The data associated with this application object are the following:

  • mounting_connection_area

4.2.618.1 mounting_connection_area

The mounting_connection_area specifies the role of the Connection_zone for the Part_mounting_feature. The mounting_connection_area provides a means to geometrically specify a surface area for critical mechanical mounting properties.

NOTE: The Feature_shape_occurrence specified by the mounting feature may be the same Feature_shape_occurrence that is specified by the associated Connection_zone. In this case the whole mounting feature is the Connection_zone.
