4.2.853 Stratum_feature_conductive_join

NOTE: The text description for this Application Object has not been fully updated to agree with the EXPRESS.
A Stratum_feature_conductive_join is a type of Stratum_concept_relationship. A Stratum_feature_conductive_join may be a Physical_network_supporting_stratum_feature_conductive_join. A Stratum_feature_conductive_join is a connection between two conductive Stratum_feature on disjoint Stratum that is not a passage.

NOTE: The two facing surfaces of conductive features are joined.

The data associated with this application object are the following:

  • related_concept
  • relating_concept
  • using_via

4.2.853.1 related_concept

The related_concept specifies the source Stratum_feature_template_component for the Stratum_feature_conductive_join.

4.2.853.2 relating_concept

The relating_concept specifies the target Stratum_feature_template_component for the Stratum_feature_conductive_join.

4.2.853.3 using_via

The using_via is an inverse relationship that specifies that there shall be no more than one Bonded_conductive_base_blind_via that specifies the Stratum_feature_conductive_join as its features_join. The using_via need not be specified for a particular Stratum_feature_conductive_join.

Formal constraints:

WR1: Each member of Stratum_feature_template_component in the role of relating_concept for this Stratum_feature_conductive_join shall not be in the role of related_concept for this member of Stratum_feature_conductive_join.

WR2: A Stratum_feature_template_component shall not be an ancestor or descendant of itself in this relationship.

 wr1: related_concept.stratum_feature_implementation.resident_stratum :<>:
