4.2.500 Layer_connection_point_link

A Layer_connection_point_link is a type of Managed_design_object. A Layer_connection_point_link is a directed link between two Layer_connection_point in order to support the sequence of Layer_connection_point required in Unrouted_conductive_interconnect_element.

The data associated with this application object are the following:

  • associated_stratum
  • precedent_point
  • subsequent_point

4.2.500.1 associated_stratum

An associated_stratum specifies the role of the Stratum for a Layer_connection_point_link.

4.2.500.2 precedent_point

A precedent_point specifies one role of the Layer_connection_point for a Layer_connection_point_link.

4.2.500.3 subsequent_point

A subsequent_point specifies one role of the Layer_connection_point for a Layer_connection_point_link.

Formal constraints:

WR1: The precedent_point shall not be the subsequent_point.

WR2: The precedent_point shall be on the associated_stratum.

WR3: The subsequent_point shall be on the associated_stratum.

NOTE: 
*)
ENTITY layer_connection_point_link
  SUBTYPE OF (managed_design_object);
    associated_stratum : stratum;
    precedent_point    : layer_connection_point;
    subsequent_point   : layer_connection_point;
INVERSE
  referenced_by : unrouted_conductive_interconnect_element FOR 
                  connected_points;  
WHERE
  WR1 : precedent_point <> subsequent_point;
  WR2 : associated_stratum = precedent_point.resident_design_layer_stratum;
  WR3 : associated_stratum = subsequent_point.resident_design_layer_stratum;
END_ENTITY; -- layer_connection_point_link
