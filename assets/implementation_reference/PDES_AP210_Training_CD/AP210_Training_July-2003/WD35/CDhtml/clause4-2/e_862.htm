4.2.862 Stratum_feature_template_component

A Stratum_feature_template_component is a type of Laminate_component. A Stratum_feature_template_component may be a Land or a Non_functional_land.

The data associated with this application object are the following:

  • stratum_feature_implementation
  • usage_definition

4.2.862.1 stratum_feature_implementation

The stratum_feature_implementation specifies the Stratum_feature that the Stratum_feature_template_component is helping to compose.

4.2.862.2 usage_definition

The usage_definition specifies the role of the Stratum_feature_template for the Stratum_feature_template_component.
