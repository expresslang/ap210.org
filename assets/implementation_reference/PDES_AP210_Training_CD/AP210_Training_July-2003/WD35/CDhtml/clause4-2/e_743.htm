4.2.743 Profile_boundary_definition_with_offsets

NOTE: The text description for this Application Object has not been fully updated to agree with the EXPRESS.
A Profile_boundary_definition_with_offsets is a type of Profile_boundary_definition in which the offset of each Tolerance_zone_boundary_member relative to the True_profile is explicitly defined.

The data associated with this application object are the following:

  • boundary_offsets
  • defined_zone_boundary

4.2.743.1 boundary_offsets

The boundary_offsets specifies the Tolerance_zone_boundary_member_offset for each Tolerance_zone_boundary_member of the Tolerance_zone_explicit_opposing_boundary_set specified as the defined_zone_boundary. Each Tolerance_zone_boundary_member_offset characterizes the offset of a Tolerance_zone_boundary_member from the True_profile. There shall be exactly two Tolerance_zone_boundary_member_offset specified as the boundary_offsets for the Profile_boundary_definition_with_offsets.

4.2.743.2 defined_zone_boundary

The defined_zone_boundary specifies the Tolerance_zone_explicit_opposing_boundary_set that is characterized by the Profile_boundary_definition_with_offsets.

Formal constraints:

WR1: The sum of each Length_data_element specified as the offset_value by the two Tolerance_zone_boundary_member that are specified as the boundary_offsets shall equal the Length_data_element specified by the tolerance_value of the Boundary_size_characteristic specified as the specified_boundary_size.

WR2: The value of the label specified by the type_of_offset of one of the Tolerance_zone_boundary_member_offsets specified by the boundary_offsets shall be "away_from_material".

WR3: The value of the label specified by the type_of_offset of one of the Tolerance_zone_boundary_member_offsets specified by the boundary_offsets shall be "towards offset".
