4.2.712 Pre_defined_perpendicular_datum_axis_symbol_3d_2d_relationship

NOTE: The text description for this Application Object has not been fully updated to agree with the EXPRESS.
A Pre_defined_perpendicular_datum_axis_symbol_3d_2d_relationship is a type of Volume_shape_intersection. A Pre_defined_perpendicular_datum_axis_symbol_3d_2d_relationship establishes the geometric reference relationship between a Datum_axis representation in a 3 dimensional coordinate system and the related representation in a 2 dimensional coordinate system where the Datum_axis is perpendicular to the plane that represents the 2 dimensional coordinate system in the 3 dimensional coordinate system. The controlled information is the centre of the circle.

NOTE: Only the center of the circle representing the central axis is expected to be directly referenced. Due to differences in CAD systems resolutions the lines used as crosshairs may sightly deviate from intersecting at the center of the circle.
