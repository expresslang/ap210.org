4.2.69 Bevel_edge_feature_shape

NOTE: The text description for this Application Object has not been fully updated to agree with the EXPRESS.
A Bevel_edge_feature_shape is a type of Edge_shape that describes a bevelled edge of a feature with a two-dimensional shape.

EXAMPLE: Figure befs illustrates a detail of a fabrication drawing that presents some of the information provided by Bevel_edge_feature_shape. The figure illustrates the measurement conditions (including orientation) for generic width and angle attributes.

The data associated with this application object are the following:

  • maximum_bevel_angle
  • maximum_bevel_width
  • minimum_bevel_angle
  • minimum_bevel_width

4.2.69.1 maximum_bevel_angle

The maximum_bevel_angle specifies the largest positive value of an Angle_data_element measured in the plane of the Volume_shape_projection relative to the edge surface being beveled (Datum B in figure befs). If the maximum_bevel_angle is zero, no material shall be removed. If the maximum_bevel_angle is between zero and 90 degrees material shall be removed from the surface closest to the Stratum whose attribute primary_design_layer_stratum is TRUE. If the maximum_bevel_angle is 90 degrees, no material shall be removed. If the maximum_bevel_angle is between 90 and 180 degrees, material shall be removed from the surface furthest from the Stratum whose attribute primary_design_layer_stratum is TRUE. Maximum_bevel_angles greater than 180 shall not result in material removal.

4.2.69.2 maximum_bevel_width

The maximum_bevel_width specifies the largest positive value of the Length_data_element that is the width of the bevel relative to the edge surface that is beveled for the Bevel_edge_feature_shape.

4.2.69.3 minimum_bevel_angle

The minimum_bevel_angle specifies the smallest positive value of an Angle_data_element measured in the plane of the Volume_shape_projection relative to the edge surface being beveled (Datum B in figure befs). If the minimum_bevel_angle is zero, no material shall be removed. If the minimum_bevel_angle is between zero and 90 degrees material shall be removed from the surface closest to the Stratum whose attribute primary_design_layer_stratum is TRUE. If the bevel angle is 90 degrees, no material shall be removed. If minimum_bevel_angle is between 90 and 180 degrees, material shall be removed from the surface furthest from Stratum whose attribute primary_design_layer_stratum is TRUE. Bevel_angles greater than 180 shall not result in material removal.

4.2.69.4 minimum_bevel_width

The minimum_bevel_width specifies the smallest positive value for the Length_data_element that is the width of the bevel relative to the edge surface that is beveled for the Bevel_edge_feature_shape.

Formal constraints:

WR1: The Planar_projected_shape and Volume_shape_projection application objects shall be populated when this application object is populated, and shall be provided in the geometric model associated with this application object. Two members of Datum shall be populated when this application object is populated; one member shall be Datum A and the the other member shall be Datum B.
