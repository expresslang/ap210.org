4.2.9 Altered_package_terminal

NOTE: The text description for this Application Object has not been fully updated to agree with the EXPRESS.
An Altered_package_terminal is a type of Package_terminal. An Altered_package_terminal is one or more of a Length_trimmed_terminal, a Shape_formed_terminal, or a Surface_prepped_terminal. An Altered_package_terminal describes a Package_terminal that is the result of a modification process. Material or shape properties are different from the reference terminal.

EXAMPLE: The modification process may be performed prior to installing the occurrence of the Packaged_part onto a Printed circuit board.

The data associated with this application object are the following:

  • associated_definition
  • terminal_to_be_altered

4.2.9.1 associated_definition

The associated_definition specifies the role of the Altered_package for the Altered_package_terminal.

4.2.9.2 terminal_to_be_altered

The terminal_to_be_altered specifies the Package_terminal that will be modified for the Altered_package_terminal.

Formal constraints:

WR1: A Package_terminal shall not be in the role of terminal_to_be_altered for itself either directly or indirectly.

ENTITY altered_package_terminal
  ABSTRACT SUPERTYPE OF ((shape_formed_terminal ANDOR

  SUBTYPE OF (package_terminal);
    terminal_to_be_altered                  : package_terminal;
    SELF\part_feature.associated_definition : altered_package;
