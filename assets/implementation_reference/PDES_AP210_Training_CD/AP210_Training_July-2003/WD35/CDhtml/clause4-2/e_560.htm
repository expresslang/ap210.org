4.2.559 Non_feature_shape_definition

NOTE: The text description for this Application Object has not been fully updated to agree with the EXPRESS.
A Non_feature_shape_definition is a type of Shape_definition. A Non_feature_shape_definition may be one of a View_level_non_feature_shape_definition, or a Pre_defined_datum_axis_3d_symbol, or a Pre_defined_datum_plane_3d_symbol, or a Pre_defined_parallel_datum_axis_2d_symbol, or a Pre_defined_perpendicular_datum_axis_2d_symbol, or a Pre_defined_perpendicular_datum_plane_2d_symbol. A Non_feature_shape_definition may also be one of a Physical_unit_3d_keepout_shape, or a Physical_unit_planar_keepout_shape. A Non_feature_shape_definition shall be used to represent annotation information or other shape property information that is not appropriate for Feature_shape_definition.

NOTE: Shape information that is derived from a physical shape and whose algorithmic derivation details are unavailable may be exchanged with this Application Object.

The data associated with this application object are the following:

  • associated_element
  • model_shape

4.2.559.1 associated_element

The associated_element specifies the role of the Non_feature_shape_element for the Non_feature_shape_definition.

4.2.559.2 model_shape

The model_shape specifies the role of the Feature_shape_definition for the Non_feature_shape_definition. The model_shape need not be supplied.

ENTITY non_feature_shape_definition
  SUPERTYPE OF ((ONEOF(
    pre_defined_datum_axis_3d_symbol,
    pre_defined_datum_plane_3d_symbol,
    pre_defined_parallel_datum_axis_2d_symbol,
    pre_defined_perpendicular_datum_axis_2d_symbol,
    pre_defined_perpendicular_datum_plane_2d_symbol,
    view_level_non_feature_shape_definition)) ANDOR
    (ONEOF(physical_unit_3d_keepout_shape,
    physical_unit_planar_keepout_shape)));
  SUBTYPE OF (shape_definition);
     associated_element : non_feature_shape_element;
     model_shape        : OPTIONAL feature_shape_definition;
END_ENTITY; -- non_feature_shape_definition
