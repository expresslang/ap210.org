4.2.243 Datum_usage_in_datum_system_relationship

NOTE: The text description for this Application Object has not been fully updated to agree with the EXPRESS.
A Datum_usage_in_datum_system_relationship is a type of Managed_design_object. A Datum_usage_in_datum_system_relationship is a relationship that associates a Datum_system with a Datum that participates in establishing the Datum_system.

The data associated with this application object are the following:

  • established_datum_system
  • precedence_assignment
  • used_datum

4.2.243.1 established_datum_system

The established_datum_system specifies the Datum_system that the Datum specified as the used_datum participates in establishing for the Datum_usage_in_datum_system_relationship.

4.2.243.2 precedence_assignment

The precedence_assignment is an inverse relationship that specifies that the existence of the Datum_usage_in_datum_system_relationship is dependent on the existence of the Datum_precedence_assignment that specifies the Datum_usage_in_datum_system_relationship as its assigned_to.

4.2.243.3 used_datum

The used_datum specifies the Datum that is used to establish the Datum_system specified as the established_datum_system for the Datum_usage_in_datum_system_relationship.

Formal constraints:

UR1: The combination of used_datum and established_datum_system shall be unique within a population of Datum_usage_in_datum_system_relationship. That is, a specific Datum shall not be used more than once to establish a specific Datum_system.

WR1: The Datum specified as the used_datum shall either be a Common_datum or a Single_datum.

Informal constraints:

IP1: The Datum specified as the used_datum shall either be a constituent Datum of the Datum_reference_frame established by the Datum_system specified by the established_datum_system, or it shall be dimensioned from one or more constituent Datum of the established Datum_reference_frame.

ENTITY datum_usage_in_datum_system_relationship
  SUBTYPE OF (managed_design_object);
    established_datum_system : datum_system;
    used_datum               : datum;
  INVERSE
    precedence_assignment : datum_precedence_assignment FOR assigned_to;
  UNIQUE
    ur1 : used_datum, established_datum_system;
END_ENTITY; -- datum_usage_in_datum_system_relationship
