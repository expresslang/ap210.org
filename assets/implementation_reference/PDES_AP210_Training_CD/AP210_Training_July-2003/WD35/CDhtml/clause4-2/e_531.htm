4.2.531 Material_definition

A Material_definition is a type of Ee_product_definition. A Material_definition is intended solely to capture a minimal definition and identification of raw material for bill of material or product data purposes.

The data associated with this application object are the following:

  • material

4.2.531.1 material

The material specifies role of the Ee_material for the Material_definition.

NOTE:

ENTITY material_definition
  SUBTYPE OF (ee_product_definition);
  material: ee_material;
