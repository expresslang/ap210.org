4.2.800 Scalar_terminal_definition_link

NOTE: The text description for this Application Object has not been fully updated to agree with the EXPRESS.
A Scalar_terminal_definition_link is a type of Managed_design_object.

The data associated with this application object are the following:

  • precedent_terminal_definition
  • subsequent_terminal_definition

4.2.800.1 precedent_terminal_definition

The precedent_terminal_definition specifies a Scalar_terminal_definition for the Scalar_terminal_definition_link.

4.2.800.2 subsequent_terminal_definition

The subsequent_terminal_definition specifies a Scalar_terminal_definition for the Scalar_terminal_definition_link.

Formal constraints:

UR1: The combination of precedent_terminal_definition and subsequent_terminal_definition shall be unique within a population of Scalar_terminal_definition_link.

WR1: The precedent_terminal_definition shall not be the subsequent_terminal_definition within a population of Scalar_terminal_definition_link.
