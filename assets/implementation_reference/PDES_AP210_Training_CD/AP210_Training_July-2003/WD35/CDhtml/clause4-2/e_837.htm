4.2.837 Standard_uncertainty

NOTE: The text description for this Application Object has not been fully updated to agree with the EXPRESS.
A Standard_uncertainty is a type of Expanded_uncertainty. A Standard_uncertainty may be an Expanded_uncertainty. The uncertainty is defined in clause 2 of "The Guide to the Expression of Uncertainty in Measurement".
