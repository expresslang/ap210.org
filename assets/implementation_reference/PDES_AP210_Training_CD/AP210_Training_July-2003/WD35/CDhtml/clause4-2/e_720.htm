4.2.720 Primitive_2d

NOTE: The text description for this Application Object has not been fully updated to agree with the EXPRESS.
A Primitive_2d is a type of Shape_item. A Primitive_2d may be one of a Circular_area, an Elliptic_area, a Rectangular_area, or a Polygonal_area.

Formal constraints:

WR1: The dimensionality attribute of the Cartesian_coordinate_system referenced by the geometric_context attribute inherited from the Feature_shape_definition shall be two_dimensional within a population of Primitive_2d.

NOTE: 
*)

  SUBTYPE OF (shape_item);
