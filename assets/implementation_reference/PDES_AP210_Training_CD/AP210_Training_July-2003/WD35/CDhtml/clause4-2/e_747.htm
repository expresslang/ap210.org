4.2.747 Profile_of_any_surface_tolerance

NOTE: The text description for this Application Object has not been fully updated to agree with the EXPRESS.
A Profile_of_any_surface_tolerance is a type of Geometric_tolerance that specifies or partially specifies the design requirement on the form and possibly on the orientation, size, and location of a surface. A Profile_of_any_surface_tolerance is either a Profile_of_any_surface_tolerance_with_referenced_datum_system or a Profile_of_any_surface_tolerance_without_referenced_datum_system. A Profile_of_any_surface_tolerance may also be a Composite_surface_profile_refinement_tolerance. The Profile_of_any_surface_tolerance defines a uniform Tolerance_zone_boundary along the true profile of the surface within which the actual surface must lie. If the design requirements are such that the Tolerance_zone_boundary is unequally disposed about the True_profile, either unilaterally or bilateral, a Profile_boundary_definition_with_offsets shall be used to specify this requirement.

NOTE: Profile of any surface tolerance is defined in terms of tolerance zone definitions in 14.6 of ISO 1101.

Formal constraints:

WR1: The Shape_element specified as the toleranced_shape_element shall be a Physical_feature_or_part_template.

NOTE: The attribute toleranced_shape_element is declared in the Geometric_tolerance entity of which this entity is a subtype.

WR2: The Profile_of_any_surface_tolerance shall not be a Geometric_tolerance_with_applied_material_condition_property.

WR3: The Profile_of_any_surface_tolerance shall specify as the tolerance_zone_or_boundary_definition a Tolerance_zone_definition that specifies a Profile_boundary_definition as its boundary_definition.
