4.2.809 Shape_dimension_representation

NOTE: The text description for this Application Object has not been fully updated to agree with the EXPRESS.
A Shape_dimension_representation is a type of Managed_design_object. A Shape_dimension_representation is one of an Externally_defined_dimension_representation, a Limit_dimension_representation, a Plus_minus_tolerance_range_representation, or a Singular_dimension_representation. A Shape_dimension_representation is a representation of a Dimensional_characteristic. The Shape_dimension_representation explicitly describes one or more Dimensional_characteristic with a single value, a range of values, a combination of values, or values and references to the standard documents that define the meaning of these values.

Formal constraints:

WR1: The Shape_dimension_representation shall be specified as a representation of at least one Dimensional_characteristic_representation.

NOTE: 
*)
ENTITY shape_dimension_representation
  ABSTRACT SUPERTYPE OF (ONEOF (limit_dimension_representation,
      singular_dimension_representation,
	externally_defined_dimension_representation,
      plus_minus_tolerance_range_representation))
  SUBTYPE OF (managed_design_object);
END_ENTITY; -- shape_dimension_representation
