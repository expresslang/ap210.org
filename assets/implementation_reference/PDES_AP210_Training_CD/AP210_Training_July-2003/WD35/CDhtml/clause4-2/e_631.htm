4.2.627 Part_template_or_physical_unit_3d_shape_representation_relationship

NOTE: The text description for this Application Object has not been fully updated to agree with the EXPRESS.
A Part_template_or_physical_unit_3d_shape_representation_relationship is a type of Managed_design_object.

The data associated with this application object are the following:

  • principal_part_shape
  • related_part_shape

4.2.627.1 principal_part_shape

The principal_part_shape specifies the role of one of a Part_template_3d_shape, a Part_template_non_planar_2d_shape, or a Physical_unit_3d_shape for the Part_template_or_physical_unit_3d_shape_representation_relationship.

4.2.627.2 related_part_shape

The related_part_shape specifies the role of one of a Part_template_3d_shape, a Part_template_non_planar_2d_shape, or a Physical_unit_3d_shape for the Part_template_or_physical_unit_3d_shape_representation_relationship.

Formal constraints:

UR1: The combination of principal_part_shape and related_part_shape shall be unique within a population of Part_template_or_physical_unit_3d_shape_representation_relationship.

WR1: The principal_part_shape shall not be the related_part_shape within a population of Part_template_or_physical_unit_3d_shape_representation_relationship.

WR2: The principal_part_shape and related_part_shape shall be for the same definition or item and shall exist in the same Cartesian_coordinate_system within a population of Part_template_or_physical_unit_3d_shape_representation_relationship.
