4.2.538 Mating_connector_component

NOTE: The text description for this Application Object has not been fully updated to agree with the EXPRESS.
A Mating_connector_component is a type of Managed_design_object. A Mating_connector_component is the connector on the next higher assembly with which a connector on the assembly must mate. A Mating_connector_component may be specified in the role of mating_requirement by an Interface_component_allocation.

The data associated with this application object are the following:

  • definition
  • placement_context
  • reference_designation

4.2.538.1 definition

The definition specifies the role of either an Ee_specification, a Packaged_connector, or a Printed_connector_template for the Mating_connector_component.

4.2.538.2 placement_context

The placement_context is an inverse relationship that specifies that the existence of the Mating_connector_component is dependent on the existence of the Mating_connector_placement_relationship that specifies the Mating_connector_component as its placed_connector.

4.2.538.3 reference_designation

The reference_designation specifies the name that serves as the identifier of the location of the Mating_connector_component in the next level of assembly. The reference_designation need not be specified for a particular Mating_connector_component.
