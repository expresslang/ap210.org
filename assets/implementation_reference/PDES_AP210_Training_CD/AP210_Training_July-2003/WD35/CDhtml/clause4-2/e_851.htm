4.2.851 Stratum_feature

NOTE: The text description for this Application Object has not been fully updated to agree with the EXPRESS.
A Stratum_feature is a type of Physical_feature_or_part_template. A Stratum_feature may be either a Conductive_filled_area or a Conductor, or additionally may be a Fiducial_stratum_feature. A Stratum_feature is a shape realized in the material of the Stratum.

EXAMPLE: Figure sf illustrates several types of Stratum_feature. The Conductors shown are purposely distorted in aspect ratio because Conductors are enclosed shapes.

The data associated with this application object are the following:

  • feature_of_size
  • name
  • resident_stratum

4.2.851.1 feature_of_size

The feature_of_size specifies whether or not the Stratum_feature interfaces with a feature of another product in an assembly context. A value of TRUE indicates that the Stratum_feature interfaces with a feature of another product. A value of FALSE indicates that the Stratum_feature does not interface with a feature of another product.

NOTE: Certain tolerance information depends on knowledge of a particular feature interfacing with features of a mating product.

4.2.851.2 name

The name specifies the identifier of the Stratum_feature.

4.2.851.3 resident_stratum

The resident_stratum specifies the Stratum being composed by the Stratum_feature.

Formal constraints:

UR1: The combination of the name and the resident_stratum shall be unique within a population of Stratum_features.
