4.2.876 Styled_text

NOTE: The text description for this Application Object has not been fully updated to agree with the EXPRESS.
A Styled_text is a type of Shape_item that is formatted text that appears in the material in a Interconnect_module. The text shall be styled by font. The definition specifies font styling that shall be applied to realized characters. Externally defined text font and font family are supported.

NOTE: This part of ISO 10303 does not standardize the display of text on computer systems.

EXAMPLE: Figure st illustrates styling that may be applied to text that appears in the material. The principal styling supported is font size through the bounding box specification. The other variations illustrated in the figure occur through modification of the text orientation during placement, and may require grouping of the text with other shape data. Some of the justification and orientation capability portrayed in the figure is provided by the Laminate_text_string_component

The data associated with this application object are the following:

  • text_font

4.2.876.1 text_font

The text_font specifies either the Font_definition or the Registered_font for the Styled_text.
