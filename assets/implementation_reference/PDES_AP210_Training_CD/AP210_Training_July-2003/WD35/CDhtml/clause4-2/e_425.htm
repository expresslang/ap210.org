4.2.422 Geometric_tolerance_with_referenced_datum_system

NOTE: The text description for this Application Object has not been fully updated to agree with the EXPRESS.
A Geometric_tolerance_with_referenced_datum_system is a type of Geometric_tolerance that specifies or partially specifies the design requirement on one or more geometric characteristics, such as orientation, of the toleranced Shape_element in relation to the Datum_system defined by the Datum_system_defining_conditions that is specified as the datum_system_definition. In such cases, the Tolerance_zone is located and/or oriented; and thus the toleranced Shape_element is located and/or oriented; in relation to the Datum_system. A Geometric_tolerance_with_referenced_datum_system is one of an Angularity_tolerance, a Circular_runout_tolerance, a Concentricity_and_coaxiality_tolerance, a Parallelism_tolerance, a Perpendicularity_tolerance, a Profile_of_any_line_tolerance_with_referenced_datum_system, a Profile_of_any_surface_tolerance_with_referenced_datum_system, a Position_tolerance_with_referenced_datum_system, a Symmetry_tolerance, or a Total_runout_tolerance.

The data associated with this application object are the following:

  • datum_system_definition

4.2.422.1 datum_system_definition

The datum_system_definition specifies the Datum_system_defining_conditions that define the Datum_system that in relation to which the Geometric_tolerance_with_referenced_datum_system specifies or partially specifies design requirements.

Formal constraints:

WR1: The Geometric_tolerance_with_referenced_datum_system shall be specified by at most one Separate_geometric_tolerance_requirement as one of the separated_geometric_tolerances.
