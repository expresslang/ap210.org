4.2.885 Tangent_shape_element

NOTE: The text description for this Application Object has not been fully updated to agree with the EXPRESS.
A Tangent_shape_element is a type of Derived_shape_element that is either a straight line that contacts a curve at only the high points or is a plane that contacts a surface at only its high points. The Shape_element from which the Tangent_shape_element is derived is associated with the Tangent_shape_element by the Shape_element_deriving_relationship that is specified as the deriving_relationships. A Tangent_shape_element may be a Tangent_plane.

NOTE: The attribute deriving_relationships is declared in the Derived_shape_element entity of which this entity is a subtype.

Formal constraints:

WR1: The Tangent_shape_element shall be derived from only one Shape_element.

Informal constraints:

IP1: The Shape_element from which the Tangent_shape_element is derived shall be a curve or surface.

NOTE:

ENTITY tangent_shape_element
  SUPERTYPE OF (tangent_plane)
  SUBTYPE OF (derived_shape_element);
END_ENTITY; -- tangent_shape_element
