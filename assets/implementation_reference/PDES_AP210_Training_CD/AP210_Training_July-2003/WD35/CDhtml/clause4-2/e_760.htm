4.2.760 Range_characteristic

NOTE: The text description for this Application Object has not been fully updated to agree with the EXPRESS.
A Range_characteristic is a type of Coordinated_characteristic that specifies a range of values. The range may be open or closed. The range may be single ended.

The data associated with this application object are the following:

  • component_characteristic
  • range_type

4.2.760.1 component_characteristic

The component_characteristic specifies the value of the Numeric_parameter for the Range_characteristic. There shall be exactly two Numeric_parameter for the Range_characteristic.

4.2.760.2 range_type

The range_type specifies the classification of the Range_characteristic. The Range_characteristic shall be open, closed, lower_open or upper_open. Open shall be interpreted as not including the end point or points in the range. Closed shall be interpreted ass including the end point or points in the range.
