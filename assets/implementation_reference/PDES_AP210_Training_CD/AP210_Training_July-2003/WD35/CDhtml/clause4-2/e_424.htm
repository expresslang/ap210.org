4.2.421 Geometric_tolerance_with_applied_material_condition_property

NOTE: The text description for this Application Object has not been fully updated to agree with the EXPRESS.
A Geometric_tolerance_with_applied_material_condition_property is a type of Geometric_tolerance that specifies or partially specifies the design requirement on one or more geometric characteristics of the toleranced Shape_element in which the restrictiveness of the requirement is dependent on the actual size of the produced Shape_element or the actual size of the produced Shape_element from which the toleranced Shape_element is derived. A Geometric_tolerance_with_applied_material_condition_property in which the applied_material_condition_property_name is "maximum_material_principle" participates in specifying a requirement that is less stringent as the produced Shape_element deviates from its maximum material condition towards its least material condition. In this case, the specified size of the Tolerance_zone_boundary is the base size from which it is increased by the amount the produced Shape_element deviates from its maximum material condition towards its least material condition. A Geometric_tolerance_with_applied_material_condition_property in which the applied_material_condition_property_name is "least_material_requirement" participates in specifying a requirement that is less stringent as the produced Shape_element deviates from its least material condition towards its maximum material condition. In this case the specified size of the Tolerance_zone_boundary is the base size from which it is increased by the amount the produced Shape_element deviates from its least material condition towards its maximum material condition. A Geometric_tolerance_with_applied_material_condition_property may be a Boundary_based_position_tolerance.

The data associated with this application object are the following:

  • applied_material_condition_property_name

4.2.421.1 applied_material_condition_property_name

The applied_material_condition_property_name specifies the type of the applied material condition property, either "least_material_requirement" or "maximum_material_principle" for the Geometric_tolerance_with_applied_material_condition_property.

Formal constraints:

WR1: The Shape_element specified as the toleranced_shape_element shall either be a Centre_axis, Centre_plane, Centre_point, or Physical_feature_or_part_template.

NOTE: The attribute toleranced_shape_element is declared in the Geometric_tolerance entity of which this entity is a subtype.

WR2: If the Shape_element specified as the toleranced_shape_element is a Physical_feature_or_part_template, the Geometric_tolerance_with_applied_material_condition_property shall also be a Boundary_based_position_tolerance.
