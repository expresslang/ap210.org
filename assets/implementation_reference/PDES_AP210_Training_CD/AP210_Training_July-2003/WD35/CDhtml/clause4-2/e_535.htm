4.2.535 Material_removal_laminate_component

NOTE: The text description for this Application Object has not been fully updated to agree with the EXPRESS.
A Material_removal_laminate_component is a type of Laminate_component that indicates that material is to be removed in the realization of the feature that is in the role of design_intent. A Material_removal_laminate_component may be a Filled_area_material_removal_structure. The requirement shall be interpreted mathematically as a requirement for application of the regularized Boolean subtraction operation. A Material_removal_laminate_component may participate in a relationship with a Design_intent_modification_sequence in the case of complex layout arrangements to resolve the order of the Boolean operations.

The data associated with this application object are the following:

  • design_intent
  • usage_definition

4.2.535.1 design_intent

The design_intent specifies the role of the Stratum_feature for the Material_removal_laminate_component.

4.2.535.2 usage_definition

The usage_definition specifies the role of the Material_removal_feature_template for the Material_removal_laminate_component.
