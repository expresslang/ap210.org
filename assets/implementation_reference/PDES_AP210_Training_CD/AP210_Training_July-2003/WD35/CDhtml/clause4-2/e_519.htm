4.2.519 Located_interconnect_module_thickness_requirement

NOTE: The text description for this Application Object has not been fully updated to agree with the EXPRESS.
A Located_interconnect_module_thickness_requirement is a type of Managed_design_object. A Located_interconnect_module_thickness_requirement is a definition that states, at a specific location, that a thickness requirement exists for an Interconnect_module.

The data associated with this application object are the following:

  • design
  • reference_location
  • maximum_thickness_requirement
  • minimum_thickness_requirement

4.2.519.1 design

The design is an inverse relationship that specifies that the existence of the Located_interconnect_module_thickness_requirement is dependent on the existence of the Interconnect_module_usage_view that specifies the Located_interconnect_module_thickness_requirement as its overall_thickness_requirement. There shall be one or more Interconnect_module_usage_view for a Located_interconnect_module_thickness_requirement.

NOTE: Multiple versions of a product may have the same thickness requirement.

4.2.519.2 reference_location

The reference_location specifies the role of the Axis_placement for the Located_interconnect_module_thickness_requirement.

4.2.519.3 maximum_thickness_requirement

The maximum_thickness_requirement specifies the Length_data_element that is the upper bound for the Located_interconnect_module_thickness_requirement.

4.2.519.4 minimum_thickness_requirement

The minimum_thickness_requirement specifies the Length_data_element that is the lower bound for the Located_interconnect_module_thickness_requirement.

Formal constraints:

UR1: The combination of the design and the reference_location shall be unique within a population of Located_interconnect_module_thickness_requirements.

ENTITY located_interconnect_module_thickness_requirement
  SUBTYPE OF (managed_design_object);
    reference_location            : axis_placement;
    maximum_thickness_requirement : length_data_element;
    minimum_thickness_requirement : length_data_element;
  INVERSE
    design : SET [1:?] OF 
             interconnect_module_usage_view FOR located_thickness_requirement;
  UNIQUE
    ur1 : design, reference_location;
END_ENTITY; -- located_interconnect_module_thickness_requirement
