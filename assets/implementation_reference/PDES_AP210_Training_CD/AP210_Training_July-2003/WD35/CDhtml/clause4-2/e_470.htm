4.2.431 Inter_stratum_extent

NOTE: The text description for this Application Object has not been fully updated to agree with the EXPRESS.
An Inter_stratum_extent is a type of Managed_design_object. An Inter_stratum_extent is the extent of Stratum specified for a design purpose. The extent includes the from_stratum and to_stratum.

The data associated with this application object are the following:

  • from_stratum
  • to_stratum

4.2.431.1 from_stratum

The from_stratum specifies the Stratum at one end of the Inter_stratum_extent. This Stratum is the Stratum closer to the Design_layer_stratum whose primary_design_layer_stratum attribute value is TRUE than is the to_stratum.

NOTE: The from_stratum by convention is closest to the primary side of the assembly and furthest from the secondary side of the assembly.

4.2.431.2 to_stratum

The to_stratum specifies the Stratum at one end of the Inter_stratum_extent. This Stratum is further from the Design_layer_stratum whose primary_design_layer_stratum attribute value is TRUE than is the from_stratum.

NOTE: The to_stratum by convention is furthest from the primary side of the assembly, and closest to the secondary side of the assembly.

Formal constraints:

WR1: The members of stratum specified by from_stratum and to_stratum shall be associated with the same definition of the same product.

NOTE: When the from_stratum and to_stratum are the same Stratum the extent of the Inter_stratum_extent is that Stratum.

ENTITY inter_stratum_extent
  SUBTYPE OF (managed_design_object);
    from_stratum : stratum;
    to_stratum   : stratum;
END_ENTITY; -- inter_stratum_extent
