4.2.751 Profile_related_positional_boundary_definition

NOTE: The text description for this Application Object has not been fully updated to agree with the EXPRESS.
A Profile_related_positional_boundary_definition is a type of Positional_boundary_definition that characterizes a Profile_related_positional_boundary. A Profile_related_positional_boundary_definition is defined by a Boundary_based_position_tolerance in conjunction with a Profile_of_any_surface_tolerance.

The data associated with this application object are the following:

  • boundary_offset
  • defined_positional_boundary

4.2.751.1 boundary_offset

The boundary_offset specifies the Positional_boundary_offset that characterizes the offset of the Profile_related_positional_boundary for the Profile_related_positional_boundary_definition.

4.2.751.2 defined_positional_boundary

The defined_positional_boundary specifies the Profile_related_positional_boundary that is characterized by the Profile_related_positional_boundary_definition.

Formal constraints:

WR1: The Profile_related_positional_boundary specified as the offset_positional_boundary by the Positional_boundary_and_profile_boundary_member_relationship that is specified as the offset_boundary_relationship by the Positional_boundary_offset that is specified as the boundary_offset shall be the same instance of Profile_related_positional_boundary as specified as the defined_positional_boundary.

WR2: The Profile_related_positional_boundary_definition shall be specified by exactly one Geometric_tolerance as the tolerance_zone_or_boundary_definition, and that Geometric_tolerance shall be a Boundary_based_position_tolerance.
