4.2.39 Assembly_composition_relationship

NOTE: The text description for this Application Object has not been fully updated to agree with the EXPRESS.
An Assembly_composition_relationship is a type of Managed_design_object. An Assembly_composition_relationship may be a Next_higher_assembly_relationship. An Assembly_composition_relationship relates an Assembly_component to the assembly that it helps to compose.

The data associated with this application object are the following:

  • assembly
  • assembly_bonding_agent
  • component
  • security_code

4.2.39.1 assembly

The assembly specifies the Physical_unit_design_view for the product realized by this Assembly_composition_relationship.

4.2.39.2 assembly_bonding_agent

The assembly_bonding_agent specifies the role of the Ee_material for the Assembly_composition_relationship. There may be more than one Ee_material for the Assembly_composition_relationship. The assembly_bonding_agent need not be specified for a particular Assembly_composition_relationship.

NOTES:

  1. The use of this attribute is appropriate where no feature shape data is available.
  2. To identify quantity of bulk material, this part of ISO 10303 predefines Assembly_material_composition_relationship.

4.2.39.3 component

The component specifies the role of the Assembly_component for the Assembly_composition_relationship.

4.2.39.4 security_code

The security_code specifies the role of the Security_classification for the Assembly_composition_relationship. The security_code is optional and need not be supplied.

Formal constraints:

UR1: The combination of the assembly and the component shall be unique within a population of Assembly_composition_relationship.

WR1: At least one member of Assembly_composition_relationship shall specify a member of Assembly_component that is also a member of Interface_component.

NOTE: This part of ISO 10303 requires the interface to the next level of assembly to exist. This is because this part of ISO 10303 supports design and archival of designs that may be used in higher levels of assembly.

ENTITY assembly_composition_relationship
  SUPERTYPE OF (ONEOF (next_higher_assembly_relationship))
  SUBTYPE OF (managed_design_object);
    assembly               : physical_unit_design_view;
    component              : assembly_component;
    security_code          : OPTIONAL security_classification;
    assembly_bonding_agent : OPTIONAL SET [1:?] OF ee_material;
  UNIQUE
    ur1 : assembly, component;
END_ENTITY; -- assembly_composition_relationship
