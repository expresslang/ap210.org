4.2.160 Composite_linear_profile_locating_tolerance

NOTE: The text description for this Application Object has not been fully updated to agree with the EXPRESS.
A Composite_linear_profile_locating_tolerance is a type of Profile_of_any_line_tolerance_with_referenced_datum_system. The Composite_linear_profile_locating_tolerance is used in conjunction with one to three Composite_linear_profile_refinement_tolerance to apply a more restrictive specification on the form, orientation, and possibly size of a Physical_feature_or_part_template than on the location of the Physical_feature_or_part_template. The Tolerance_zone defined by the Composite_linear_profile_locating_tolerance is located relative to the Datum_system the definition of which is specified as the datum_system_definition.

NOTE: The attribute datum_system_definition is declared in the Geometric_tolerance_with_referenced_datum_system entity of which this entity is a subtype.

Formal constraints:

WR1: The Composite_linear_profile_locating_tolerance shall not also be a Composite_linear_profile_refinement_tolerance.

WR2: The Composite_linear_profile_locating_tolerance shall be specified as the refined_control by exactly one Composite_linear_profile_refinement_tolerance.
