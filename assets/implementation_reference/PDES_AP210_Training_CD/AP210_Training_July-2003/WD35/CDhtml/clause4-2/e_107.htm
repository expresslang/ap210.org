4.2.107 Component_2d_embedded_location

NOTE: The text description for this Application Object has not been fully updated to agree with the EXPRESS.
A Component_2d_embedded_location is a type of Component_2d_location. The Component_2d_embedded_location places components in a substrate under the constraint that is is required to maintain contact with Stratum_surface on both the top and bottom of the component.

NOTE: The Embedded_component_terminal referenced by the Fabrication_joint referenced by reference_joint should be a constituent of one of the surfaces referenced by precedent_surface or subsequent_surface.

The data associated with this application object are the following:

  • precedent_surface
  • subsequent_surface
  • reference_terminal

4.2.107.1 precedent_surface

The precedent_surface specifies the Adjacent_stratum_surface_embedded_component_surface_definition that joins the Component_feature of the component being located to the associated stratum surface. The Adjacent_stratum_surface_embedded_component_surface_definition in this role is identifying the stratum surface that is closer to the primary stratum in the substrate than is the embedded component itself.

4.2.107.2 subsequent_surface

The subsequent_surface specifies the Adjacent_stratum_surface_embedded_component_surface_definition that joins the Component_feature of the component being located to the associated stratum surface. The Adjacent_stratum_surface_embedded_component_surface_definition in this role is identifying the stratum surface that is furthere away from the primary stratum in the substrate than is the embedded component itself.

4.2.107.3 reference_terminal

The reference_terminal specifies the Fabrication_joint that joins the Primary_reference_terminal of the component being located to the Land_join_terminal or Non_functional_land_join_terminal that implements the internal connection to the circuitry in the rest of the substrate.

Formal constraints:

WR1: The Embedded_component_terminal referenced by reference_joint shall reference the same component as that referenced by the Component_feature referenced by the Adjacent_stratum_surface_embedded_component_surface_definition referenced by either the precedent_surface or the subsequent_surface.
