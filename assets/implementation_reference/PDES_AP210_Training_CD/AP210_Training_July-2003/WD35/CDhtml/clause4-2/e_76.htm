4.2.77 Boundary_based_position_tolerance

NOTE: The text description for this Application Object has not been fully updated to agree with the EXPRESS.
A Boundary_based_position_tolerance is a type of Position_tolerance_with_referenced_datum_system and a type of Geometric_tolerance_with_applied_material_condition_property. The Boundary_based_position_tolerance defines a Tolerance_zone_boundary that is offset from either the least material condition boundary or the maximum material condition boundary depending on the value of the applied_material_condition_property_name.

NOTE: The attribute applied_material_condition_property_name is declared in the Geometric_tolerance_with_applied_material_condition_property entity of which this entity is a subtype.
The least material condition boundary and the maximum material condition boundary are established either by one or more Dimensional_characteristic or by a Profile_of_any_surface_tolerance. If the Boundary_based_position_tolerance specifies an applied_material_condition_property_name of "maximum_material_principle", the Tolerance_zone_boundary is offset from the maximum material condition boundary by half of the offset value and in the direction away from the least material condition boundary. If the Boundary_based_position_tolerance specifies an applied_material_condition_property_name of "least_material_requirement", then the Tolerance_zone_boundary is offset from the least material condition boundary by half of the offset value and in the direction away from the maximum material condition boundary.

NOTE: Boundary based position tolerances are discussed in 5.10.1 and 6.5.5.1 of ASME Y14.5M.

The data associated with this application object are the following:

  • profile_control

4.2.77.1 profile_control

The profile_control specifies a Profile_of_any_surface_tolerance_with_referenced_datum_system that defines a Tolerance_zone_boundary that establishes the least material condition boundary and the maximum material condition boundary from which the Positional_boundary defined by the Boundary_based_position_tolerance is based. A profile_control need not be specified for a Boundary_based_position_tolerance.

Formal constraints:

WR1: The Shape_element specified as the toleranced_shape_element shall be a Physical_feature_or_part_template.

NOTE: The attribute toleranced_shape_element is declared in the Geometric_tolerance entity of which this entity is a subtype.

WR2: If a Profile_of_any_surface_tolerance_with_referenced_datum_system is specified as the profile_control, both the Profile_of_any_surface_tolerance_with_referenced_datum_system and the Boundary_based_position_tolerance shall specify the same instance of Physical_feature_or_part_template as their toleranced_shape_element.

WR3: If a Profile_of_any_surface_tolerance_with_referenced_datum_system is specified as the profile_control, the Datum in the Datum_system of the Profile_of_any_surface_tolerance_with_referenced_datum_system shall be in the Datum_system of the Boundary_based_position_tolerance and shall be assigned the same datum precedence.

WR4: If the Boundary_based_position_tolerance specifies a Profile_of_any_surface_tolerance as the profile_control, the Boundary_based_position_tolerance shall specify a Profile_related_positional_boundary_definition as the tolerance_zone_or_boundary_definition; otherwise, the Boundary_based_position_tolerance shall specify a Positional_boundary_member_definition as the tolerance_zone_or_boundary_definition.
