4.2.27 Apex

NOTE: The text description for this Application Object has not been fully updated to agree with the EXPRESS.
An Apex is a type of Derived_shape_element that is either a point that is derived by projecting a conical surface or the common point that is derived by projecting two or more conical surfaces. The Shape_element from which the Apex is derived are associated with the Apex by the Shape_element_deriving_relationship that are specified as the deriving_relationships.

NOTE: The attribute deriving_relationships is declared in the Derived_shape_element entity of which this entity is a subtype.

Informal constraints:

IP1: The common intersection of three or more planes or two curves shall be treated as a Geometric_intersection rather than as an Apex.

IP2: The form of the Shape_elements from which the Apex is derived shall be consistent with conical surfaces from which an Apex can be derived.

NOTE: 
*)
ENTITY apex
  SUBTYPE OF (derived_shape_element);
END_ENTITY; -- apex
