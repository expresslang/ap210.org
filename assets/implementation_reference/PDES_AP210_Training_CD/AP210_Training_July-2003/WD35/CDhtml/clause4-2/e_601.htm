4.2.602 Parallelism_tolerance

NOTE: The text description for this Application Object has not been fully updated to agree with the EXPRESS.
A Parallelism_tolerance is a type of Geometric_tolerance_with_referenced_datum_system that specifies or partially specifies the design requirement on the parallelism of a Shape_element in relation to a Datum_plane or Datum_axis.

NOTE: Parallelism tolerance is defined in terms of tolerance zone definitions in 14.7 of ISO 1101.

Formal constraints:

WR1: The Shape_element specified as the toleranced_shape_element shall be a Centre_axis, Centre_plane, Physical_feature_or_part_template, or Tangent_plane.

NOTE: The attribute toleranced_shape_element is declared in the Geometric_tolerance entity of which this entity is a subtype.

WR2: There shall be at most two Datum in the Datum_system the definition of which is specified as the datum_system_definition.

NOTE: The attribute datum_system_definition is declared in the Geometric_tolerance_with_referenced_datum_system entity of which this entity is a subtype.

WR3: At least one Datum_plane or Datum_axis shall be in the Datum_system the definition of which is specified as the datum_system_definition.

WR4: If the toleranced_shape_element is a Centre_plane, Physical_feature_or_part_template, or Tangent_plane, the Parallelism_tolerance shall specify a Tolerance_zone_definition as its tolerance_zone_or_boundary_definition; and that Tolerance_zone_definition shall specify a Tolerance_zone_boundary_definition_with_specified_size as its boundary_definition; and that Tolerance_zone_boundary_definition_with_specified_size shall specify an Opposing_boundary_set_size_characteristic as its specified_boundary_size.

Informal constraints:

IP1: The form of the toleranced Shape_element shall be consistent with the form of a shape element for which the application of a parallelism tolerance is sensible.
