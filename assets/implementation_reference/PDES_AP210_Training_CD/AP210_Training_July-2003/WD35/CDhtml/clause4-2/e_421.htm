4.2.419 Geometric_tolerance

NOTE: The text description for this Application Object has not been fully updated to agree with the EXPRESS.
A Geometric_tolerance is a type of Managed_design_object. A Geometric_tolerance is one of a Geometric_tolerance_without_referenced_datum_system or a Geometric_tolerance_with_referenced_datum_system. Additionally a Geometric_tolerance may be one of a Position_tolerance, a Profile_of_any_line_tolerance, or a Profile_of_any_surface_tolerance. A Geometric_tolerance may be Geometric_tolerance_with_applied_material_condition_property. Additionally, a Geometric_tolerance may be a Statistical_geometric_tolerance. A Geometric_tolerance is a specification or partial specification of a design requirement on one or more of the form, location, or orientation characteristics of a Shape_element. This type of design requirement is specified in terms of a Tolerance_zone within which the toleranced Shape_element must reside; or in the case of a Boundary_based_position_tolerance, a boundary that the toleranced Shape_element must not violate. A Geometric_tolerance_with_referenced_datum_system participates in specifying a design requirement when the requirement is on one or more characteristics such as location of the toleranced Shape_element in relation to a Datum_system. In such cases, the Tolerance_zone is located and/or oriented, and thus the toleranced Shape_element is located and/or oriented, in relation to the Datum_system. In some cases the design requirement is such that the boundary of the Tolerance_zone is on a per unit basis.

EXAMPLE: The design requirement on the flatness of a surface is such that the surface should not deviate by more than 0.5 mm in any 10 mm by 10 mm area. A Geometric_tolerance that defines a Tolerance_zone_definition_with_per_unit_size_specification participates in specifying this type of requirement.
In some cases a design requirement on the geometric characteristic or characteristics of a Shape_element is such that it is dependent upon the produced size of the Shape_element, or upon the produced size of the Shape_element from which it is derived. A Geometric_tolerance_with_applied_material_condition_property specifies this type of requirement.

The data associated with this application object are the following:

  • tolerance_zone_or_boundary_definition
  • toleranced_shape_element

4.2.419.1 tolerance_zone_or_boundary_definition

The tolerance_zone_or_boundary_definition specifies either a Conical_tolerance_zone_boundary_diametrical_size_characteristic, a Positional_boundary_member_definition, a Profile_related_positional_boundary_definition, or a Tolerance_zone_definition that defines the constraints placed on the Shape_element specified as the toleranced_shape_element.

4.2.419.2 toleranced_shape_element

The toleranced_shape_element specifies either a Centre_Axis, Centre_plane, Centre_point, Physical_feature_or_part_template, or Tangent_plane to which the Geometric_tolerance is applied.

Formal constraints:

WR1: The Geometric_tolerance shall either be a Geometric_tolerance_with_referenced_datum_system or a Geometric_tolerance_without_referenced_datum_system.

WR2: The Geometric_tolerance shall be specified by at most one Simultaneous_geometric_tolerance_requirement as one of the grouped_geometric_tolerances.

WR3: There shall be exactly one Ee_requirement_occurrence with the description of "Geometric Dimensioning and Tolerancing Reference" that specifies at least one Ee_specification as the requirement_specification.

Informal constraints:

IP1: There shall be exactly one Ee_requirement_occurrence with the description of "Geometric Dimensioning and Tolerancing Reference" that specifies at least one Ee_specification as the requirement_specification. The Ee_specification shall reference those standards by which the geometric dimensioning and tolerancing data is to be interpreted.

EXAMPLE: An Ee_requirement_occurrence with a description of "Geometric Dimensioning and Tolerancing Reference" specifies an Ee_specification as the requirement_specification. The Ee_specification may specify "Technical drawings -- Geometrical tolerancing -- Tolerancing of form, orientation, location and run-out -- Generalities, definitions, symbols, indications on drawings" as the document_name, 1995 as the year of the creation_date, "ISO" as the publisher, and an Ee_document_identifier as the base_identifier that has an assigned_value of "1101" and an identifier_name of "document number".

ENTITY geometric_tolerance
  ABSTRACT SUPERTYPE OF (((ONEOF (
      geometric_tolerance_with_referenced_datum_system,
      geometric_tolerance_without_referenced_datum_system) ANDOR ONEOF (
      profile_of_any_line_tolerance,position_tolerance,
      profile_of_any_surface_tolerance)) ANDOR 
      geometric_tolerance_with_applied_material_condition_property) ANDOR
      statistical_geometric_tolerance)
  SUBTYPE OF (managed_design_object);
    tolerance_zone_or_boundary_definition : constraining_element_definitions;
    toleranced_shape_element              : toleranceable_shape_element;
END_ENTITY; -- geometric_tolerance
