4.2.638 Passage_technology

NOTE: The text description for this Application Object has not been fully updated to agree with the EXPRESS.
A Passage_technology is a type of Managed_design_object. A Passage_technology may be either a Default_component_termination_passage_definition, a Default_unsupported_passage_definition, or a Default_via_definition. A Passage_technology specifies material properties and parametric data associated with a specific manufacturing process for identified material, where that material and manufacturing process has been selected for a specific design. A Passage_technology includes the material stackupt parametric data related to the design of the interconnect substrate associated with the Stratum the Passage_technology is related to through the as_finished_inter_stratum_extent attribute.

NOTE: Passage_technology also supports substrate edges.

The data associated with this application object are the following:

  • associated_definition
  • as_finished_inter_stratum_extent
  • deposition_material
  • maximum_aspect_ratio
  • maximum_as_finished_deposition_thickness
  • maximum_as_finished_passage_extent
  • minimum_as_finished_deposition_thickness
  • minimum_as_finished_passage_extent
  • name
  • plated_passage
  • specification

4.2.638.1 associated_definition

The associated_definition specifies the role of the Interconnect_module that uses the Passage_technology.

NOTE: The Passage_technology is for a particular design since the vertical extent is within the context of the design.

4.2.638.2 as_finished_inter_stratum_extent

The as_finished_inter_stratum_extent specifies the Inter_stratum_extent that will be created for the Passage_technology. Material additions by plating or other means will extend the reach of the Inter_stratum_extent to the as-finished condition because these Stratum are not present during the machining operation but are created during the finishing operations.

4.2.638.3 deposition_material

The deposition_material specifies the role of the Ee_material that forms the vertical wall of a feature that uses the Passage_technology. The deposition_material need not be specified for a particular Passage_technology.

4.2.638.4 maximum_aspect_ratio

The maximum_aspect_ratio specifies the largest allowable ratio of passage length divided by passage diameter for the Passage_technology. The maximum_aspect_ratio need not be specified for a particular Passage_technology.

NOTE: The aspect ratio is a factor in determining the expected quality of the electroplating process in depositing metal in the passage. If the aspect ratio is large, meaning an extremely long passage in relation to its diameter, the deposition will be thinner in the central portion of the passage length than at each end of the passage. For this reason, a maximum_aspect_ratio is often determined as a factor in deciding upon the smallest passage diameters to be allowed in a PCB design. Thicker PCBs require larger minimum passage diameters, for the same expected plating quality.

4.2.638.5 maximum_as_finished_deposition_thickness

The maximum_as_finished_deposition_thickness specifies the Length_data_element that is the upper bound of the thickness of the deposition metal in the passage by the Passage_technology.

NOTE: Material deposition within a passage, by plating or other means, decreases the diameter of the passage. This attribute assists in providing the information required to select the correct tool for creating the passage before plating. The maximum deposition thickness results in the smallest passage diameter after finishing.

4.2.638.6 maximum_as_finished_passage_extent

The maximum_as_finished_passage_extent specifies the Length_data_element of the upper bound of the diametrical extent after processing for the Passage_technology. A maximum_as_finished_passage_extent need not be specified for a particular Passage_technology.

4.2.638.7 minimum_as_finished_deposition_thickness

The minimum_as_finished_deposition_thickness specifies the Length_data_element of the lower bound of the thickness of deposited plating in the passage of a PCB for the Passage_technology. The minimum_as_finished_deposition_thickness need not be specified for a particular Passage_technology.

NOTE: In order to help insure continuity of the deposited conductive material in a passage, and therefore to help insure connectivity between the plating in a passage and the lands of every Design_layer_stratum that intersect that passage, a minimum plating thickness is specified. This plating thickness is usually verified in the fabrication process by including test coupons in the panel of PCBs being fabricated. These coupons include typically sized lands and passages as are found on the PCBs being fabricated; the coupons are removed from the panel during fabrication and metallurgical cross sections are created for process quality verification. These cross sections provide visibility of both the electroless and electrolytic plating process quality as well as the quality of the final tin-lead plating or coating of the passage walls as well as of the so-called knee area, where the passage wall intersects the lands at each end of the passage. Solderability of the tin-lead is partially a factor of the minimum plating thickness at the knee, since tin-lead solder and copper form a visible intermetallic structure when one dissolves into the other during the process of melting the solder. This visible intermetallic structure is brittle and is not indicative of a solder joint of high quality. There must be ample solder, in the bare PCB, over this intermetallic knee, in order to assist in providing a PCB that has good solderability to the assembly operation.

4.2.638.8 minimum_as_finished_passage_extent

The minimum_as_finished_passage_extent specifies the Length_data_element of the lower bound of the diametrical extent for the Passage_technology. The minimum_as_finished_passage_extent need not be specified for a particular Passage_technology.

4.2.638.9 name

The name identifies the Passage_technology.

4.2.638.10 plated_passage

The plated_passage specifies whether or not the deposition_material is added to the passage for the Passage_technology. The value of TRUE indicates that the material is added. The value of FALSE indicates that the material is not added.

NOTES:

  1. This boolean makes no reference to the process to be used for adding a tin-lead alloy or other solder to the conductive walls of the plated_passage. Some processes electroplate the tin-lead alloy and later melt, or reflow, the alloy to reduce its granular appearance; other processes coat the passage walls and other exposed conductive surfaces of the PCB with molten solder in a process called solder-coating. These processes are only applicable to plated_passages in the PCB.
  2. This part of ISO 10303 does not presume a Pcb wet-chemistry only process, but allows for other processes of metallization of passage walls.

4.2.638.11 specification

The specifcation establishes the role of Process_specification for the Passage_technology. The specification need not be specified for a particular Passage_technology.

Formal constraints:

UR1: The name shall be unique within associated_definition.

WR1: Each passage that is plated shall have deposition_material provided.

WR2: Each passage that has maximum_as_finished_deposition_thickness provided shall have deposition_material provided.

WR3: Each passage that has minimum_as_finished_deposition_thickness provided shall have deposition_material provided.

WR4: If plated_passage has a value of TRUE Passage_technology shall reference Material_conductivity_classification that equals "conductive".

ENTITY passage_technology
  SUPERTYPE OF (ONEOF (default_via_definition,
      default_component_termination_passage_definition,
      default_unsupported_passage_definition))
  SUBTYPE OF (managed_design_object);
    associated_definition                    : interconnect_module;
    as_finished_inter_stratum_extent         : inter_stratum_extent;
    deposition_material                      : OPTIONAL ee_material;
    plated_passage                           : BOOLEAN;
    maximum_as_finished_passage_extent       : OPTIONAL length_data_element;
    minimum_as_finished_passage_extent       : OPTIONAL length_data_element;
    name                                     : ee_name;
    maximum_as_finished_deposition_thickness : OPTIONAL length_data_element;
    maximum_aspect_ratio                     : OPTIONAL REAL;
    minimum_as_finished_deposition_thickness : OPTIONAL length_data_element;
    specification                            : OPTIONAL process_specification;
  UNIQUE
    ur1 : name,associated_definition;
END_ENTITY; -- passage_technology
