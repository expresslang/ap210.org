4.2.869 Stratum_surface_in_stratum_3d_position

NOTE: The text description for this Application Object has not been fully updated to agree with the EXPRESS.
A Stratum_surface_in_stratum_3d_position is a type of Managed_design_object. A Stratum_surface_in_stratum_3d_position is the three-dimensional position and orientation of a two-dimensional constructed surface model in the three-dimensional model of the Stratum.

NOTE: The surface represented by the construct may be constructed in a CAD system as a design abstraction or may be a realizeable surface.

The data associated with this application object are the following:

  • stratum_shape
  • surface_shape
  • transformation

4.2.869.1 stratum_shape

The stratum_shape specifies the role of the Stratum_3d_shape for the Stratum_surface_in_stratum_3d_position.

4.2.869.2 surface_shape

The surface_shape specifies the role of either the Stratum_average_surface_shape or of the Stratum_surface_shape for the Stratum_surface_in_stratum_3d_position.

4.2.869.3 transformation

The transformation specifies the role of the Axis_placement_3d for the Stratum_surface_in_stratum_3d_position.
