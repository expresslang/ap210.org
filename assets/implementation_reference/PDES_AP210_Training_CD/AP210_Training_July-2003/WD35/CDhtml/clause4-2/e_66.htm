4.2.66 Bare_die_terminal

NOTE: The text description for this Application Object has not been fully updated to agree with the EXPRESS.
A Bare_die_terminal is a type of Minimally_defined_bare_die_terminal. A Bare_die_terminal is the surface area on a Bare_die that corresponds to the functional terminal on that Bare_die.

EXAMPLE: A device_terminal_map in combination with a part_device_terminal are populated to map the functionality of a Bare_die to the functionality of the packaged_part for the occurrence of the Bare_die in the Packaged_part.

The data associated with this application object are the following:

  • external_connection_area
  • material

4.2.66.1 external_connection_area

The external_connection_area specifies the Connection_zone of the Bare_die_terminal. The external_connection_area is the area on the surface of a Bare_die to which an electrically conducting material shall be attached to access the electrical functionality of the Bare_die. There may be more than one Connection_zone for a Bare_die_terminal. The external_connection_area is optional and need not be supplied if the data is unavailable in the source system. If the data is available in the source system, it shall be supplied.

NOTE: The Feature_shape_occurrence inherited by the terminal may be the same Feature_shape_occurrence that is specified by the associated Connection_zone. In this case the whole terminal is the Connection_zone.

4.2.66.2 material

The material specifies the role of the Ee_material for the Bare_die_terminal.

Formal constraints:

WR1: The points comprising the external_connection_area shall reside in the shape of the Bare_die being accessed.

WR2: The points comprising the external_connection_area shall be defined in the coordinate system of the device being accessed via the external_connection_area.
