4.2.884 Tangent_plane

NOTE: The text description for this Application Object has not been fully updated to agree with the EXPRESS.
A Tangent_plane is a type of Tangent_shape_element that is planar in form.

EXAMPLE: An Angularity_tolerance might be applied to the Tangent_plane derived from a surface of a Physical_unit, as opposed to the surface itself, if variations in the form of the surface are unimportant or are sufficiently constrained by other tolerances.

Informal constraints:

IP1: The form of the Shape_element from which the Tangent_plane is derived shall be consistent with the form of a shape element from which a tangent plane can be derived.

NOTE: 
*)
ENTITY tangent_plane
  SUBTYPE OF (tangent_shape_element);
END_ENTITY; -- tangent_plane
