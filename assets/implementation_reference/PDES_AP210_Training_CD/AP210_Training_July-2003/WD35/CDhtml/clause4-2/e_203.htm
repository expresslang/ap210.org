4.2.203 Curve

NOTE: The text description for this Application Object has not been fully updated to agree with the EXPRESS.
A Curve is a type of Shape_item. A Curve may be a Bounding_curve or a Closed_curve, or additionally may be a Composite_curve or additionally may be a Trimmed_line. A Curve is the path of a point moving through its three-dimensional coordinate space or of a point moving through its two-dimensional coordinate space. A Curve shall be arc-wise connected. A Curve shall have an arc length greater than zero.

NOTE: 
*)
ENTITY curve
  SUPERTYPE OF ((ONEOF (closed_curve,bounding_curve) ANDOR trimmed_line) 
      ANDOR composite_curve)
  SUBTYPE OF (shape_item);
END_ENTITY; -- curve
