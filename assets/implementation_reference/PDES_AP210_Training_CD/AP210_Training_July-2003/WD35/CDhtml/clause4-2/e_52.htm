4.2.52 Assembly_module_usage_view_connector_relationship

NOTE: The text description for this Application Object has not been fully updated to agree with the EXPRESS.
An Assembly_module_usage_view_connector_relationship is a type of Usage_concept_usage_relationship that associates the placement and orientation of a Packaged_connector with an Assembly_module_usage_view. An Assembly_module_usage_view_connector_relationship supports the identification of connector placement data, when the design details are not available.

The data associated with this application object are the following:

  • externally_visible_partial_reference_designation

4.2.52.1 externally_visible_partial_reference_designation

An externally_visible_partial_reference_designation specifies an Ee_name for an Assembly_module_usage_view_connector_relationship. An externally_visible_partial_reference_designation supports the identification of a connector as a partial reference designation independent of the internal reference designation assigned by the designer of the product. This independence is required when the connector is actually designed as part of a complete lower level assembly.

Formal constraints:

UR1: The combination of externally_visible_partial_reference_designation and associating_usage inherited from Usage_concept_usage_relationship shall be unique in a population of Assembly_module_usage_view_connector_relationship.

Informal constraints:

IP1: The associating_usage inherited from Usage_concept_usage_relationship shall be an Assembly_module_usage_view.

IP2: The associated_usage inherited from Usage_concept_usage_relationship shall be a Packaged_connector.
