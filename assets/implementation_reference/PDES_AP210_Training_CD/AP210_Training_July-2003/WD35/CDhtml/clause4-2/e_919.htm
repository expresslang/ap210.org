4.2.919 Tolerance_zone_circular_or_cylindrical_or_spherical_boundary

NOTE: The text description for this Application Object has not been fully updated to agree with the EXPRESS.
A Tolerance_zone_circular_or_cylindrical_or_spherical_boundary is a type of Tolerance_zone_boundary that is circular, cylindrical, or spherical in form. The actual form of the Tolerance_zone_boundary is dictated by the type of tolerance and the form of the toleranced Shape_element .

The data associated with this application object are the following:

  • size_characteristic

4.2.919.1 size_characteristic

A size_characteristic is an inverse relationship that specifies that the existence of the Tolerance_zone_circular_or_cylindrical_or_spherical_boundary is dependent on the existence of the Diametrical_boundary_size_characteristic that specifies the Tolerance_zone_circular_or_cylindrical_or_spherical_boundary as its sized_boundary.

EXAMPLE: A cylindrical Tolerance_zone_boundary could be defined by a position tolerance applied to an axis of a cylindrical hole.
