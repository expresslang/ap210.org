4.2.42 Assembly_material_composition_relationship

An Assembly_material_composition_relationship is a type of Managed_design_object. An Assembly_material_composition_relationship is the association between a Physical_unit_design_view and Ee_material for the purpose of bulk material callout.

NOTE: This application object is intended solely to support raw material callout whose shape is not explicitly modeled in the product model.

EXAMPLE: Solder identified as a bonding agent may be aggregated for the assembly using this Application Object.

The data associated with this application object are the following:

  • as_required
  • assembly
  • component_material
  • quantity
  • security_code

4.2.42.1 as_required

The as_required specifies the LOGICAL type for the Assembly_material_composition_relationship. This type permits a user to specify that the amount of material to be supplied with the product may be determined at assembly on an item by item basis, where the item referred to is a real product delivered to a customer.

EXAMPLE: Adhesives, insulation and wire may all be cases where the value of the attribute is TRUE.

4.2.42.2 assembly

The assembly specifies the role of the Physical_unit_design_view for the Assembly_material_composition_relationship.

4.2.42.3 component_material

The component_material specifies the role of the Material_definition for the Assembly_material_composition_relationship.

4.2.42.4 quantity

The quantity specifies the role of the Data_element for the Assembly_material_composition_relationship. The quantity need not be specified for the Assembly_material_composition_relationship.

4.2.42.5 security_code

The security_code specifies the role of the Security_classification for the Assembly_material_composition_relationship. The security_code is optional and need not be supplied.

Formal constraints:

WR1: For an occurrence of a bulk material product, units shall be specified as one of the following: length_unit; amount_of_substance_unit; mass_unit; volume_unit; area_unit.

Informal constraints:

IP1: Wire used for jumpers that are explicitly defined shall be specified as a member of Interconnect_component.

NOTE: 
*)
ENTITY assembly_material_composition_relationship
  SUBTYPE OF (managed_design_object);
    quantity           : OPTIONAL data_element;
    assembly           : physical_unit_design_view;
    component_material : material_definition;
    security_code      : OPTIONAL security_classification;
    as_required        : LOGICAL;
END_ENTITY; -- assembly_material_composition_relationship
