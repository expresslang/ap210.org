4.2.432 Inter_stratum_feature

NOTE: The text description for this Application Object has not been fully updated to agree with the EXPRESS.
An Inter_stratum_feature is a type of Laminate_component. An Inter_stratum_feature is one of a Cutout, a Cutout_edge_segment, a Dielectric_material_passage, an Interconnect_module_edge, an Interconnect_module_edge_segment, a Plated_inter_stratum_feature, or an Unsupported_passage. An Inter_stratum_feature spans one or more Stratum.

NOTES:

  1. An Inter_stratum_feature may be realized by removal of material by drilling, routing, punching, chemically etching, laser burning, or otherwise penetrating one or more sequential Stratum; by the addition of material; or by combining both removal and addition of material in the fabrication process.
  2. Keep out or keep in regions can be specified by populating Interconnect_module_constraint_region and its optional set of Interconnect_module_desgin_object_select. The Interconnect_module_design_object_select can select an Inter_startum_feature as the design feature of interest.

NOTE: Minimum and maximum material conditions are related to Inter_stratum_feature by its attribute usage_definition. The maximum and minimum material conditions established keep-out areas. Since these maximum and minimum conditions reflect a fabrication process, the designer does not have this area to locate other features. A situation such as tight spacing of conductors on a PWB must take into account the maximum and minimum material condition or the design wil not be optimally realized through the fabrication process.

The data associated with this application object are the following:

  • feature_of_size
  • usage_definition

4.2.432.1 feature_of_size

The feature_of_size specifies whether or not the Inter_stratum_feature interfaces with a feature of another product in an assembly context. A value of TRUE specifies that it interfaces. A value of FALSE specifies that it does not interface.

NOTE: Certain tolerance information depends on knowledge of a particular feature interfacing with features of a mating product. The maximum material conditions of the Inter_stratum_feature and of the feature of the mating product are taken into consideration for calculating the fit at assembly.

4.2.432.2 usage_definition

The usage_definition specifies the role of the Inter_stratum_feature_template for the Inter_stratum_feature.
