4.2.600 Padstack_occurrence_sub_assembly_relationship

A Padstack_occurrence_sub_assembly_relationship is a type of Managed_design_object. A Padstack_occurrence_sub_assembly_relationship is an association that specifies a subassembly constraint among two or more Laminate_component that is aggregated into an Padstack_occurrence. A Land may participate in a Padstack_occurrence. In that case, there is no need to populate a separate Assembly_composition_relationship between the Land and the Pcb, since the Assembly_composition_relationship between the Padstack_occurrence and the Pcb establishes the required association. Also, in that case, there is no need to separately populate the usage_definition from Land to Land_physical_template because the Land template may be deduced from the Padstack_definition structure. A Via may appear in a Padstack_occurrence.

The data associated with this application object are the following:

  • related_component
  • relating_component

4.2.600.1 sub_assembly_reference_designation

The sub_assembly_reference_designation specifies the string which is the identifier for this Padstack_occurrence_sub_assembly_relationship in the context of the relating_component.

4.2.600.2 related_component

The related_component specifies the dependent Laminate_component of the Padstack_occurrence_sub_assembly_relationship.

4.2.600.3 relating_component

The relating_component specifies the independent Padstack_occurrence which is the aggregation of two or more Assembly_component specified by related_component.

Formal constraints:

UR1: The combination of relating_component, resident_stratum of the related_component, and sub_assembly_reference_designation shall be unique in a population of Padstack_occurrence_sub_assembly_relationship.

WR1: An Assembly_component specified as a relating_component shall not appear in the chain of components in the set of Assembly_component specified by related_component.

ENTITY padstack_occurrence_sub_assembly_relationship
  SUBTYPE OF (managed_design_object);
    related_component : laminate_component;
    relating_component : padstack_occurrence;
    sub_assembly_reference_designation : string;
UNIQUE
  UR1: sub_assembly_reference_designation, 
       relating_component, 
       related_component;
END_ENTITY; -- padstack_occurrence_sub_assembly_relationship
