4.2.102 Circular_runout_tolerance

NOTE: The text description for this Application Object has not been fully updated to agree with the EXPRESS.
A Circular_runout_tolerance is a type of Geometric_tolerance_with_referenced_datum_system that specifies or partially specifies the design requirement on the run-out of circular elements of a Physical_feature_or_part_template in relation to a Datum_axis. Where run-out is the quality or state of having perfect form and axial alignment in relation to an axis. The Physical_feature_or_part_template may be coaxial or normal to the Datum_axis. If the Circular_runout_tolerance is applied at a specified angle the defined Tolerance_zone_definition is a Runout_tolerance_zone_definition_with_specified_angle.

NOTES:

  1. A circular run-out tolerance is typically verified by placing a full movement indicator against the surface of the part at the desired orientation and location and then rotating the part one complete revolution about the datum axis. The value of the full indicator movement is then read. This process is repeated at each of the desired test locations. If any one of the full indicator movement readings is greater than the specified tolerance value the surface has exceeded one of the tolerance zones and the part fails the inspection. In theory, there are an infinite number of test locations. However, in practice, a finite number of test locations are measured.
  2. Circular run-out tolerance is defined in terms of tolerance zone definitions in 14.13 of ISO 1101.

Formal constraints:

WR1: The Shape_element specified as the toleranced_shape_element shall be a Physical_feature_or_part_template.

NOTE: The attribute toleranced_shape_element is declared in the Geometric_tolerance entity of which this entity is a subtype.

WR2: The Circular_runout_tolerance shall not be a Geometric_tolerance_with_applied_material_condition_property.

WR3: There shall be at most two Datum in the Datum_system the definition of which is specified as the datum_system_definition.

NOTE: The attribute datum_system_definition is declared in the Geometric_tolerance_with_referenced_datum_system entity of which this entity is a subtype.

WR4: There shall be a Datum_axis in the Datum_system the definition of which is specified as the datum_system_definition.

WR5: The Datum_system_defining_conditions that is specified as the datum_system_definition shall be a Datum_system_defining_conditions_without_material_conditions.

WR6: The Circular_runout_tolerance shall specify a Tolerance_zone_definition as its tolerance_zone_or_boundary_definition; and that Tolerance_zone_definition shall specify a Tolerance_zone_boundary_definition_with_specified_size as its boundary_definition; and that Tolerance_zone_boundary_definition_with_specified_size shall specify an Opposing_boundary_set_size_characteristic as its specified_boundary_size.

Informal constraints:

IP1: The form of the toleranced Physical_feature_or_part_template shall be consistent with the form of a physical feature for which the application of a circular run-out tolerance is sensible.

ENTITY circular_runout_tolerance
  SUBTYPE OF (geometric_tolerance_with_referenced_datum_system);
