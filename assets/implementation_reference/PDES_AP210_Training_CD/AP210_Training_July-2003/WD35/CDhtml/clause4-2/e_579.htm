4.2.590 Packaged_connector_terminal_relationship

NOTE: The text description for this Application Object has not been fully updated to agree with the EXPRESS.
A Packaged_connector_terminal_relationship is a type of Managed_design_object. A Packaged_connector_terminal_relationship is the association between one interface terminal and a corresponding join terminal of the Packaged_connector that specifies that the two terminals are shorted internally to the Packaged_connector as part of its required functionality. This relationship is an explicit statement of the inherent functionality of a connector. Therefore no separate Functional_unit_network_definition or Analytical_model is required to specify the nominal functionality of a connector. A Functional_unit_usage_view may be supplied to support mapping the functional design to the connector join terminals.

NOTES:

  1. One use for this relationship is to specify the short between the terminal that is installed to the printed circuit assembly and the terminal that provides the access to the outside environment when an occurrence of Packaged_connector is installed in the assembly.
  2. Installation into the next higher assembly is not necessary to achieve this relationship.
  3. There is a member of this relationship populated for every pair of terminals in the Packaged_connector.

The data associated with this application object are the following:

  • connector
  • interface_terminal
  • join_terminal

4.2.590.1 connector

The connector specifies the Packaged_connector that has the Package_terminal that are defined to be shorted by the Packaged_connector_terminal_relationship.

4.2.590.2 interface_terminal

The interface_terminal specifies the role of the Packaged_part_interface_terminal for the Packaged_connector_terminal_relationship.

4.2.590.3 join_terminal

The join_terminal specifies the role of the Packaged_part_join_terminal for the Packaged_connector_terminal_relationship.

Formal constraints:

UR1: The combination of the connector and the join_terminal shall be unique within a population of Packaged_connector_terminal_relationship.

UR2: The combination of the connector and the interface_terminal shall be unique within a population of Packaged_connector_terminal_relationship.

WR1: The Packaged_part associated with the interface_terminal shall be the Packaged_connector specified by connector.

WR2: The Packaged_part associated with the join_terminal shall be the Packaged_connector specified by connector.

ENTITY packaged_connector_terminal_relationship
  SUBTYPE OF (managed_design_object);
    connector          : packaged_connector;
    join_terminal      : packaged_part_join_terminal;
    interface_terminal : packaged_part_interface_terminal;
  UNIQUE
    ur1 : join_terminal, connector;
    ur2 : interface_terminal, connector;
END_ENTITY; -- packaged_connector_terminal_relationship
