4.2.943 Usage_view_connection_zone_terminal_shape_relationship

A Usage_view_connection_zone_terminal_shape_relationship is a type of Managed_design_object. This relationship positions and orients the geometric model of a Connection_zone into the model of feature using a transformation. The interpretation of the associated_connection_zone_shape_definition shall be that it shall be a shape_representation for the Connection_zone in the role of associated_usage.

The data associated with this application object are the following:

  • associating_terminal_shape
  • associated_usage
  • associated_connection_zone_shape_definition
  • associated_usage_placement

4.2.943.1 associating_terminal_shape

The associating_terminal_shape specifies the Feature_shape_definition that is the model shape for the Usage_view_connection_zone_terminal_shape_relationship.

4.2.943.2 associated_usage

The associated_usage specifies the Connection_zone that is the target item for the Usage_view_connection_zone_terminal_shape_relationship.

4.2.943.3 associated_connection_zone_shape_definition

The associated_connection_zone_shape_definition specifies the Connection_zone_shape that is the source shape for the Usage_view_connection_zone_terminal_shape_relationship.

4.2.943.4 associated_usage_placement

The associated_usage_placement specifies the Axis_placement that is the target in the coordinate system of the model for the Usage_view_connection_zone_terminal_shape_relationship, including any transformation required.

  SUBTYPE OF (managed_design_object);

    associated_usage                            : connection_zone;

 WR1: associating_terminal_shape :<>:
