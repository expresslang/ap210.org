4.2.739 Probe_access_area

NOTE: The text description for this Application Object has not been fully updated to agree with the EXPRESS.
A Probe_access_area is a type of Laminate_component. A Probe_access_area may be an Internal_probe_access_area. A Probe_access_area is a feature incorporated into the design of an Interconnect_module for the purpose of measuring electrical characteristics of the design. It is realized by an exposed surface of conducting material that is in physical contact with the Conductor or Conductive_filled_area upon which the measurement is to be made.

EXAMPLE: Figure paa illustrates a detail of a layout drawing that shows a probe area in the layout. Sufficient detail may be included in the design to evaluate accurately probe placement and volume restrictions.

The data associated with this application object are the following:

  • probe_contact_zone
  • probed_layout_item

4.2.739.1 probe_contact_zone

The probe_contact_zone specifies the role of the Connection_zone for the Probe_access_area.

4.2.739.2 probed_layout_item

The probed_layout_item specifies the role of the Stratum_feature for the Probe_access_area.
