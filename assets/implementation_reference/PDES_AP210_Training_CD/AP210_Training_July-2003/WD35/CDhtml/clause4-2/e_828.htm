4.2.828 Single_datum

NOTE: The text description for this Application Object has not been fully updated to agree with the EXPRESS.
A Single_datum is a Datum that is established by a single Datum_feature.

NOTE: The establishment of a datum by a single datum feature is described in 6.2.1 of ISO 5459.

Formal constraints:

WR1: Each Single_datum shall participate in establishing at least one Datum_system.

WR2: Each Single_datum shall be specified as the relating_shape_element by exactly one Datum_feature_usage_in_single_datum_relationship.

NOTE: 
*)
ENTITY single_datum
  SUPERTYPE OF (edge_segment_vertex)
  SUBTYPE OF (datum);
END_ENTITY; -- single_datum
