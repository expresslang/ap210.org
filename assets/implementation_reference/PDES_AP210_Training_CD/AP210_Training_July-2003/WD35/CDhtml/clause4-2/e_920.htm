4.2.920 Tolerance_zone_definition

NOTE: The text description for this Application Object has not been fully updated to agree with the EXPRESS.
A Tolerance_zone_definition is a type of Managed_design_object. A Tolerance_zone_definition may be a one of Conical_tolerance_zone_definition, Projected_tolerance_zone_definition, Runout_tolerance_zone_definition_with_specified_angle, or Tolerance_zone_definition_with_per_unit_size_specification. Additionally, a Tolerance_zone_definition may be a Tolerance_zone_definition_with_specified_orientation. A Tolerance_zone_definition is a definition that characterizes a Tolerance_zone.

The data associated with this application object are the following:

  • boundary_definition
  • defined_tolerance_zone
  • form

4.2.920.1 boundary_definition

The boundary_definition specify the Tolerance_zone_boundary_definition that defines the limits of the Tolerance_zone for the Tolerance_zone_definition.

4.2.920.2 defined_tolerance_zone

The defined_tolerance_zone specifies the Tolerance_zone that is characterized by the Tolerance_zone_definition.

4.2.920.3 form

The form specifies the Tolerance_zone_form that characterizes the form of the specified Tolerance_zone. The form need not be specified for a particular Tolerance_zone_definition.

Formal constraints:

WR1: If the Tolerance_zone_form is specified, the Tolerance_zone specified as the characterized_tolerance_zone of the Tolerance_zone_form that is specified as the form shall be the same instance of Tolerance_zone as specified as the defined_tolerance_zone.

WR2: The Tolerance_zone_boundary specified as the defined_zone_boundary of the Tolerance_zone_boundary_definition that is specified as the boundary_definition shall be the same instances of Tolerance_zone_boundary as specified as the zone_boundary of the Tolerance_zone specified as the defined_tolerance_zone.

WR3: If the Tolerance_zone_definition is not a Conical_tolerance_zone_definition, the Tolerance_zone_definition shall be specified as the tolerance_zone_or_boundary_definition by exactly one Geometric_tolerance.

ENTITY tolerance_zone_definition
  SUPERTYPE OF (ONEOF (projected_tolerance_zone_definition,
      runout_tolerance_zone_definition_with_specified_angle,
      tolerance_zone_definition_with_per_unit_size_specification,
      conical_tolerance_zone_definition,
      tolerance_zone_definition_with_specified_orientation))
  SUBTYPE OF (managed_design_object);
    defined_tolerance_zone : tolerance_zone;
    boundary_definition    : tolerance_zone_boundary_definition;
    form                   : OPTIONAL tolerance_zone_form;
END_ENTITY; -- tolerance_zone_definition
