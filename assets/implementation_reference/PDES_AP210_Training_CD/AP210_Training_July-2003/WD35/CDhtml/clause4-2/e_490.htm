4.2.490 Land

NOTE: The text description for this Application Object has not been fully updated to agree with the EXPRESS.
A Land is a type of Stratum_feature_template_component. A Land may be a Component_termination_passage_and_contact_size_dependent_land, a Component_termination_passage_dependent_land, a Contact_size_dependent_land, a Via_dependent_land, or a Via_and_contact_size_dependent_land. A Land helps compose a Conductor or a Conductive_filled_area.

NOTE: In the context of this standard, a Land may be external or internal to the interconnect.

EXAMPLE: Figure land illustrates the relationship of a Land to the other elements of a construction known in the domain as a padstack.

The data associated with this application object are the following:

  • access_mechanism
  • alternate_land_definition
  • usage_definition

4.2.490.1 access_mechanism

The access_mechanism is an inverse relationship that specifies that the existence of the Land is dependent on the existence of the Land_join_terminal that specifies the Land as its associated_component. There shall be one or more Land_join_terminal for a Land. This relationship is for internal access only.

4.2.490.2 alternate_land_definition

The alternate_land_definition specifies one role of the Land_physical_template for the Land. The alternate_land_definition need not be specified for a particular Land.

4.2.490.3 usage_definition

The usage_definition specifies the primary role of the Land_physical_template for the Land. The usage_definition need not be specified for a particular Land.

NOTE: The Land_join_terminal allows an application to discover the usage_definition for the Land through the Land_join_terminal.definition.associated_definition path.

Formal constraints:

WR1: A member of land shall be directly connected to a member of conductive_interconnect_element or to a member of conductive_area.

WR2: When an alternate_land_definition is present it overrides the usage_definition for the Land.

ENTITY land
  SUPERTYPE OF (ONEOF (via_dependent_land,
      component_termination_passage_dependent_land,
      contact_size_dependent_land,
      component_termination_passage_and_contact_size_dependent_land,
      via_and_contact_size_dependent_land))
  SUBTYPE OF (stratum_feature_template_component);
    alternate_land_definition                : OPTIONAL land_physical_template;
    SELF\assembly_component.usage_definition : OPTIONAL land_physical_template;
  INVERSE
    access_mechanism           : SET [1:?] OF 
                                 land_join_terminal FOR associated_component;
END_ENTITY; -- land
