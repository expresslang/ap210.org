4.2.736 Printed_part_template_material_link

NOTE: The text description for this Application Object has not been fully updated to agree with the EXPRESS.
A Printed_part_template_material_link is a type of Managed_design_object.

The data associated with this application object are the following:

  • precedent_material
  • subsequent_material

4.2.736.1 precedent_material

The precedent_material specifies a Template_material_cross_section_boundary for the Printed_part_template_material_link.

4.2.736.2 subsequent_material

The subsequent_material specifies a Template_material_cross_section_boundary for the Printed_part_template_material_link.

Formal constraints:

UR1: The combination of precedent_material and subsequent_material shall be unique within a population of Printed_part_template_material_link.

WR1: The precedent_material shall not be the subsequent_material within a population of Printed_part_template_material_link.

WR2: The name attribute inherited from Shape_element for the precedent_material shall be one of "bottom" or "right" within a population of Template_material_cross_section_boundary.

WR3: The name attribute inherited from Shape_element for the subsequent_material shall be one "top" or "left" within a population of Template_material_cross_section_boundary.

ENTITY printed_part_template_material_link
  SUBTYPE OF (managed_design_object);
    precedent_material : template_material_cross_section_boundary;
    subsequent_material : template_material_cross_section_boundary;
UNIQUE
  ur1: precedent_material, subsequent_material;
WHERE
 wr1: precedent_material :<>: subsequent_material;
--tt tc1 wd8 added
 wr2: precedent_material\shape_element.name in ['bottom', 'right'];
 wr3: subsequent_material\shape_element.name in ['top', 'left'];
END_ENTITY; -- printed_part_template_material_link
