4.2.511 Length_data_element

NOTE: The text description for this Application Object has not been fully updated to agree with the EXPRESS.
A Length_data_element is a type of Data_element where the measurement is of distance. A member of Length_data_element may also be a member of Qualified_data_element in order to convey uncertainty.

NOTE: 
*)
ENTITY length_data_element
  SUBTYPE OF (data_element);
WHERE
 WR1: SELF\data_element.measure_value >= 0.0;
END_ENTITY; -- length_data_element
