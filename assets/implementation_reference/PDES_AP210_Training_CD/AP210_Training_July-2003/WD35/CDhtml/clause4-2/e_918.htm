4.2.918 Tolerance_zone_boundary_member_offset

NOTE: The text description for this Application Object has not been fully updated to agree with the EXPRESS.
A Tolerance_zone_boundary_member_offset is a type of Managed_design_object. A Tolerance_zone_boundary_member_offset is a definition that characterizes the size and direction that a Tolerance_zone_boundary_member is offset from the True_profile of the toleranced Physical_feature_or_part_template.

The data associated with this application object are the following:

  • boundary_definition
  • offset_tolerance_zone_boundary_member
  • offset_value
  • type_of_offset

4.2.918.1 boundary_definition

The boundary_definition is an inverse relationship that specifies that the existence of the Tolerance_zone_boundary_member_offset is dependent on the existence of the Profile_boundary_definition_with_offsets that specifies the Tolerance_zone_boundary_member_offset as one of its boundary_offsets.

4.2.918.2 offset_tolerance_zone_boundary_member

The offset_tolerance_zone_boundary_member specifies the Tolerance_zone_boundary_member the offset of which is characterized by the Tolerance_zone_boundary_member_offset.

4.2.918.3 offset_value

The offset_value specifies the Length_data_element that represents distance the Tolerance_zone_boundary_member is offset from the True_profile.

4.2.918.4 type_of_offset

The type_of_offset specifies a label that indicates the direction that the Tolerance_zone_boundary_member is offset from the True_profile. The type_of_offset shall either be "away_from_material" or "towards_material".

Formal constraints:

WR1: The Tolerance_zone_explicit_opposing_boundary_set that is specified as the explicit_opposing_boundary_set by the Tolerance_zone_boundary_member that is specified as the offset_tolerance_zone_boundary_member shall be the same instance of Tolerance_zone_explicit_opposing_boundary_set that is specified as the defined_zone_boundary by the Profile_boundary_definition_with_offsets specified as the boundary_definition.
