4.2.843 Step_edge_feature_shape

NOTE: The text description for this Application Object has not been fully updated to agree with the EXPRESS.
A Step_edge_feature_shape is a type of Edge_shape that is the two-dimensional definition of the construction of a step edge. See Bevel_edge_feature_shape for additional supporting geometric information that shall be provided.

EXAMPLE: Figure sefs illustrates a detail of a fabrication drawing that shows some of the information exchanged for construction of a step edge.

The data associated with this application object are the following:

  • maximum_step_depth
  • maximum_step_width
  • minimum_step_depth
  • minimum_step_width

4.2.843.1 maximum_step_depth

The maximum_step_depth specifies the Length_data_element of the maximum depth of the step relative to the datum surface.

4.2.843.2 maximum_step_width

The maximum_step_width specifies the Length_data_element of the maximum width of the step relative to the edge surface that is stepped.

4.2.843.3 minimum_step_depth

The minimum_step_depth specifies the Length_data_element of the minimum depth of the step relative to the datum surface.

4.2.843.4 minimum_step_width

The minimum_step_width specifies the Length_data_element of the minimum width of the step relative to the edge surface that is stepped.
