4.2.696 Position_tolerance_with_referenced_datum_system

NOTE: The text description for this Application Object has not been fully updated to agree with the EXPRESS.
A Position_tolerance_with_referenced_datum_system is a type of Geometric_tolerance_with_referenced_datum_system and a type of Position_tolerance that participates in specifying a design requirement on the location of the toleranced Shape_element in relation to a Datum_system. A Position_tolerance_with_referenced_datum_system may be either a Composite_feature_locating_position_tolerance or a Boundary_based_position_tolerance.
