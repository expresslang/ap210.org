4.2.815 Shape_element_locating_relationship

NOTE: The text description for this Application Object has not been fully updated to agree with the EXPRESS.
A Shape_element_locating_relationship is a type of Managed_design_object. A Shape_element_locating_relationship is the association between two Shape_element in which the associated Shape_elements are located in relation to each other.

The data associated with this application object are the following:

  • located_shape_element
  • locating_shape_element

4.2.815.1 located_shape_element

The located_shape_element specifies the Shape_element that is located in relation to the Shape_element specified by the locating_shape_element for the Shape_element_locating_relationship.

4.2.815.2 locating_shape_element

The locating_shape_element specifies the Shape_element in relation to which the Shape_element specified by the located_shape_element is located for the Shape_element_locating_relationship.

Formal constraints:

UR1: The combination of located and locating Shape_elements shall be unique within a population of Shape_element_locating_relationship. That is, the same Shape_elements shall not be located relative to each other more than once.

WR1: The Shape_element specified by the located_shape_element shall not be the same Shape_element specified by the locating_shape_element.

WR2: The Shape_element_locating_relationship shall be specified as either the located_elements by a Dimensional_location or as the boundary_separation by a Dimensional_size_based_on_opposing_boundaries.
