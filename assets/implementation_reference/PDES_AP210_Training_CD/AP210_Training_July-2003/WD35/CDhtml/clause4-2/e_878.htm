4.2.877 Sub_assembly_relationship

A Sub_assembly_relationship is a type of Managed_design_object. A Sub_assembly_relationship is an association that specifies a subassembly constraint among two or more Assembly_component that is aggregated into an Assembly_group_component.

NOTES:

  1. Industrial practice is to associate the parts included in a Assembly_group_component with members of Product_association.
  2. A group of Lands aggregated into a Footprint_occurrence do not also need to be aggregated into Padstack_occurrence.

EXAMPLES:

  1. Figure sar illustrates a detail of an assembly drawing that shows components grouped into a sub-assembly, and the corresponding list of items and their associated reference_designations.
  2. A Land may participate in a sub assembly where that sub assembly is a Footprint_occurrence. In that case the reference_designation provides the ability to map unambiguously to the Footprint_padstack_composition_relationship Application Object that is the reference for the location and orientation of the Land in that footprint. In that case, there is no need to populate a separate Assembly_composition_relationship between the Land and the Pcb, since the Assembly_composition_relationship between the Footprint_occurrence and the Pcb establishes the required association.

The data associated with this application object are the following:

  • related_component
  • relating_component

4.2.877.1 sub_assembly_reference_designation

The sub_assembly_reference_designation specifies the string which is the identifier for this Sub_assembly_relationship in the context of the relating_component.

4.2.877.2 related_component

The related_component specify the dependent Assembly_component of the Sub_assembly_relationship. There shall be two or more Assembly_component for a Sub_assembly_relationship.

4.2.877.3 relating_component

The relating_component specifies the independent Assembly_group_component which is the aggregation of two or more Assembly_component specified by related_component.

Formal constraints:

UR1: The combination of relating_component and sub_assembly_reference_designation shall be unique in a population of sub_assembly_relationship.

WR1: An Assembly_component specified as a relating_component shall not appear in the chain of components in the set of Assembly_component specified by related_component.

ENTITY sub_assembly_relationship
  SUBTYPE OF (managed_design_object);
    related_component : assembly_component;
    relating_component : assembly_group_component;
    sub_assembly_reference_designation : string;
UNIQUE
  UR1: sub_assembly_reference_designation, relating_component;    
END_ENTITY; -- sub_assembly_relationship
