4.2.905 Thermal_isolation_requirement_assignment

NOTE: The text description for this Application Object has not been fully updated to agree with the EXPRESS.
A Thermal_isolation_requirement_assignment is a type of Managed_design_object. A Thermal_isolation_requirement_assignment is the relationship among requirements for thermal isolation and the resulting structure of the Interconnect_module or the template.

The data associated with this application object are the following:

  • angular_orientation_requirement
  • effective_current_capacity_requirement
  • resulting_structure
  • thermal_isolation_spacing_requirement

4.2.905.1 angular_orientation_requirement

The angular_orientation_requirement specifies one role of the Ee_requirement_occurrence for the Thermal_isolation_requirement_assignment.

4.2.905.2 effective_current_capacity_requirement

The effective_current_capacity_requirement specifies one role of the Ee_requirement_occurrence for the Thermal_isolation_requirement_assignment.

4.2.905.3 resulting_structure

The resulting_structure specifies the role of the Thermal_isolation_removal_structure or of the Thermal_isolation_removal_template for the Thermal_isolation_requirement_assignment.

4.2.905.4 thermal_isolation_spacing_requirement

The thermal_isolation_spacing_requirement specifies one role of the Ee_requirement_occurrence for the Thermal_isolation_requirement_assignment.
