4.2.337 Ee_rule_version

An Ee_rule_version is a type of Ee_product_version. An Ee_rule_version is the management of the assignment of Ee_rule_definition to Ee_rule. An Ee_rule may evolve with usage for any number of reasons.

The data associated with this application object are the following:

  • management_action

4.2.337.1 management_action

The management_action is an inverse relationship that specifies that the existence of the Ee_rule_version is dependent on the existence of the Rule_action that specifies the Ee_rule_version as its subject_rule. There shall be one or more Rule_action for an Ee_rule_version.

ENTITY ee_rule_version
  SUBTYPE OF (ee_product_version);
  INVERSE
    management_action : SET [1:?] OF rule_action FOR subject_rule;
END_ENTITY; -- ee_rule_version
