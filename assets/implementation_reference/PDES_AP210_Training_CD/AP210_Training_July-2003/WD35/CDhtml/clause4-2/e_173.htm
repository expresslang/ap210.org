4.2.173 Conductive_interconnect_element_terminal_link

A Conductive_interconnect_element_terminal_link is a type of Managed_design_object. A Conductive_interconnect_element_terminal_link is a directed link between two Conductive_interconnect_element_terminal in order to support the sequence of Conductive_interconnect_element_terminal required in Conductive_interconnect_element_with_pre_defined_transitions.

The data associated with this application object are the following:

  • associated_element
  • precedent_point
  • subsequent_point

4.2.173.1 associated_element

An associated_element specifies the role of the Conductive_interconnect_element_with_pre_defined_transitions for a Conductive_interconnect_element_terminal_link.

4.2.173.2 precedent_point

A precedent_point specifies one role of the Conductive_interconnect_element_terminal for a Conductive_interconnect_element_terminal_link.

4.2.173.3 subsequent_point

A subsequent_point specifies one role of the Conductive_interconnect_element_terminal for a Conductive_interconnect_element_terminal_link.

Formal constraints:

WR1: The precedent_point shall not be the subsequent_point.

WR2: The precedent_point shall be on the associated_element.

WR3: The subsequent_point shall be on the associated_element.

NOTE: 
*)
ENTITY conductive_interconnect_element_terminal_link
  SUBTYPE OF (managed_design_object);
    associated_element : conductive_interconnect_element_with_pre_defined_transitions;
    precedent_point    : conductive_interconnect_element_terminal;
    subsequent_point   : conductive_interconnect_element_terminal;
WHERE
  WR1 : precedent_point <> subsequent_point;
  WR2 : precedent_point.associated_component = associated_element;
  WR3 : subsequent_point.associated_component = associated_element;
END_ENTITY; -- conductive_interconnect_element_terminal_link
