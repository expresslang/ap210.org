4.2.135 Component_placement_restriction_assignment

NOTE: The text description for this Application Object has not been fully updated to agree with the EXPRESS.
A Component_placement_restriction_assignment is a type of Managed_design_object. A Component_placement_restriction_assignment is the relationship between the area of an entire Interconnect_module_component or a portion thereof and restrictions to placement of other components in that area due to an item in the design. The context of a Component_placement_restriction_assignment is the design of the assembly within which both the Interconnect_module_component and associated components are identified.

The data associated with this application object are the following:

  • area
  • components_permitted
  • maximum_mounting_clearance
  • maximum_negative_component_height
  • maximum_positive_component_height
  • minimum_mounting_clearance
  • requirement
  • volume

4.2.135.1 area

The area specifies the role of the Mounting_restriction_area for the Component_placement_restriction_assignment. The area is an area on an Interconnect_module_component.

4.2.135.2 components_permitted

The components_permitted specifies whether or not there is a restriction upon placement of components in an area for the Component_placement_restriction_assignment. The value of TRUE states that components are permitted; the value of FALSE states that components are not permitted to be placed in the defined area.

4.2.135.3 maximum_mounting_clearance

The maximum_mounting_clearance specifies the largest Datum_based_length_measure for which the Component_placement_restriction_assignment is made. The maximum_mounting_clearance need not be specified for a particular Component_placement_restriction_assignment.

EXAMPLE: An example of the maximum_mounting_clearance attribute is for a printed circuit assembly with through-hole components, where some packages are required to be mounted other than flush with the surface of the printed circuit board, for purposes of cleaning or of thermal dissipation. The maximum_mounting_clearance is less frequently seen than the minimum_mounting_clearance, but it is included for completeness. When a maximum dimension is required, it is often described by the maximum_positive_component_height attribute, which takes into consideration the maximum material condition of the package as well as the clearance from the printed circuit board.

4.2.135.4 maximum_negative_component_height

The maximum_negative_component_height specifies the largest Datum_based_length_measure in the negative direction from that Datum, for which the Component_placement_restriction_assignment is made. The maximum_negative_component_height need not be specified for a particular Component_placement_restriction_assignment.

EXAMPLE: An example of the maximum_negative_component_height attribute is a printed circuit assembly with through-hole components, where the maximum lead protrusion dimension is given. That dimension is the maximum_negative_component_height. Another example of the maximum_negative_component_height attribute is a printed circuit assembly with surface mounted components on both sides. The maximum_negative_component_height attribute is the distance between the established datum and the maximum material condition of the vertical dimension of the highest surface mount component on the secondary side of the printed circuit assembly.

4.2.135.5 maximum_positive_component_height

The maximum_positive_component_height specifies the largest Datum_based_length_measure in the positive direction from the Datum, for which the Component_placement_restriction_assignment is made. The maximum_positive_component_height need not be specified for a particular Component_placement_restriction_assignment.

EXAMPLE: An example of the maximum_positive_component_height attribute is a printed circuit assembly where the maximum component height in the positive direction relative to a datum is given. This dimension pertains to the fit of the printed circuit assembly in its next assembly. That dimension is the maximum_positive_component_height.

4.2.135.6 minimum_mounting_clearance

The minimum_mounting_clearance specifies the smallest Datum_based_length_measure for which the Component_placement_restriction_assignment is made. The minimum_mounting_clearance need not be specified for a particular Component_placement_restriction_assignment.

EXAMPLE: An example of the minimum_mounting_clearance attribute is for a printed circuit assembly with through-hole components, where some packages are required to be mounted other than flush with the surface of the printed circuit board for purposes of cleaning or of thermal dissipation. The minimum_mounting_clearance is more frequently seen than the maximum_mounting_clearance. The usual default clearance dimension is zero, meaning that the component package is intended to be mounted flush with the printed circuit board. Packages that often require a minimum_mounting_clearance include radial capacitors and certain other devices where the leads protrude from the bottom of the package, and where the package is not usually intended to be bonded directly to the printed circuit board.

4.2.135.7 requirement

The requirement specifies the role of the Item_restricted_requirement_occurrence for the Component_placement_restriction_assignment.

4.2.135.8 volume

The volume specifies the role of the Mounting_restriction_volume for the Component_placement_restriction_assignment. The volume is in the context of the assembly.

Formal constraints:

WR1: The direction of negative measurement is antiparallel to the normal of the surface of the Interconnect_module_component specified by area.mounting_surface.

WR2: The direction of positive measurement is parallel to the normal of the surface of the Interconnect_module_component specified by area.mounting_surface.

WR3: The location of the Datum for the maximum_mounting_clearance_relationship shall be on the same side of the Interconnect_module_component as that specified by area.mounting_surface.

WR4: The location of the Datum for the minimum_mounting_clearance_relationship shall be on the same side of the Interconnect_module_component as that specified by area.mounting_surface.

WR5: At least one of area or volume shall be provided.

ENTITY component_placement_restriction_assignment
  SUBTYPE OF (managed_design_object);
    maximum_negative_component_height : OPTIONAL datum_based_length_measure;
    maximum_positive_component_height : OPTIONAL datum_based_length_measure;
    area                              : OPTIONAL mounting_restriction_area;
    volume                            : OPTIONAL mounting_restriction_volume;
    requirement                       : item_restricted_requirement_occurrence;
    components_permitted              : BOOLEAN;
    maximum_mounting_clearance        : OPTIONAL datum_based_length_measure;
    minimum_mounting_clearance        : OPTIONAL datum_based_length_measure;
WHERE
  WR1: EXISTS(area) OR EXISTS(volume);
END_ENTITY; -- component_placement_restriction_assignment
