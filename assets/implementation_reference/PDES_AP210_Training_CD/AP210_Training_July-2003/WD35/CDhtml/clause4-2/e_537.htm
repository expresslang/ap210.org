4.2.537 Material_specification

NOTE: The text description for this Application Object has not been fully updated to agree with the EXPRESS.
A Material_specification is a type of Ee_specification that specifies properties applicable to raw material, mixtures, or semi-fabricated material that are used in the fabrication of a product. A Material_specification may be a Surface_finish_specification.

EXAMPLE: An example of a semi-fabricated material is the partially cured adhesive sheets which often are composed of a glass fiber mat and "B-stage" epoxy. Semi-fabricated material may be purchased or sold by an enterprise, in the same way that raw materials or mixtures may.

ENTITY material_specification
  SUPERTYPE OF (surface_finish_specification)
  SUBTYPE OF (ee_specification);
