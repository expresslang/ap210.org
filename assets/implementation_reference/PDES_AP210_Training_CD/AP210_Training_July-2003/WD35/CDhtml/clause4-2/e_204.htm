4.2.204 Curve_dimension

NOTE: The text description for this Application Object has not been fully updated to agree with the EXPRESS.
A Curve_dimension is a type of Single_boundary_dimension that is measured along the entire path of the characterized Shape_element the form of which is that of a curve.

NOTES:

  1. The attribute dimension_type is declared in the Dimensional_characteristic entity of which this entity is a subtype.
  2. The attribute size_of is declared in the Dimensional_size entity of which this entity is a subtype.

Formal constraints:

WR1: The dimension_type of the Curve_dimension shall not be "angular".

Informal constraints:

IP1: The form of the Shape_element that is specified as the size_of shall be consistent with the form of a curve.

NOTE: 
*)
ENTITY curve_dimension
  SUBTYPE OF (single_boundary_dimension);
END_ENTITY; -- curve_dimension
