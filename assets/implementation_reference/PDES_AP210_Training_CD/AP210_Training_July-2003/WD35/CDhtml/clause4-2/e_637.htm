4.2.633 Part_tooling_feature

NOTE: The text description for this Application Object has not been fully updated to agree with the EXPRESS.
A Part_tooling_feature is a type of Part_feature. A Part_tooling_feature may be a Fiducial_part_feature or may be a Test_point_part_feature. A Part_tooling_feature identifies a feature for tooling purposes. A feature may be used for other purposes or may intrinsically be in a particular feature category, and in either of those cases the feature would be a Part_tooling_feature and also the other type of feature.

NOTE: Tooling purposes as used herein includes test instrumentation and measurement purposes, not just inspection, fabrication and assembly tooling.

EXAMPLE: Examples of a Part_tooling_feature include, but are not limited, to slots, passages, notches defined by Geometric Dimensioning and Tolerancing methodology, as well as fiducials.
