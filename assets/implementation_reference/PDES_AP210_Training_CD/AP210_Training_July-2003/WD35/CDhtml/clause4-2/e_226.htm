4.2.226 Datum_feature

NOTE: The text description for this Application Object has not been fully updated to agree with the EXPRESS.
A Datum_feature is a type of Physical_feature_or_part_template that is used to establish one or more Datum. A Datum_feature may be a Datum_target_set. A Datum_feature may be used to establish one Single_datum, and one or more Common_datum.

NOTE: The definition of datum feature is given in 3.3 of ISO 5459.

The data associated with this application object are the following:

  • datum_feature_usages
  • identification

4.2.226.1 datum_feature_usages

The datum_feature_usages is an inverse relationship that specifies that the existence of the Datum_feature is dependent on the existence of the Datum_feature_usage_in_datum_relationship that specifies the Datum_feature as its related_shape_element. There shall be one or more Datum_feature_usage_in_datum_relationship for a Datum_feature.

4.2.226.2 identification

The identification specifies the label by which the Datum_feature is referred.

NOTE: Datum letter identifiers are examples of identifiers of Datum features. The Datum letter identifier is described in 6.1.2 of ISO 5459.

Formal constraints:

WR1: A Datum_feature shall be used to establish at most one Single_datum.

  INVERSE
