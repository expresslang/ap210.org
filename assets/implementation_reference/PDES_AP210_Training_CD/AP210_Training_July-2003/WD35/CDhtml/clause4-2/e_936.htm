4.2.936 Uncertainty_qualifier

NOTE: The text description for this Application Object has not been fully updated to agree with the EXPRESS.
An Uncertainty_qualifier is a type of Managed_design_object. The uncertainty may be Standard_uncertainty or Qualitative_uncertainty. The uncertainty is defined in clause 2 of "The Guide to the Expression of Uncertainty in Measurement".

NOTE: 
*)
ENTITY uncertainty_qualifier
  SUPERTYPE OF (ONEOF(standard_uncertainty, qualitative_uncertainty))
  SUBTYPE OF (managed_design_object);
    measure_name : text;
    description : text;
END_ENTITY;
