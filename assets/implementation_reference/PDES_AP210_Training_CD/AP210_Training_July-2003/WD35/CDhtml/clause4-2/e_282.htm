4.2.282 Design_view_terminal_component_shape_relationship

NOTE: The text description for this Application Object has not been fully updated to agree with the EXPRESS.
A Design_view_terminal_component_shape_relationship is a type of Managed_design_object. A Design_view_terminal_component_shape_relationship is the association between a Conductive_interconnect_element_terminal, or an Interconnect_component_join_terminal, or an Interconnect_component_interface_terminal, or a Movable_packaged_component_join_terminal as a source and either a Conductive_interconnect_element or a Physical_component or a Packaged_component as the target, respectively. This relationship positions and orients the geometric model of the source into the model of the target using a transformation. A Conductive_interconnect_element_terminal shall not be associated with a Physical_component or with a Packaged_component. An Interconnect_component_join_terminal shall not be associated with a Conductive_interconnect_element or a Packaged_component. An Interconnect_component_interface_terminal shall not be associated with a Conductive_interconnect_element or a Packaged_component. A Movable_packaged_component_join_terminal shall only be associated with a Packaged_component.

The data associated with this application object are the following:

  • associating_component_shape
  • associated_usage
  • associated_terminal_shape_definition
  • associated_usage_placement

4.2.282.1 associating_component_shape

The associating_component_shape specifies the Feature_shape_definition, that is the component shape for the Design_view_terminal_component_shape_relationship.

4.2.282.2 associated_usage

The associated_usage specifies the Interconnect_component_join_terminal, or the Movable_packaged_component_join_terminal, or the Conductive_interconnect_element_terminal, or the Interconnect_component_interface_terminal that is the source item for the Design_view_terminal_component_shape_relationship.

4.2.282.3 associated_terminal_shape_definition

The associated_terminal_shape_definition specifies the Feature_shape_definition that is the source shape for the Design_view_terminal_component_shape_relationship.

4.2.282.4 associated_usage_placement

The associated_usage_placement specifies the Axis_placement that is the target for the Design_view_terminal_component_shape_relationship.
