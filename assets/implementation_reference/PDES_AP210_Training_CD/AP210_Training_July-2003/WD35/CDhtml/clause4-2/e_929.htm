4.2.929 Total_runout_tolerance

NOTE: The text description for this Application Object has not been fully updated to agree with the EXPRESS.
A Total_runout_tolerance is a type of Geometric_tolerance_with_referenced_datum_system that specifies or partially specifies the design requirement on the run-out of a Physical_feature_or_part_template in relation to a Datum_axis of a Datum_system. Where run-out is the quality or state of having perfect form and axial alignment in relation to an axis. A Total_runout_tolerance may either be applied normal to the surface or at a specified angle with respect to the Datum_axis. If the Total_runout_tolerance is applied at a specified angle the Tolerance_zone_definition is a Runout_tolerance_zone_definition_with_specified_angle. The Physical_feature_or_part_template may be either coaxial or normal to the Datum_axis.

NOTE: Total run-out tolerance is defined in terms of tolerance zone definitions in 14.14 of ISO 1101.

Formal constraints:

WR1: The Shape_element specified as the toleranced_shape_element shall be a Physical_feature_or_part_template.

NOTE: The attribute toleranced_shape_element is declared in the Geometric_tolerance entity of which this entity is a subtype.

WR2: The Total_runout_tolerance shall not be a Geometric_tolerance_with_applied_material_condition_property.

WR3: There shall be at most two Datum in the Datum_system the definition of which is specified by the datum_system_definition.

NOTE: The attribute datum_system_definition is declared in the Geometric_tolerance_with_referenced_datum_system entity of which this entity is a subtype.

WR4: There shall be a Datum_axis in the Datum_system the definition of which is specified by the datum_system_definition.

WR5: The Datum_system_defining_conditions that is specified as the datum_system_definition shall be a Datum_system_defining_conditions_without_material_conditions.

WR6: The Total_runout_tolerance shall specify a Tolerance_zone_definition as its tolerance_zone_or_boundary_definition; and that Tolerance_zone_definition shall specify a Tolerance_zone_boundary_definition_with_specified_size as its boundary_definition; and that Tolerance_zone_boundary_definition_with_specified_size shall specify an Opposing_boundary_set_size_characteristic as its specified_boundary_size.

Informal constraints:

IP1: The form of the toleranced Physical_feature_or_part_template shall be consistent with the form of a physical feature for which the application of a total run-out tolerance is sensible.

ENTITY total_runout_tolerance
