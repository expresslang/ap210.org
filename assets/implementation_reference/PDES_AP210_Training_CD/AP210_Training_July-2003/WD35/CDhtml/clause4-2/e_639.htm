4.2.639 Pca

NOTE: The text description for this Application Object has not been fully updated to agree with the EXPRESS.
A Pca is a type of Assembly_module. A Pca describes a printed circuit assembly. A printed circuit assembly is nearly a synonym for printed wiring assembly, except that a printed circuit assembly may include printed components in its printed circuit board and a printed wiring assembly is constructed upon a printed wiring board that is totally interconnect without printed components. Another synonym for printed circuit assembly is circuit card assembly.

EXAMPLE: See figure pca for an illustration of a Pca.

The data associated with this application object are the following:

  • usage_view

4.2.639.1 usage_view

The usage_view specifies the Pca_usage_view, as seen from the context of its next assembly, for the Pca.
