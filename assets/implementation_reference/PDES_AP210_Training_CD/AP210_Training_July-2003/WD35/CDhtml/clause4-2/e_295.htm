4.2.302 Dimensional_size_based_on_opposing_boundaries

NOTE: The text description for this Application Object has not been fully updated to agree with the EXPRESS.
A Dimensional_size_based_on_opposing_boundaries is a type of Dimensional_size that characterizes the size of a Composite_unit_shape_element as well as characterizing the relationship between two opposing Shape_element from which the Composite_shape_element is composed.

The data associated with this application object are the following:

  • boundary_separation
  • size_of

4.2.302.1 boundary_separation

The boundary_separation specifies the Shape_element_locating_relationship that specifies the two Shape_elements that are on the boundary of the Composite_unit_shape_element for which the separation is characterized by the Dimensional_size_based_on_opposing_boundaries and is the basis for the size of the Composite_unit_shape_element.

4.2.302.2 size_of

The size_of specifies the Composite_unit_shape_element for which the size is characterized by the Dimensional_size_based_on_opposing_boundaries.

NOTE: The attribute size_of is declared in the Dimensional_size entity of which this entity is a subtype.

EXAMPLE: The size of a Shape_element that consists of two opposing surfaces of a notch would be characterized by a Dimensional_size_based_on_opposing_boundaries.

Formal constraints:

WR1: The Shape_element that is specified as the located_shape_element by the Shape_element_locating_relationship specified as the boundary_separation shall be specified as the related_shape_element by a Shape_element_composing_relationship that specifies the Composite_unit_shape_element that is specified as the size_of as the relating_shape_element.

WR2: The Shape_element that is specified as the locating_shape_element by the Shape_element_locating_relationship specified as the boundary_separation shall be specified as the related_shape_element by a Shape_element_composing_relationship that specifies the Composite_unit_shape_element that is specified as the size_of as the relating_shape_element.

WR3: The dimension_type shall be either "angular" or "linear".

NOTE: The attribute dimension_type is declared in the Dimensional_characteristic entity of which this entity is a subtype.

Informal constraints:

IP1: The two shape elements that correspond to the Shape_elements that compose the Composite_unit_shape_element to which the Dimensional_size_based_on_opposing_boundaries is applied shall be opposite each other.
