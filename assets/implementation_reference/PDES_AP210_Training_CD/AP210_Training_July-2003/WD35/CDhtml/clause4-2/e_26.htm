4.2.25 Angular_size_dimension

NOTE: The text description for this Application Object has not been fully updated to agree with the EXPRESS.
An Angular_size_dimension is a type of Dimensional_size that characterizes the angularity of a Shape_element. If the Angular_size_dimension is a Single_boundary_dimension, the Angular_size_dimension specifies the angle formed by the characterized Shape_element. If the Angular_size_dimension is a Dimensional_size_based_on_opposing_boundaries, the Angular_size_dimension specifies the angle formed by two Shape_elements that make up the characterized Shape_element.

The data associated with this application object are the following:

  • half_angle
  • major_angle

4.2.25.1 half_angle

The half_angle specifies the boolean value that indicates whether the full angle is being characterized by the Angular_size_dimension, or whether the angle that is being characterized is the angle between the characterized Shape_element and a centre line or centre plane of the characterized Shape_element. A value of TRUE indicates that the angle between the Shape_element and a centre line or centre plane is being characterized. A value of FALSE indicates that the full angle is being characterized.

4.2.25.2 major_angle

The major_angle specifies the angle to which the Angular_size_dimension is applied. A value of LARGE indicates that it is the larger of the angles formed by the characterized Shape_element. A value of SMALL indicates that it is the smaller of the angles formed by the characterized Shape_element. A value of EQUAL indicates that it may be either of the angles formed by the characterized Shape_element, since they are defined to be equal.

Formal constraints:

WR1: The dimension_type shall be "angular".

NOTE: The attribute dimension_type is declared in the Dimensional_characteristic entity of which this entity is a subtype.

ENTITY angular_size_dimension
  SUBTYPE OF (dimensional_size);
    half_angle  : BOOLEAN;
    major_angle : angle_relator;
