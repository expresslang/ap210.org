4.2.131 Component_overlap_relationship

NOTE: The text description for this Application Object has not been fully updated to agree with the EXPRESS.
A Component_overlap_relationship is a type of Managed_design_object. A Component_overlap_relationship is the relationship between one component and another in their next assembly, with respect to the physical overlap between them. This overlap is often derived from the mechanism requirements of the factory equipment that assembles the components to their next assembly. The sequence of assembly is established by the previously_placed_component and the current_component, in turn, and the minimum clearance between the two components is taken into consideration during assembly.

The data associated with this application object are the following:

  • current_component
  • maximum_added_clearance
  • minimum_added_clearance
  • previously_placed_component

4.2.131.1 current_component

The current_component specifies the Next_higher_assembly_relationship, of the component to be placed next, for the Component_overlap_relationship.

4.2.131.2 maximum_added_clearance

The maximum_added_clearance specifies the Length_data_element of the maximum distance between the current_component and the previously_placed_component for the Component_overlap_relationship. The maximum_added_clearance need not be specified for a particular Component_overlap_relationship.

4.2.131.3 minimum_added_clearance

The minimum_added_clearance specifies the Length_data_element of the minimum distance between the current_component and the previously_placed_component for the Component_overlap_relationship.

4.2.131.4 previously_placed_component

The previously_placed_component specifies the Next_higher_assembly_relationship, of the component previously placed for the Component_overlap_relationship.

Formal constraints:

WR1: The previously_placed_component shall not be the current_component.

WR2: Both components shall be in the same version of the assembly.

WR3: A Datum_plane shall be defined for each of the current_component and previously_placed_component.

WR4: The length shall be measured between the two Datum_planes.

WR5: Each Datum_plane shall be based on a physical feature of the component.

ENTITY component_overlap_relationship
  SUBTYPE OF (managed_design_object);
    previously_placed_component : next_higher_assembly_relationship;
    current_component           : next_higher_assembly_relationship;
    minimum_added_clearance     : length_data_element;
    maximum_added_clearance     : OPTIONAL length_data_element;
END_ENTITY; -- component_overlap_relationship
