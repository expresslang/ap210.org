4.2.836 Specification_allocation

NOTE: The text description for this Application Object has not been fully updated to agree with the EXPRESS.
A Specification_allocation is a type of Managed_design_object. A Specification_allocation is the association between an Ee_specification and one or more of an Ee_product, an Ee_product_configuration, an Ee_product_definition, an Ee_product_version, a Physical_connectivity_definition, a Physical_connectivity_element, one or more of an Assembly_component, a Component_feature, a Stratum, one or more of an Inter_stratum_feature, a Laminate_component, or a Stratum_feature that results from the design activity.

The data associated with this application object are the following:

  • allocated_specification
  • target_product_object

4.2.836.1 allocated_specification

The allocated_specification specifies the role of the Ee_specification for the Specification_allocation.

4.2.836.2 target_product_object

The target_product_object specifies either an Ee_product, an Ee_product_configuration, an Ee_product_definition, an Ee_product_version, a Part_feature, a Physical_connectivity_definition a Physical_connectivity_element, or one of an Assembly_component, a Component_external_reference, a Component_feature, a Component_feature_external_reference, a Stratum or one of an Inter_stratum_feature, a Laminate_component, a Stratum_feature, or a Stratum_surface as the target through which the Ee_specification is realized by the Specification_allocation. There shall be one or more Ee_product, Ee_product_configuration, Ee_product_definition, Ee_product_version, Part_feature, Physical_connectivity_definition, Physical_connectivity_element, Assembly_component, Component_external_reference, Component_feature, Component_feature_external_reference, Stratum, Inter_stratum_feature, Laminate_component, Stratum_feature, or Stratum_surface for a Specification_allocation.
