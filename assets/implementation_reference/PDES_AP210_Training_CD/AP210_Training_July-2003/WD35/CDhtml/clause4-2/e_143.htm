4.2.143 Component_termination_passage

NOTE: The text description for this Application Object has not been fully updated to agree with the EXPRESS.
A Component_termination_passage is a type of Plated_passage through which a Component_terminal may pass. The properties of the Component_termination_passage are selected to support the Component_terminal.

NOTES:

  1. The model in this part of ISO 10303 is that the wall of the passage is directly supporting current flow.
  2. Support for the member of Component_terminal that passes through an Inter_stratum_feature but is not assembled to the wall of that Inter_stratum_feature is provided by the Cutout concept.
  3. Support for the member of Component_terminal that electrically is connected only to the surface of an Interconnect_module is provided by the Land concept.
  4. There is no requirement for the Component_termination_passage to completely penetrate the interconnect substrate.

EXAMPLES:

  1. This example omits the details of how this part of ISO 10303 establishes the relationship between the terminal and the passage for clarity.
  2. Figure ctp2 is a detail of a cross-section of an interconnect substrate showing a case of complete penetration of the substrate by the Component_termination_passage.

The data associated with this application object are the following:

  • access_mechanism
  • interface_access_mechanism
  • usage_definition

4.2.143.1 access_mechanism

The access_mechanism is an inverse relationship that specifies that the existence of the Component_termination_passage is dependent on the existence of the Component_termination_passage_join_terminal that specifies the Component_termination_passage as its associated_component. There shall be one or more Component_termination_passage_join_terminal for a Component_termination_passage.

4.2.143.2 interface_access_mechanism

The interface_access_mechanism is an inverse relationship that specifies that the existence of the Component_termination_passage is dependent on the existence of the Component_termination_passage_interface_terminal that specifies the Component_termination_passage as its associated_component.

4.2.143.3 usage_definition

The usage_definition specifies the role of the Component_termination_passage_template for the Component_termination_passage.

Formal constraints:

WR1: A Component_termination_passage shall be a feature of size.

NOTE: A Component_termination_passage is a feature of size, because it is part of the interface to another product in the assembly and the size of the passage shall be compatible with the appropriate assembly requirements of that product. Information about the other product's features needed for this function is specified in this part of ISO 10303 in the Default_component_termination_passage_definition specified by usage_definition.of_passage_technology.
