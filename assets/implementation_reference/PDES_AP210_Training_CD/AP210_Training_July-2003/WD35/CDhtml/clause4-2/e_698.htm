4.2.701 Positional_boundary_member

NOTE: The text description for this Application Object has not been fully updated to agree with the EXPRESS.
A Positional_boundary_member is a type of Non_feature_shape_element that is a member of a Dimension_related_positional_boundary. A Positional_boundary_member is defined by a Boundary_based_position_tolerance in conjunction with a Dimensional_size.

The data associated with this application object are the following:

  • boundary_member_definition
  • composed_positional_boundary

4.2.701.1 boundary_member_definition

The boundary_member_definition is an inverse relationship that specifies that the existence of the Positional_boundary_member is dependent on the existence of the Positional_boundary_member_definition that specifies the Positional_boundary_member as its defined_positional_boundary_member.

4.2.701.2 composed_positional_boundary

The composed_positional_boundary is an inverse relationship that specifies that the existence of the Positional_boundary_member is dependent on the existence of the Dimension_related_positional_boundary that specifies the Positional_boundary_member as one of its boundary_members.
