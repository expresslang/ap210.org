4.2.643 Pcb_usage_view

NOTE: The text description for this Application Object has not been fully updated to agree with the EXPRESS.
A Pcb_usage_view is a type of Interconnect_module_usage_view that provides the information necessary to include the Pcb in its next higher level assembly for design and some simulation purposes, but may not include sufficient information to reproduce the Pcb.

EXAMPLE: Some of the information in figures pcbcir and pcbcom that communicate fit tolerances may be included in a usage view.

NOTE: 
*)
ENTITY pcb_usage_view
  SUBTYPE OF (interconnect_module_usage_view);
END_ENTITY; -- pcb_usage_view
