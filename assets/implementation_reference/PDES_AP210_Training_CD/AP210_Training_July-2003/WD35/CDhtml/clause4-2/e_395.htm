4.2.395 Frozen_design_object

NOTE: The text description for this Application Object has not been fully updated to agree with the EXPRESS.
A Frozen_design_object is a type of Managed_design_object. A Frozen_design_object identifies the Managed_design_object which participates in the Frozen_assignment.

NOTE: The purpose of Frozen_design_object is for approval of design aspects. The typical use is to freeze allocations between the functional and physical design aspects. This use includes gate swapping and terminal swapping.

Informal constraints:

IP1: Each member of Frozen_design_object shall not be a member of any of the following: Certification, Contract, Ee_approval, Ee_product_configuration, Ee_product_definition, Ee_product_version, Planned_effectivity, Security_classification, Supplied_product_version, Work_order, or Work_request.
