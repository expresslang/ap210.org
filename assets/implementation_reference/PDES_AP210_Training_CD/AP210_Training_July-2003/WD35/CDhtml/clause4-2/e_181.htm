4.2.181 Conical_tolerance_zone_boundary_diametrical_size_characteristic

NOTE: The text description for this Application Object has not been fully updated to agree with the EXPRESS.
A Conical_tolerance_zone_boundary_diametrical_size_characteristic is a type of Managed_design_object. A Conical_tolerance_zone_boundary_diametrical_size_characteristic is a definition that characterizes the size of a Conical_tolerance_zone_boundary at one of the Physical_feature_or_part_template that defines the ends of the Conical_tolerance_zone_boundary.

The data associated with this application object are the following:

  • boundary_definition
  • sized_boundary_at_surface
  • tolerance_value

4.2.181.1 boundary_definition

The boundary_definition is an inverse relationship that specifies that the existence of the Conical_tolerance_zone_boundary_diametrical_size_characteristic is dependent on the existence of the Conical_tolerance_zone_boundary_definition that specifies the Conical_tolerance_zone_boundary_diametrical_size_characteristic as one of its boundary_sizes.

4.2.181.2 sized_boundary_at_surface

The sized_boundary_at_surface specifies the Conical_tolerance_zone_boundary_and_surface_relationship that associates the Conical_tolerance_zone_boundary with a Physical_feature_or_part_template that defines an end of the Conical_tolerance_zone_boundary at which the size is characterized by the Conical_tolerance_zone_boundary_diametrical_size_characteristic.

4.2.181.3 tolerance_value

The tolerance_value specifies the Length_data_element that represents the diameter of the specified Conical_tolerance_zone_boundary at the specified Physical_feature_or_part_template for the Conical_tolerance_zone_boundary_diametrical_size_characteristic.

Formal constraints:

WR1: The Conical_tolerance_zone_boundary specified as the sized_tolerance_zone_boundary by the Conical_tolerance_zone_boundary_and_surface_relationship that is specified as the sized_boundary_at_surface shall be the same instance of Conical_tolerance_zone_boundary as specified as the defined_zone_boundary by the Conical_tolerance_zone_boundary_definition that is specified as the boundary_definition. That is, the Conical_tolerance_zone_boundary_diametrical_size_characteristic and the Conical_tolerance_zone_boundary_definition specified as the boundary_definition shall define the same Conical_tolerance_zone_boundary.

WR2: The Conical_tolerance_zone_boundary_diametrical_size_characteristic shall be specified by exactly one Geometric_tolerance as the tolerance_zone_or_boundary_definition, and that Geometric_tolerance shall be one of a Position_tolerance, a Parallelism_tolerance, a Perpendicularity_tolerance, or an Angularity_tolerance.
