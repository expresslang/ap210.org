4.2.903 Thermal_isolation_removal_structure

NOTE: The text description for this Application Object has not been fully updated to agree with the EXPRESS.
A Thermal_isolation_removal_structure is a type of Filled_area_material_removal_structure included in the design for reasons of thermal isolation.

EXAMPLE: Figure tirs illustrates the removal of material from a power plane based on the shape of this structure.

The data associated with this application object are the following:

  • thermal_isolation_spacing_requirement

4.2.903.1 thermal_isolation_spacing_requirement

The thermal_isolation_spacing_requirement is an inverse relationship that specifies that the existence of the Thermal_isolation_removal_structure is dependent on the existence of the Thermal_isolation_requirement_assignment that specifies the Thermal_isolation_removal_structure as its resulting_structure.
