4.2.449 Interconnect_module_edge

NOTE: The text description for this Application Object has not been fully updated to agree with the EXPRESS.
An Interconnect_module_edge is a type of Inter_stratum_feature. When this information is represented by a planar shape, the interpretation is to be that the planar shape represents a projection of the maximum extent of the Interconnect_module_edge onto the planar shape. The Interconnect_module_edge forms an outer physical boundary of the interconnect substrate.

Formal constraints:

WR1: Each member of Interconnect_module_edge shall only be an external feature of the Interconnect_module.

WR2: Any geometry representing a member of Interconnect_module_edge shall be interpreted by a receiving system as a closed curve or a closed volume, depending on the dimensionality of the geometry.

NOTE:

ENTITY interconnect_module_edge
  SUPERTYPE OF (ONEOF(partially_plated_interconnect_module_edge,
	plated_interconnect_module_edge))
  SUBTYPE OF (inter_stratum_feature);
END_ENTITY; -- interconnect_module_edge
