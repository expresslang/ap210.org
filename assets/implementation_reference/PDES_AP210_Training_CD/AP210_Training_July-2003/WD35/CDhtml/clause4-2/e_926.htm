4.2.926 Tolerance_zone_opposing_boundary_set

NOTE: The text description for this Application Object has not been fully updated to agree with the EXPRESS.
A Tolerance_zone_opposing_boundary_set is a type of Tolerance_zone_boundary that consists of a set of two like boundary members that are separated by a constant distance. A Tolerance_zone_opposing_boundary_set is either a Tolerance_zone_explicit_opposing_boundary_set or a Tolerance_zone_implicit_opposing_boundary_set.

The data associated with this application object are the following:

  • size_characteristic

4.2.926.1 size_characteristic

The size_characteristic is an inverse relationship that specifies that the existence of the Tolerance_zone_opposing_boundary_set is dependent on the existence of the Opposing_boundary_set_size_characteristic that specifies the Tolerance_zone_opposing_boundary_set as its sized_boundary_set.

EXAMPLE: Two opposing planes comprise the Tolerance_zone_boundary_set that is defined when a Flatness_tolerance is applied to the top surface of a printed circuit board. The size_characteristic specifies the separation between the two opposing planes.
