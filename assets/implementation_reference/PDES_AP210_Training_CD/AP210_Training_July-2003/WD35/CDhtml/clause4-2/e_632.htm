4.2.629 Part_template_planar_keepout_shape

NOTE: The text description for this Application Object has not been fully updated to agree with the EXPRESS.
A Part_template_planar_keepout_shape is a type of Planar_shape and a type of Non_feature_shape_definition. A Part_template_planar_keepout_shape is the projected shape of a keepout region around the Part_template in two dimensions.

NOTES:

  1. Keepout regions typically include some minimal spacing around the extreme shape represented by Part_template_planar_shape where the exact relationship between the two shapes is unrecorded.
  2. There may be multiple Part_template_planar_keepout_shape for a particular purpose. The recommended method to aggregate several Part_template_planar_keepout_shape that together describe a volumetric constraint is to reference them from a member of Shape_and_ee_product_definition_based_constraint_occurrence.

The data associated with this application object are the following:

  • application_technology_constraint
  • centroid_location
  • constrained_design_object_category
  • shape_characterized_part_template
  • shape_environment
  • shape_material_condition
  • shape_purpose
  • shape_representation

4.2.629.1 application_technology_constraint

The application_technology_constraint specifies the role of Ee_requirement_occurrence for the Part_template_3d_shape. The application_technology_constraint is the relationship between keepout shapes and process constraints under which those keepout shapes are valid.

EXAMPLE: An example of an application_technology_constraint is the relationship of the shape of a clearance area to an assembly or testing process which requires the clearance.

4.2.629.2 centroid_location

The centroid_location is a Cartesian_point which is the center of a Part_template_planar_keepout_shape. A centroid_location need not be specified for a particular Part_template_planar_keepout_shape.

4.2.629.3 constrained_design_object_category

The constrained_design_object_category specifies the category of design object for which the Part_template_planar_keepout_shape is a constraint.

4.2.629.4 shape_characterized_part_template

The shape_characterized_part_unit specifies the role of the Part_template for the Part_template_planar_keepout_shape.

4.2.629.5 shape_environment

The shape_environment specifies either manufacturing or an end_user_application as the environment for the Part_template_planar_keepout_shape.

4.2.629.6 shape_material_condition

The shape_material_condition specifies either the maximum_material_condition, the minimum_material_condition, or the nominal_material_condition as the material condition for the Part_template_planar_keepout_shape.

4.2.629.7 shape_purpose

The shape_purpose specifies either electro_magnetic_compatibility, shock, thermal, vibration, or an external_definition as the purpose for the Part_template_planar_keepout_shape.
