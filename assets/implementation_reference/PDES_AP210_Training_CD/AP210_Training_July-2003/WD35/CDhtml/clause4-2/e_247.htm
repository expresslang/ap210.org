4.2.247 Default_attachment_size_based_land_physical_template

A Default_attachment_size_based_land_physical_template is a type of Default_land_physical_template. A Default_attachment_size_based_land_physical_template may be a Default_attachment_size_and_component_termination_passage_based_land_physical_template or a Default_attachment_size_and_via_based_land_physical_template. This template shape is dependent only on the shape of the Part_terminal it is intended to mate with.

The data associated with this application object are the following:

  • interface_access_mechanisms
  • minimum_attachment_region_size
  • maximum_attachment_region_size

4.2.247.1 interface_access_mechanisms

The interface_access_mechanisms is an inverse relationship that specifies that the existence of the Default_attachment_size_based_land_physical_template is dependent on the existence of the Land_template_interface_terminal that specifies the Default_attachment_size_based_land_physical_template as its associated_definition. There shall be one or more Land_template_interface_terminal for a Default_attachment_size_based_land_physical_template. Fabrication and assembly tolerances define the required shapes and variances for the terminal shapes.

4.2.247.2 minimum_attachment_region_size

The minimum_attachment_region_size specifies one of the roles of the Connection_zone for the Default_attachment_size_based_land_physical_template. The minimum_attachment_region_size specifies the smallest Connection_zone for the Default_attachment_size_based_land_physical_template.

4.2.247.3 maximum_attachment_region_size

The maximum_attachment_region_size specifies one of the roles of the Connection_zone for the Default_attachment_size_based_land_physical_template. The maximum_attachment_region_size specifies the largest Connection_zone for the Default_attachment_size_based_land_physical_template.

ENTITY default_attachment_size_based_land_physical_template
  SUPERTYPE OF (ONEOF (
	default_attachment_size_and_component_termination_passage_based_land_physical_template,
      default_attachment_size_and_via_based_land_physical_template))
  SUBTYPE OF (default_land_physical_template);
    minimum_attachment_region_size : OPTIONAL connection_zone;
    maximum_attachment_region_size : OPTIONAL connection_zone;
  INVERSE
    interface_access_mechanisms : SET [1:?] OF land_template_interface_terminal FOR
                        associated_definition;
WHERE
  WR1: NOT(EXISTS(minimum_attachment_region_size) AND 
           EXISTS(maximum_attachment_region_size)) OR
       (minimum_attachment_region_size :<>:
       maximum_attachment_region_size);
END_ENTITY; -- default_attachment_size_based_land_physical_template
