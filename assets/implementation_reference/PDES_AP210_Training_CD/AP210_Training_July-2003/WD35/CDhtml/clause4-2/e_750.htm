4.2.750 Profile_related_positional_boundary

NOTE: The text description for this Application Object has not been fully updated to agree with the EXPRESS.
A Profile_related_positional_boundary is a type of Positional_boundary that is a theoretical boundary that is defined by a Boundary_based_position_tolerance in conjunction with a Profile_of_any_surface_tolerance. The Profile_of_any_surface_tolerance defines a Tolerance_zone that is bounded by two Tolerance_zone_boundary_member. The Profile_related_positional_boundary is offset from one of these Tolerance_zone_boundary_member. The particular Tolerance_zone_boundary_member and the direction of offset are determined by the material conditions specified by the Boundary_based_position_tolerance. If the Boundary_based_position_tolerance is specified on a "maximum material principle" basis, then the Tolerance_zone_boundary_member from which the Profile_related_positional_boundary is offset is the one that is offset from the True_profile"away from material". Similarly, if the Boundary_based_position_tolerance is specified on a "least material requirement" basis, then the Tolerance_zone_boundary_member from which the Profile_related_positional_boundary is offset is the one that is offset from the True_profile "towards material". The Profile_related_positional_boundary is offset from the Tolerance_zone_boundary_member in the same direction that the Tolerance_zone_boundary_member is offset from the True_profile.

The data associated with this application object are the following:

  • associating_relationship

4.2.750.1 associating_relationship

The associating_relationship is an inverse relationship that specifies that the existence of the Profile_related_positional_boundary is dependent on the existence of the Positional_boundary_and_profile_boundary_member_relationship that specifies the Profile_related_positional_boundary as its offset_positional_boundary.

Formal constraints:

WR1: The Positional_boundary_definition specified as the boundary_definition shall be a Profile_related_positional_boundary_definition.

NOTE: The attribute boundary_definition is declared in the Positional_boundary entity of which this entity is a subtype.
