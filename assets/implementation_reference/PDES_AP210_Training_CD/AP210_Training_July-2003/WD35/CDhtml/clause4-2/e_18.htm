4.2.18 Analytical_model_port_assignment

NOTE: The text description for this Application Object has not been fully updated to agree with the EXPRESS.
An Analytical_model_port_assignment is a type of Managed_design_object. An Analytical_model_port_assignment is the relationship between a Functional_unit_usage_view_terminal_definition and an Analytical_model_port for the purpose of conducting analyses of the design. An Analytical_model_port_assignment binds or associates a behavioural representation to a structural representation. The cardinality or size of the Analytical_model_port shall be consistent with the cardinality or size of the terminal in the structural representation.

NOTE: This mechanism allows model ports and functional terminal definitionss to be associated regardless of their individual names.

The data associated with this application object are the following:

  • assigned_analytical_model_port
  • assigned_functional_unit_terminal

4.2.18.1 assigned_analytical_model_port

The assigned_analytical_model_port specifies the role of the Analytical_model_port for the Analytical_model_port_assignment.

4.2.18.2 assigned_functional_unit_terminal

The assigned_functional_unit_terminal specifies the role of the Functional_unit_usage_view_terminal_definition for the Analytical_model_port_assignment.

NOTE: Usually, only one Analytical_model is bound to a Functional_unit_definition at a time.

ENTITY analytical_model_port_assignment
  SUBTYPE OF (managed_design_object);
    assigned_functional_unit_terminal : functional_unit_usage_view_terminal_definition;
    assigned_analytical_model_port    : analytical_model_port;
WHERE
 WR1: NOT(('AP210_ARM.ANALYTICAL_MODEL_SCALAR_PORT' IN TYPEOF(assigned_analytical_model_port))
      XOR ('AP210_ARM.SCALAR_TERMINAL_DEFINITION' IN TYPEOF(assigned_functional_unit_terminal)));
END_ENTITY; -- analytical_model_port_assignment
