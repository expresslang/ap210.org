4.2.568 Opposing_boundary_set_size_characteristic

NOTE: The text description for this Application Object has not been fully updated to agree with the EXPRESS.
An Opposing_boundary_set_size_characteristic is a type of Boundary_size_characteristic that defines the distance between the two members of a Tolerance_zone_opposing_boundary_set. The two members may or may not be explicitly identified.

The data associated with this application object are the following:

  • sized_boundary_set

4.2.568.1 sized_boundary_set

The sized_boundary_set specifies the Tolerance_zone_opposing_boundary_set of which the distance between its two members is defined by the Opposing_boundary_set_size_characteristic.

Formal constraints:

WR1: The Tolerance_zone_boundary specified as the sized_boundary_set shall be the same instance of Tolerance_zone_boundary as specified by the defined_zone_boundary of the Boundary_zone_definition_with_specified_size that is specified as the boundary_definition.

NOTE: The attribute boundary_definition is declared in the Boundary_size_characteristic entity of which this entity is a subtype.
