4.2.19 Analytical_model_port_make_from_relationship

NOTE: The text description for this Application Object has not been fully updated to agree with the EXPRESS.
An Analytical_model_port_make_from_relationship is a type of Managed_design_object.

The data associated with this application object are the following:

  • associated_make_from
  • reusable_feature
  • resultant_feature

4.2.19.1 associated_make_from

The associated_make_from specifies an Analytical_model_make_from_relationship for the Analytical_model_port_make_from_relationship.

4.2.19.2 reusable_feature

The reusable_feature specifies an Analytical_model_port for the Analytical_model_port_make_from_relationship.

4.2.19.3 resultant_feature

The resultant_feature specifies an Analytical_model_port for the Analytical_model_port_make_from_relationship.

Formal constraints:

UR1: The combination of reusable_feature and resultant_feature shall be unique within a population of Analytical_model_port_make_from_relationship.

WR1: The reusable_feature shall not be the resultant_feature within a population of Analytical_model_port_make_from_relationship.

WR2: The Analytical_model referenced by the reusable_feature shall not be the Analytical_model referenced by the resultant_feature within a population of Analytical_model_port_make_from_relationship.
