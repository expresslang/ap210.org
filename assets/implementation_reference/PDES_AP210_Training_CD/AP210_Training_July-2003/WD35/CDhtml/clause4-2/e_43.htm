4.2.43 Assembly_module

NOTE: The text description for this Application Object has not been fully updated to agree with the EXPRESS.
An Assembly_module is a type of Physical_unit_design_view. An Assembly_module may be a Pca. An Assembly_module is an assembly design view of a product. This application object supports design reuse, modular product definitions, component libraries, and related technologies. There may be discipline views that further specialize this view.

EXAMPLE: Figure pudv is an illustration of an Assembly_module.

NOTES:

  1. An Assembly_module could be a ball grid array design, an MCM design, a disk drive design, a line card or plug-in module in a telecommunications rack, or a line replaceable unit in an airframe or spacecraft. Incomplete designs that have some specified functionality as defined by a functional definition may be included in a component library application, for example. The designs may be included in an active design by reference. At the appropriate time in the design phase, the single reference is replaced with individual references to the elements that compose the Assembly_module. Support is provided for maintaining the information necessary to trace back to the original Assembly_module from the released Assembly_component through the Component_make_from_relationship.
  2. Product categories other than Pca, such as MCM-L (Multichip Module, Laminated), MCM-D (Multichip Module, Deposited) and MCM-C (Multichip Module, Ceramic) may be included through the use of this application object, and population of the Technology and Ee_product_category application objects.

EXAMPLE: In this example (based on the previous notes), assembly technology is the process that bonds two products together (e.g., soldering, welding) by joining individual features of one product to individual features of the other product. The Assembly_module would have two components: A Packaged_part and an Interconnect_module. The Interconnect_module would include a Land pattern designed to optimize the assembly process yield for the Packaged_part and the Stratum stackup of the Interconnect_module combination. Full knowledge of the Interconnect_module technology is available through the complete definition of the Passage_technology and Stratum_technology supported in this part of ISO 10303. The assembly technology characteristics assigned by an enterprise may be exchanged through population of the Ee_product_category and Ee_product_related_product_category application objects. Technology classification may be exchanged using the Technology application object. If desired, Analytical_representation may be populated to support process simulation. Package may be used instead of Packaged_part in the Assembly_module if there is no interest in electrical characteristics.

Formal constraints:

WR1: A Laminate_component shall not be in the role of component for the Assembly_composition_relationship that references an Assembly_module in the role of assembly
