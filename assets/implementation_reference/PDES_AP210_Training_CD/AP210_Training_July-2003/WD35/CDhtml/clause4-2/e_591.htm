4.2.581 Package_terminal

NOTE: The text description for this Application Object has not been fully updated to agree with the EXPRESS.
A Package_terminal is a type of Part_feature. A Package_terminal may be one or more of a Guided_wave_terminal, a Altered_package_terminal, a Wire_terminal, or a Primary_reference_terminal. When the case or package body is used as a terminal then Package_terminal shall be populated to specify the feature of the Package_body that serves that purpose. The Package_terminal shall be in a sequence, with the initial member being Primary_reference_terminal, and each member that is not a Primary_reference_terminal shall reference a previous member in the sequence using the precedent_feature attribute inherited from Part_feature. The seating_plane_zone shall not exist if the seating_plane_intersection attribute is 'does not intersect'. The seating_plane_intersection attribute shall not be 'does not intersect' if the seating_plane_zone attribute exists.

NOTE: The Seating_plane referenced indirectly by this terminal may be in the role of interface_plane for the Package referenced by associaed_definition.

4.2.581.1 associated_definition

The associated_definition specifies the role of the Package for the Package_terminal.

4.2.581.2 external_connection_zone

The external_connection_zone specifies the Connection_zone on the outside surface of the Package_terminal. There may be more than one Connection_zone for a Package_terminal. The external_connection_zone is optional and need not be supplied if the source system does not contain the data. If the source system contains the data, then the external_connection_zone shall be supplied.

NOTE: The Feature_shape_occurrence specified by the terminal or mounting feature may be the same Feature_shape_occurrence that is specified by the associated Connection_zone. In this case the whole terminal is the Connection_zone.

4.2.581.3 feature_shape

The feature_shape specifies the role of the Feature_shape_occurrence for the Package_terminal. There shall be one or more Feature_shape_occurrence for the Package_terminal.

4.2.581.4 internal_connection_zone

The internal_connection_zone_specifies the Connection_zone on the inside portion of the Package_terminal. There may be more than one Connection_zone in the role of internal_connection_zone. The internal_connection_zone need not be specified for a particular Package_terminal.

4.2.581.5 seating_plane_intersection

The seating_plane_intersection specifies whether or not the shape of the Package_terminal intersects the shape of the seating plane to within the tolerance of the geometric context. The values allowed for the attribute are 'surface_intersection', 'through_intersection', 'does_not_intersect'.

NOTE: Not all terminals need to intersect the seating plane.

4.2.581.6 maximum_terminal_diametrical_extent

The maximum_terminal_diametrical_extent specifies the Length_data_element that represents the maximum allowed diameter of a Package_terminal. The Length_data_element need not be specified for a Package_terminal. The Length_data_element is the diameter of a circle that circumscribes the cross-sectional shape of the terminal.

4.2.581.7 minimum_terminal_diametrical_extent

The minimum_terminal_diametrical_extent specifies the Length_data_element that represents the minimum allowed diameter of a Package_terminal. The Length_data_element need not be specified for a Package_terminal. The Length_data_element is the diameter of a circle that circumscribes the cross-sectional shape of the terminal.

4.2.581.8 model_representation

The model_representation specifies the Analytical_representation that embodies the analysis characteristics identified as terminal_characteristics. The model_representation need not be specified for a particular Package_terminal. There may be more than one Analytical_representation for a Package_terminal.

4.2.581.9 terminal_characteristic

The terminal_characteristic specifies the Characteristic of the Package_terminal. The terminal_characteristic need not be specified for a particular Package_terminal. There may be more than one Characteristic for a Package_terminal.

4.2.581.10 terminal_core_material

The terminal_core_material specifies the role of the Material_definition for the Package_terminal core material. The terminal_core_material is optional and need not be supplied.

4.2.581.11 terminal_surface_material

The terminal_surface_material specifies the role of the Material_definition for the outer surface of the Package_terminal surface material.

Formal constraints:

WR1: The Package instance specified by associated_definition shall have a cartesian coordinate system frame of reference.

ENTITY package_terminal
  SUPERTYPE OF (((altered_package_terminal ANDOR guided_wave_terminal) 
      ANDOR wire_terminal) ANDOR primary_reference_terminal)
  SUBTYPE OF (part_feature);
    SELF\part_feature.associated_definition : package;
    external_connection_zone                : OPTIONAL SET [1:?] OF connection_zone;
    internal_connection_zone                : OPTIONAL SET [1:?] OF connection_zone;
    seating_plane_intersection              : seating_plane_intersection_type;
    terminal_surface_material               : material_definition;
    model_representation                    : OPTIONAL SET [1:?] OF 
                                              analytical_representation;
    terminal_characteristic                 : OPTIONAL SET [1:?] OF characteristic;
    terminal_core_material                  : OPTIONAL material_definition;
    minimum_terminal_diametrical_extent     : OPTIONAL length_data_element;
    maximum_terminal_diametrical_extent     : OPTIONAL length_data_element;
    seating_plane_zone                      : 
                       OPTIONAL connection_zone_interface_plane_relationship;
DERIVE
    mates_with_substrate : BOOLEAN := (EXISTS(seating_plane_zone)) AND 
      (associated_definition.package_seating_plane :=:
       seating_plane_zone.interface_plane);
INVERSE
    SELF\part_feature.feature_shape         : SET [1:?] OF usage_concept_usage_relationship FOR
    associated_usage;
WHERE
 WR1: NOT(EXISTS(seating_plane_zone)) OR
      (SIZEOF(QUERY( cz <* external_connection_zone |
      (seating_plane_zone.derived_zone = cz)
      )) = 1);
 WR2: NOT(EXISTS(seating_plane_zone)) OR
      (SIZEOF(QUERY( cz <* external_connection_zone |
      (seating_plane_zone.mating_zone = cz)
      )) = 1);
 WR3: NOT(EXISTS(seating_plane_zone)) OR
      ((associated_definition.package_seating_plane :=:
       seating_plane_zone.interface_plane) OR
      (associated_definition.interface_plane[1] :=:
       seating_plane_zone.interface_plane));
 WR4: NOT(EXISTS(seating_plane_zone)) OR
         ((seating_plane_intersection = surface_intersection) OR
          (seating_plane_intersection = through_intersection));
 WR5: NOT((seating_plane_intersection = does_not_intersect) AND
      (EXISTS(seating_plane_zone)));
END_ENTITY; -- package_terminal
