4.2.641 Pca_usage_view

NOTE: The text description for this Application Object has not been fully updated to agree with the EXPRESS.
A Pca_usage_view is a type of Assembly_module_usage_view. A Pca_usage_view provides the information needed to include the Pca in the design of the next higher level assembly, but not necessarily sufficient information to reproduce that Pca.

NOTE: A Packaged_connector may be located in a usage view in addition to terminals. Reference Assembly_module_usage_view_connector_relationship

EXAMPLE: Figure puv illustrates details available in a usage view for a Pca. The information available in the usage view specifically is separate from that in the design view and the relationships between the two views are usually controlled by a design organization.

Formal constraints:

WR1: Each Pca_usage_view shall have at least two terminals.
