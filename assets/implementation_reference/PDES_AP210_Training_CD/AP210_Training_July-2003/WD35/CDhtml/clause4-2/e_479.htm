4.2.479 Join_relationship

NOTE: The text description for this Application Object has not been fully updated to agree with the EXPRESS.
A Join_relationship is a type of Managed_design_object. A Join_relationship is either an Inter_stratum_join_relationship or an Intra_stratum_join_relationship. A Join_relationship identifies a strict subset of the Layer_connection_point identified by Physical_network topology. This subset is then inherited by both types of Join_relationship to ensure topological integrity.

The data associated with this application object are the following:

  • associated_layout
  • network_topology
  • points_to_be_connected

4.2.479.1 associated_layout

The associated_layout specifies the Interconnect_module that is the design in which the Join_relationship is contained.

4.2.479.2 network_topology

The network_topology is an inverse relationship that specifies that the existence of the Join_relationship is dependent on the existence of the Physical_network that specifies the Join_relationship as its interconnect_module_network_topology.

4.2.479.3 points_to_be_connected

The points_to_be_connected specifies the role of the Layer_connection_point for the Join_relationship. There shall be at least two Layer_connection_point in a Join_relationship.

  ABSTRACT SUPERTYPE OF (ONEOF (inter_stratum_join_relationship,
      intra_stratum_join_relationship,
      unrouted_join_relationship))
  SUBTYPE OF (managed_design_object);
   associated_layout : interconnect_module;
