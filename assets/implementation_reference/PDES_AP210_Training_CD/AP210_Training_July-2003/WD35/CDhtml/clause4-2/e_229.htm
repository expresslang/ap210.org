4.2.229 Datum_feature_usage_in_datum_relationship

NOTE: The text description for this Application Object has not been fully updated to agree with the EXPRESS.
A Datum_feature_usage_in_datum_relationship is a type of Shape_element_relationship that associates a Datum_feature with a Datum that the Datum_feature participates in establishing. A Datum_feature_usage_in_datum_relationship is either a Datum_feature_usage_in_common_datum_relationship or a Datum_feature_usage_in_single_datum_relationship.

The data associated with this application object are the following:

  • related_shape_element
  • relating_shape_element

4.2.229.1 related_shape_element

The related_shape_element specifies the Datum_feature that is used to establish the Datum specified as the relating_shape_element for the Datum_feature_usage_in_datum_relationship.

4.2.229.2 relating_shape_element

The relating_shape_element specifies the Datum that the specified Datum_feature participates in establishing for the Datum_feature_usage_in_datum_relationship.

Formal constraints:

UR1: The combination of relating_shape_element and related_shape_element shall be unique within a population of Datum_feature_usage_in_datum_relationship. That is, a specific Datum_feature shall not be used more than once to establish a specific Datum.
