4.2.171 Conductive_interconnect_element

A Conductive_interconnect_element is a type of Laminate_component. A Conductive_interconnect_element is either a Conductive_interconnect_element_with_pre_defined_transitions or a Conductive_interconnect_element_with_user_defined_single_transition or an Unrouted_conductive_interconnect_element. A Conductive_interconnect_element specifies one portion of a Stratum_feature that has a sequential arrangement of Layer_connection_point.

NOTE: A domain term for a Conductive_interconnect_element is a trace. The domain term may include additional aspects over that defined in this application object in certain cases. For that reason, this part of ISO 10303 does not attempt to prescribe or limit the application of the term trace by standardizing it.

EXAMPLE: An example of multiple Conductive_interconnect_element, Land, and Stratum_feature_template_component that contribute to the definition of a Conductor are shown in the partially exploded view of a Conductor in figure cie.

The data associated with this application object are the following:

  • access_mechanisms
  • composed_conductor

4.2.171.1 access_mechanisms

The access_mechanisms is an inverse relationship that specifies that the existence of the Conductive_interconnect_element is dependent on the existence of the Conductive_interconnect_element_terminal that specifies the Conductive_interconnect_element as its associated_component. There shall be at least two Conductive_interconnect_element_terminals for a Conductive_interconnect_element.

4.2.171.2 composed_conductor

The composed_conductor specifies the role of the Stratum_feature for the Conductive_interconnect_element. The composed_conductor need not be specified for a Conductive_interconnect_element.

Formal constraints:

WR1: The members of Layer_connection_point specified by a member of Conductive_interconnect_element shall also be specified by the feature this interconnect_element is composing. This is a non-exclusive rule. Other members of Layer_connection_point may be specified by the conductor.

    composed_conductor : OPTIONAL stratum_feature;
