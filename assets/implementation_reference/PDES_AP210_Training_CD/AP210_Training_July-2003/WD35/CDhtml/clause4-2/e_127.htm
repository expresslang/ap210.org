4.2.127 Component_mating_constraint_condition

NOTE: The text description for this Application Object has not been fully updated to agree with the EXPRESS.
A Component_mating_constraint_condition is a type of Managed_design_object. A Component_mating_constraint_condition identifies the degrees of freedom that have been constrained by a set of members of Assembly_joint. A Component_mating_constraint_condition provides "x","y","z" as the set of axes, and "u","v","w" as the set of rotations about those axes. If an axis is included in the set, then translation along that axis is constrained. If a rotation is included in the set, then rotation about the corresponding axis is constrained. The pairs of axis,rotation are: x,u; y,v;z,w. The axes are referenced in the local coordinate system of the component referenced by the related attribute of the members of Assembly_joint referenced by constraining_joints. Exactly one member of Assembly_component shall be referenced by the related attribute of members of Assembly_joint referenced by constraining_joints.

The data associated with this application object are the following:

  • constraining_joint
  • constrained_degree_of_freedom
  • of_context
  • name

4.2.127.1 constraining_joint

The constraining_joint specifies the Assembly_joint for the Component_mating_constraint_condition. There shall be at least one member of constraining_joint and there shall be no more than three members of constraining_joint.

4.2.127.2 constrained_degrees_of_freedom

The constrained_degrees_of_freedom specifies the identifier of the degrees of freedom that are constrained. There shall be at least one and there shall be no more than five degrees of freedom specified.

4.2.127.3 context

The of_context specifies the role of the Physical_unit_design_view for the Component_mating_constraint_condition.

4.2.127.4 name

The name specifies the identifier of the Component_mating_constraint_condition in the context oof the Physical_unit_design_view.
