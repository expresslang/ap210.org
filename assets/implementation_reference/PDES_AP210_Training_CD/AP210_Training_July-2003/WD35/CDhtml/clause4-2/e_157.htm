4.2.158 Composite_feature_relating_position_tolerance

NOTE: The text description for this Application Object has not been fully updated to agree with the EXPRESS.
A Composite_feature_relating_position_tolerance is a type of Position_tolerance that is used in conjunction with a Composite_feature_locating_position_tolerance and possibly two other Composite_feature_relating_position_tolerance to apply a less restrictive specification on the location of a Shape_element group with respect to one or more Datum than on the location and orientation of those Shape_element within the group with respect to each other. If the Composite_feature_relating_position_tolerance is also a Position_tolerance_with_referenced_datum_system, the defined Tolerance_zone is oriented but not located relative to the Datum_system the definition of which is specified as the datum_system_definition.

NOTE: The attribute datum_system_definition is declared in the Geometric_tolerance_with_referenced_datum_system entity of which this entity is a subtype.

The data associated with this application object are the following:

  • refined_control

4.2.158.1 refined_control

The refined_control specifies either a Composite_feature_locating_position_tolerance or another Composite_feature_relating_position_tolerance to which this Composite_feature_relating_position_tolerance assists in specifying design requirements by specifying a further refinement of the location and orientation of the group of Shape_element in relation to each other.

Formal constraints:

WR1: The Shape_element specified as the toleranced_shape_element by this Composite_feature_relating_position_tolerance shall be the same instance of Shape_element as specified as the toleranced_shape_element by the Position_tolerance that is specified as the refined_control.

WR2: If this Composite_feature_relating_position_tolerance is also a Position_tolerance_without_referenced_datum_system, it shall not be specified as a refined_control by another Composite_feature_relating_position_tolerance.

WR3: If this Composite_feature_relating_position_tolerance specifies a Composite_feature_relating_position_tolerance as the refined_control, this Composite_feature_relating_position_tolerance shall either be a Position_tolerance_without_referenced_datum_system or have less Datum in its Datum_system than the Datum_system of the Composite_feature_relating_position_tolerance specified as the refined_control.

WR4: If this Composite_feature_relating_position_tolerance specifies a Composite_feature_locating_position_tolerance as the refined_control, this Composite_feature_relating_position_tolerance shall either be a Position_tolerance_without_referenced_datum_system or have less than or an equal number of Datum in its Datum_system than the Datum_system of the Composite_feature_locating_position_tolerance specified as the refined_control.

WR5: If this Composite_feature_relating_position_tolerance is a Position_tolerance_with_referenced_datum_system, the Datum in its Datum_system shall be in the Datum_system of the Position_tolerance specified as the refined_control and shall be assigned the same datum precedence.

WR6: If this Composite_feature_relating_position_tolerance specifies a Position_tolerance that specifies a Datum_system_defining_conditions_without_material_conditions as the datum_system_definition, the Position_tolerance specified as the refined_control shall also specify a Datum_system_defining_conditions_without_material_conditions as its datum_system_definition.

WR7: If this Composite_feature_relating_position_tolerance specifies a Position_tolerance that specifies a Datum_system_defining_conditions_with_material_conditions as the datum_system_definition; the Position_tolerance specified as the refined_control shall also specify a Datum_system_defining_conditions_with_material_conditions as its datum_system_definition, and each Datum_feature of the Datum_system of this Composite_feature_relating_position_tolerance shall be assigned the same material condition property as is assigned to it in the Datum_system of the Position_tolerance specified as the refined_control.

WR8: The size of the Tolerance_zone_boundary of this Composite_feature_relating_position_tolerance shall be less than the size of the Tolerance_zone_boundary of the Position_tolerance specified as the refined_control.

WR9: Both the Composite_feature_relating_position_tolerance and the Position_tolerance specified as the refined_control shall specify the same type of Tolerance_zone_definition.

WR10: The Composite_feature_relating_position_tolerance shall be specified as a refined_control by at most one Composite_feature_relating_position_tolerance.

WR11: Both the Composite_feature_relating_position_tolerance and the Position_tolerance specified as the refined_control shall specify the same type of Tolerance_zone_boundary_definition.

WR12: Both the Composite_feature_relating_position_tolerance and the Position_tolerance specified as the refined_control shall specify the same type of Boundary_size_characteristic.
