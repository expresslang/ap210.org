4.2.674 Placement_group

NOTE: The text description for this Application Object has not been fully updated to agree with the EXPRESS.
A Placement_group is a type of Managed_design_object. A Placement_group is a group composed of Assembly_component that are associated with each other for this purpose by the Component_group_assignment, in order to meet some specific placement requirement. The placement is assigned through the Placement_group_area_assignment object, and does not indicate the explicit geometric relationship of the Assembly_components to each other, only that they are all to be placed within an area.

EXAMPLES:

  1. An example of a Placement_group is the collection of Assembly_components necessary to satisfy the functional requirement for a power supply.
  2. An example of a Placement_group is the collection of Assembly_components necessary to satisfy the functional requirement for a digital signal processor.
  3. An example of a Placement_group is the collection of Assembly_components with a case temperature greater than 55 degrees Centigrade. The associated area would be a rectangle all of whose inner points are within 6 cm of the cooling unit.

The data associated with this application object are the following:

  • design_specific_usage
  • requirement

4.2.674.1 design_specific_usage

The design_specific_usage specifies the name of the Placement_group.

EXAMPLE: Examples of design_specific_usage include analogue input buffer, preprocessor, emi filter, video multiplexor, and high speed digital.

4.2.674.2 requirement

The requirement specifies the role of the Ee_requirement_occurrence for the Placement_group. There shall be one or more Ee_requirement_occurrence for a Placement_group.
