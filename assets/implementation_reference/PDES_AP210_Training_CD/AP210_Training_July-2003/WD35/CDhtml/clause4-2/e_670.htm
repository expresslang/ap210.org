4.2.670 Physical_unit_planar_shape

NOTE: The text description for this Application Object has not been fully updated to agree with the EXPRESS.
A Physical_unit_planar_shape is a type of Planar_projected_shape. A Physical_unit_planar_shape is the projected shape of the Physical_unit in two dimensions. A Physical_unit_planar_shape shall not reference a Packaged_part.

NOTES:

  1. When a extreme boundary of the actual shape is provided, static mass property calculations based on the extreme shape may be invalid, depending on the purpose.
  2. The shape of the Package is the shape for a Packaged_part that references that Package, eliminating the requirement to provide a shape for a Packaged_part.

The data associated with this application object are the following:

  • application_technology_constraint
  • centroid_location
  • shape_characterized_physical_unit
  • shape_environment
  • shape_distance_from_seating_plane
  • shape_location_with_respect_to_seating_plane
  • shape_material_condition
  • shape_purpose
  • shape_representation

4.2.670.1 application_technology_constraint

The application_technology_constraint specifies the role of Ee_requirement_occurrence for the Physical_unit_3d_shape. The application_technology_constraint is the relationship between physical shapes and process constraints under which those shapes are valid.

4.2.670.2 centroid_location

The centroid_location is a Cartesian_point which is the center of a Physical_unit_planar_shape. A centroid_location need not be specified for a particular Physical_unit_planar_shape.

4.2.670.3 shape_characterized_physical_unit

The shape_characterized_part_unit specifies the role of the Physical_unit for the Physical_unit_planar_shape.

4.2.670.4 shape_distance_from_seating_plane

The shape_distance_from_seating_plane specifies the distance of the Physical_unit_planar_shape from the seating_plane. The shape_distance_from_seating_plane is coordinated with the shape_location_with_respect_to_seating_plane. The shape_distance_from_seating_plane need not be provided with a Physical_unit_planar_shape.

4.2.670.5 shape_environment

The shape_environment specifies either manufacturing or an end_user_application as the environment for the Physical_unit_planar_shape.

4.2.670.6 shape_location_with_respect_to_seating_plane

The shape_location_with_respect_to_seating_plane specifies the location of the Physical_unit_planar_shape as 'above', 'below' or 'congruent' with the seating_plane.

4.2.670.7 shape_material_condition

The shape_material_condition specifies either the maximum_material_condition, the minimum_material_condition, or the nominal_material_condition as the material condition for the Physical_unit_planar_shape.

4.2.670.8 shape_purpose

The shape_purpose specifies either a design, a design_profile, a design_profile_above_seating_plane, a design_profile_below_seating_plane, an electro_magnetic_compatibility_analysis, a shock_analysis, a thermal_analysis, or a vibration_analysis, as the purpose for the Physical_unit_planar_shape.

Formal constraints:

WR1: If the shape_characterized_physical_unit attribute specifies a Bare_die, or Package, and the shape_environment is equal to "manufacturing" and the shape_purpose is equal to "design" then the planar shape shall be oriented parallel to the seating plane and the distance to the seating plane shall be greater than any point in the volume of the Physical_unit.

NOTE: This rule provides a shape representation useful for the top view of an item as may be seen in an assembly.

ENTITY physical_unit_planar_shape
  SUBTYPE OF (planar_projected_shape);
    application_technology_constraint : OPTIONAL ee_requirement_occurrence;
    shape_characterized_physical_unit : physical_unit;
    shape_distance_from_seating_plane : OPTIONAL length_data_element;
    shape_location_with_respect_to_seating_plane: shape_location;
    shape_material_condition          : material_condition;
    centroid_location                 : OPTIONAL cartesian_point;
    shape_environment                 : environment;
    shape_purpose                     : planar_purpose;
WHERE
 WR1: NOT('AP210_ARM.PACKAGED_PART' IN TYPEOF(shape_characterized_physical_unit));   
END_ENTITY; -- physical_unit_planar_shape
