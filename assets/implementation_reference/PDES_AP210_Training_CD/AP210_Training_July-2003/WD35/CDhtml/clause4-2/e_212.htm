4.2.212 Date

NOTE: The text description for this Application Object has not been fully updated to agree with the EXPRESS.
A Date is a type of Managed_design_object. A Date may be a Date_and_time. The Date is the day of the calendar.

The data associated with this application object are the following:

  • day
  • month
  • year

4.2.212.1 day

The day specifies the integer for the day of the month for the Date.

4.2.212.2 month

The month specifies the integer for the month representation for the Date.

NOTE: These integers range between 1 and 12, inclusively.

4.2.212.3 year

The year specifies the integer for the year of the Date.

Formal constraints:

WR1: The year shall be a four digit positive integer.

ENTITY date
  SUPERTYPE OF (date_and_time)
  SUBTYPE OF (managed_design_object);
    day   : INTEGER;
    year  : INTEGER;
    month : month_representation;
