4.2.659 Physical_laminate_component

A Physical_laminate_component is a type of Laminate_component and a type of Physical_feature_or_part_template. The Physical_laminate_component supports the specification of a Laminate_component as a Datum_target or Datum_feature for the purpose of estabishing a Datum_reference_frame for tolerance specification. A Physical_laminate_component shall be in a complex instance with another member of the subtype tree of Laminate_component and Physical_feature_or_part_template. The interpretation shall be in compliance with the interpretation as if Physical_laminate_component were Abstract.

NOTE: ISO 10303-11 does not permit Physical_laminate_component to be Abstract. Therefore this requirement is encoded in a rule.
