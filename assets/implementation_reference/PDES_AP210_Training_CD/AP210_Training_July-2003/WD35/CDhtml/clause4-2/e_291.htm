4.2.298 Dimensional_characteristic

NOTE: The text description for this Application Object has not been fully updated to agree with the EXPRESS.
A Dimensional_characteristic is a type of Managed_design_object. A Dimensional_characteristic is either a Dimensional_location or a Dimensional_size that characterizes either the spatial constraints between two Shape_element or the size of a Shape_element. Additionally, a Dimensional_characteristic may be a Statistical_dimensional_tolerance.

The data associated with this application object are the following:

  • dimension_type

4.2.298.1 dimension_type

The dimension_type specifies the label by which the Dimensional_characteristic is known. The dimension_type shall be one of "angular", "arc_length", "controlled_radius", "diameter", "linear", or "radius".

NOTE: A description of controlled radius is given in 2.15.2 of ASME Y14.5M.

Formal constraints:

WR1: The Dimensional_characteristic shall be specified as the dimension by at most one Dimensional_characteristic_representation that specifies a value of FALSE for the auxiliary_dimension_representation.

WR2: If the dimension_type is "angular" and the Dimensional_characteristic is represented by a Shape_dimension_representation that specifies one or more Data_element, each Data_element shall be an Angle_data_element.

WR3: The Dimensional_characteristic shall either be a Dimensional_size or a Dimensional_location.

WR4: If the dimension_type is other than "angular" and the Dimensional_characteristic is represented by a Shape_dimension_representation that specifies one or more Data_element, each Data_element shall be a Length_data_element.

WR5: There shall be exactly one Ee_requirement_occurrence with the description of "Geometric Dimensioning and Tolerancing Reference" that specifies at least one Ee_specification as the requirement_specification.

Informal constraints:

IP1: There shall be exactly one Ee_requirement_occurrence with the description of "Geometric Dimensioning and Tolerancing Reference" that specifies at least one Ee_specification as the requirement_specification. The Ee_specification shall reference those standards by which the geometric dimensioning and tolerancing data is to be interpreted.

ENTITY dimensional_characteristic
  ABSTRACT SUPERTYPE OF (ONEOF (dimensional_location,dimensional_size) 
      ANDOR statistical_dimensional_tolerance)
  SUBTYPE OF (managed_design_object);
    dimension_type : dimensional_types;
END_ENTITY; -- dimensional_characteristic
