4.2.543 Measure_qualification

NOTE: The text description for this Application Object has not been fully updated to agree with the EXPRESS.
A Measure_qualification is a type of Managed_design_object in which there is an associated uncertainty. The uncertainty may be specified as precision, qualitative uncertainty, or expanded uncertainty, and the type of the related datum shall be included. The uncertainty is defined in clause 2 of "The Guide to the Expression of Uncertainty in Measurement".

             name : text;

END_ENTITY; -- measure_qualification
