4.2.594 Packaged_part_terminal

NOTE: The text description for this Application Object has not been fully updated to agree with the EXPRESS.
A Packaged_part_terminal is a type of Part_terminal that describes a terminal of a Packaged_part. A Packaged_part_terminal is one of a Packaged_part_interface_terminal or a Packaged_part_join_terminal.

The data associated with this application object are the following:

  • associated_definition
  • terminal_connection_zone
  • terminal_of_package

4.2.594.1 associated_definition

The associated_definition specifies the Packaged_part that has the Packaged_part_terminal.

4.2.594.2 terminal_connection_zone

The terminal_connection_zone specifies the Connection_zone of the Packaged_part_terminal. There may be more than one Connection_zone for a Packaged_part_terminal. The terminal_connection_zone need not be specified for a particular Packaged_part_terminal.

4.2.594.3 terminal_of_package

The terminal_of_package specifies the role of either the Package_terminal or the Package_terminal_external_referencefor the Packaged_part_terminal. There may be more than one terminal_of_package. If there is more than one terminal_of_package then the member reference different Package members.

Formal constraints:

WR1: Each member of terminal_of_package shall specify a member of Package that is specified by the Packaged_part in the role of associated_definition as used_package. No member of Package that is specified by the Packaged_part in the role of a ssociated_definition as used_package shall be specified by more than one member of terminal_of_package.

WR2: The precedent_feature attribute inherited from Part_feature shall not exist.

  SUBTYPE OF (part_terminal);

  WR1: disjoint_package_terminals(SELF);
  WR2: NOT EXISTS(SELF\part_feature.precedent_feature);
