4.2.234 Datum_precedence_assignment

NOTE: The text description for this Application Object has not been fully updated to agree with the EXPRESS.
A Datum_precedence_assignment is a type of Managed_design_object. A Datum_precedence_assignment is a specification that defines the order in which a Datum is used to establish a Datum_system.

NOTE: Datum systems established by a sequence of datums is discussed in 6.2.3 of ISO 5459.

The data associated with this application object are the following:

  • assigned_to
  • datum_system_conditions_definition
  • name

4.2.234.1 assigned_to

The assigned_to specifies the Datum_usage_in_datum_system_relationship that associates a Datum_system with the Datum to which the precedence in establishing the Datum_system is assigned by the Datum_precedence_assignment.

4.2.234.2 datum_system_conditions_definition

The datum_system_conditions_definition is an inverse relationship that specifies that the existence of the Datum_precedence_assignment depends on the existence of the Datum_system_defining_conditions that specifies the Datum_precedence_assignment as its assigned_datum_precedences.

4.2.234.3 name

The name specifies the label that corresponds to the Datum_precedence_assignment of the specified usage of a Datum in a Datum_system. The name shall be "primary", "secondary", or "tertiary".

EXAMPLE: The requirements may be such that Datum A is assigned as the primary Datum when it is used in one Datum_system. However, additional requirements may be such that Datum A is assigned as the tertiary Datum when it is used in a second Datum_system.

Formal constraints:

UR1: The combination of name and datum_system_conditions_definition shall be unique within a population of Datum_precedence_assignment. That is, the defining conditions of a Datum_system shall be such that no two Datum of the Datum_system have been assigned the same precedence.

ENTITY datum_precedence_assignment
  SUBTYPE OF (managed_design_object);
    assigned_to : datum_usage_in_datum_system_relationship;
    name        : precedence_type;
  INVERSE
    datum_system_conditions_definition : datum_system_defining_conditions FOR 
                                         assigned_datum_precedences;
  UNIQUE
    ur1 : datum_system_conditions_definition, name;
END_ENTITY; -- datum_precedence_assignment
