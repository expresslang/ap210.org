4.2.71 Blind_via

NOTE: The text description for this Application Object has not been fully updated to agree with the EXPRESS.
A Blind_via is a type of Via. Each Blind_via is either a Bonded_conductive_base_blind_via, a Non_conductive_base_blind_via, or a Plated_conductive_base_blind_via . Blind_via is defined in IEC 60050-541.

EXAMPLE: Figure bv illustrates an idealized cross-section of an interconnect substrate, showing the depth of penetration of a Blind_via into the substrate.

Formal constraints:

WR1: Only one end of a Blind_via shall be at a surface of the Interconnect_module.

  SUBTYPE OF (via);
