4.2.118 Component_feature

NOTE: The text description for this Application Object has not been fully updated to agree with the EXPRESS.
A Component_feature is a type of Managed_design_object. A Component_feature may be one of a Component_interface_terminal, a Component_terminal, a Component_termination_passage_interface_terminal, a Land_interface_terminal, a Non_functional_land_interface_terminal, or a Printed_connector_component_interface_terminal, and in addition may be a Physical_component_feature, and in addition may be an Interconnect_module_component_surface_feature. The location of the Component_feature in the assembly model is derived from the placement of the associated_component in the assembly model and the placement of the definition model in the usage_definition model of that associated_component.

The data associated with this application object are the following:

  • associated_component
  • defined_product_feature
  • definition

4.2.118.1 associated_component

The associated_component specifies the role of the Assembly_component for the Component_feature.

NOTE: The subtypes of Component_feature specify specializations of Assembly_component that are consistent with the subtype of definition.

4.2.118.2 defined_product_feature

The defined_product_feature is an inverse relationship that specifies that there shall be no more than one Component_feature_to_physical_usage_view_assignment that specifies the Component_feature as its assigned_design_object. The defined_product_feature need not be specified for a particular Component_feature.

4.2.118.3 definition

The definition specifies either a Component_termination_passage_template_terminal, a Land_template_terminal, a Part_feature, a Part_terminal_external_reference, a Printed_part_template_terminal, or a Via_template_terminal for this Component_feature. The definition need not be specified for a particular Component_feature.

Formal constraints:

UR1: The combination of the definition and the associated_component shall be unique within a population of Component_features.

WR1: The Physical_unit or Part_template specified by the associated_component as its usage_definition shall be the same Physical_unit or Part_template specified by the definition as its associated_definition.

WR2: For each Component_feature there shall be no more than one Assembly_joint that specifies it as an assembly_feature.

NOTE: Parameters may be assigned to Component_feature by the Planned_characteristic object through the Assembly_requirements_allocation.
