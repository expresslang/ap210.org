4.2.300 Dimensional_location

NOTE: The text description for this Application Object has not been fully updated to agree with the EXPRESS.
A Dimensional_location is a type of Dimensional_characteristic that defines the location of a Shape_element relative to another Shape_element. A Dimensional_location may be one of an Angular_dimension_with_direction_vector, a Dimension_along_curve, or a Dimension_location_with_direction_vector. Additionally, the Dimensional_location may be a Dimension_location_with_specified_datum_system.

The data associated with this application object are the following:

  • directed
  • located_elements

4.2.300.1 directed

The directed specifies the boolean value that indicates whether there is significance to the direction of measure of the Dimensional_location. A value of TRUE indicates that the measurement direction shall be from the Shape_element specified as the locating_shape_element to the Shape_element specified as the located_shape_element by the Shape_element_locating_relationship that is characterized by the Dimensional_location. A value of FALSE indicates that there is no significance placed on the direction of measure.

4.2.300.2 located_elements

The located_elements specifies the Shape_element_locating_relationship that specifies the two Shape_elements for which the relationship is characterized by the Dimensional_location.

Formal constraints:

WR1: The dimension_type of the Dimensional_location shall not be "diameter".

NOTE: The attribute dimension_type is declared in the Dimensional_characteristic entity of which this entity is a subtype.

END_ENTITY; -- dimensional_location
