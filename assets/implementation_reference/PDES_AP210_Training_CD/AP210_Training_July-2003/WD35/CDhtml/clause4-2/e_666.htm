4.2.666 Physical_unit_3d_shape

NOTE: The text description for this Application Object has not been fully updated to agree with the EXPRESS.
A Physical_unit_3d_shape is a type of Bound_volume_shape and a type of Feature_shape_definition. A Physical_unit_3d_shape is the shape in three orthogonal dimensions of a Physical_unit. A Physical_unit_3d_shape may be included for purposes which include the actual shape or boundaries of the actual shape. A Physical_unit shall not be referenced by a Physical_unit_3d_shape.

NOTES:

  1. When a extreme boundary of the actual shape is provided, mass property calculations based on the extreme shape may be invalid, depending on the purpose. For example, the centroid_location in particular may not be valid for an extreme shape representing the envelope of a mult-mode vibration since the centroid may also vary over a range.
  2. The shape for a Packaged_part is provided by a Package, eliminating the requirement to support a shape for a Packaged_part separately.

The data associated with this application object are the following:

  • application_technology_constraint
  • centroid_location
  • shape_characterized_physical_unit
  • shape_environment
  • shape_material_condition
  • shape_purpose
  • shape_representation

4.2.666.1 application_technology_constraint

The application_technology_constraint specifies the role of Ee_requirement_occurrence for the Physical_unit_3d_shape. The application_technology_constraint is the relationship between physical shapes amd process constraints under which those physical shapes are valid.

4.2.666.2 centroid_location

The centroid_location is a Cartesian_point which is the center of a Physical_unit_3d_shape. A centroid_location need not be specified for a particular Physical_unit_3d_shape.

4.2.666.3 shape_characterized_physical_unit

The shape_characterized_physical_unit specifies the role of the Physical_unit for the Physical_unit_3d_shape.

4.2.666.4 shape_environment

The shape_environment specifies either manufacturing or an end_user_application as the environment for the Physical_unit_3d_shape.

4.2.666.5 shape_material_condition

The shape_material_condition specifies either the maximum_material_condition, the minimum_material_condition, or the nominal_material_condition as the material condition for the Physical_unit_3d_shape.

4.2.666.6 shape_purpose

The shape_purpose specifies either a design, a design_profile, a design_profile_above_seating_plane, a design_profile_below_seating_plane, an electro_magnetic_compatibility_analysis, a shock_analysis, a thermal_analysis, or a vibration_analysis as the purpose for the Physical_unit_3d_shape.

4.2.666.7 shape_representation

The shape_representation specifies the role of the Bound_volume_shape for the Physical_unit_3d_shape.

ENTITY physical_unit_3d_shape
  SUBTYPE OF (bound_volume_shape,feature_shape_definition);
    application_technology_constraint : OPTIONAL ee_requirement_occurrence;
    shape_characterized_physical_unit : physical_unit;
    shape_material_condition          : material_condition;
    shape_purpose                     : three_d_purpose;
    shape_environment                 : environment;
    centroid_location                 : OPTIONAL cartesian_point;
WHERE
  WR1: NOT('AP210_ARM.PACKAGED_PART' IN TYPEOF(shape_characterized_physical_unit));
END_ENTITY; -- physical_unit_3d_shape
