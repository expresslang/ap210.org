4.2.157 Composite_feature_locating_position_tolerance

NOTE: The text description for this Application Object has not been fully updated to agree with the EXPRESS.
A Composite_feature_locating_position_tolerance is a type of Position_tolerance_with_referenced_datum_system. The Composite_feature_locating_position_tolerance is used in conjunction with one to three Composite_feature_relating_position_tolerance to apply a less restrictive specification on the location of a Shape_element group with respect to one or more Datum than on the location and orientation of those Shape_element within the group with respect to each other. The Tolerance_zone defined by the Composite_feature_locating_position_tolerance is located relative to the Datum_system the definition of which is specified as the datum_system_definition.

NOTES:

  1. The attribute datum_system_definition is declared in the Geometric_tolerance_with_referenced_datum_system entity of which this entity is a subtype.
  2. Composite positional tolerancing is discussed in 5.4.1 of ASME Y14.5M.

Formal constraints:

WR1: The Composite_feature_locating_position_tolerance shall not also be a Composite_feature_relating_position_tolerance.

WR2: The Composite_feature_locating_position_tolerance shall be specified as the refined_control by exactly one Composite_feature_relating_position_tolerance.

WR3: The Shape_element that is specified as the toleranced_shape_element shall be a Composite_group_shape_element.

NOTE: The attribute toleranced_shape_element is declared in the Geometric_tolerance entity of which this entity is a subtype.
