4.2.220 Datum_based_vector_orientation

NOTE: The text description for this Application Object has not been fully updated to agree with the EXPRESS.
A Datum_based_vector_orientation is a type of Managed_design_object. A Datum_based_vector_orientation is a definition of the orientation of a shape element that has the form of a vector and is based on the intersection of two shape elements that have the form of half-planes. Where a half-plane has the form of a plane except it is bounded on one side by a straight line. Each half-plane shape element is oriented with respect to three Datum_plane and is defined by a Direction_element.

The data associated with this application object are the following:

  • direction_components

4.2.220.1 direction_components

The direction_components specifies the Direction_element that defines the half-plane shape elements the intersection of which forms the vector that is defined by the Datum_based_vector_orientation. There shall be exactly two Direction_element for the Datum_based_vector_orientation.

Formal constraints:

WR1: The Datum_based_vector_orientation shall be specified as the zone_orientation by at least one Tolerance_zone_definition_with_specified_orientation, or as the measurement_direction by at least one Dimension_location_with_direction_vector, or as the measurement_orientation by at least one Angular_dimension_with_direction_vector.

WR2: The Datum_planes specified as the base_datum_plane by the two Direction_element specified as direction_components shall be different.

WR3: The Datum_planes specified as the base_datum_plane by the two Direction_element specified as direction_components shall be Datum_planes of the same Datum_reference_frame.
