4.2.642 Pcb

NOTE: The text description for this Application Object has not been fully updated to agree with the EXPRESS.
A Pcb is a type of Interconnect_module. A Pcb describes a printed circuit board. A printed circuit board is nearly a synonym for printed wiring board, except that a printed circuit board may include printed components and a printed wiring board is totally interconnected without printed components. This part of ISO 10303 supports the exchange of product categories in order to allow the exchange partners to differentiate between printed wiring board and printed circuit board.

EXAMPLES:

  1. Figure pcb illustrates a printed wiring board design.
  2. Figures pcbcir and pcbcom illustrate complex printed wiring board mechanical designs that have functional shape requirements that are communicated using Geometric Dimensioning and Tolerancing information in a product model and not graphically.

The data associated with this application object are the following:

  • usage_view

4.2.642.1 usage_view

The usage_view specifies the role of the Pcb_usage_view for the Pcb. The usage_view shall not be specified if the Pcb is a Layout_macro_definition, otherwise it shall exist.

NOTE: 
*)
ENTITY pcb
  SUPERTYPE OF (layout_macro_definition)
  SUBTYPE OF (interconnect_module);
    SELF\physical_unit_design_view.usage_view : OPTIONAL pcb_usage_view;
WHERE
  WR1: ('AP210_ARM.LAYOUT_MACRO_DEFINITION' IN TYPEOF(SELF)) XOR
       (EXISTS(SELF\physical_unit_design_view.usage_view));
END_ENTITY; -- pcb
