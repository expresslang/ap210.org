4.2.585 Packaged_component

NOTE: The text description for this Application Object has not been fully updated to agree with the EXPRESS.
A Packaged_component is a type of Assembly_component. A Packaged_component may be a Packaged_connector_component or additionally a Routed_packaged_component. A Packaged_component is an instantiation of a Packaged_part at a given location within the context of a printed circuit assembly.

The data associated with this application object are the following:

  • access_mechanism
  • selected_package_alternate
  • usage_definition

4.2.585.1 access_mechanism

The access_mechanism is an inverse relationship that specifies that the existence of the Packaged_component is dependent on the existence of the Packaged_component_join_terminal that specifies the Packaged_component as its associated_component. There shall be one or more Packaged_component_join_terminal for a Packaged_component.

4.2.585.2 selected_package_alternate

The selected_package_alternate specifies the role of the Altered_package for the Packaged_component. The selected_package_alternate need not be specified for a particular Packaged_component.

4.2.585.3 usage_definition

The usage_definition specifies the role of the Packaged_part for the Packaged_component.

Formal constraints:

WR1: If selected_package_alternate exists, then the selected_package_alternate shall be in the role of used_package for the Packaged_part in the role of usage_definition for the Packaged_component.
