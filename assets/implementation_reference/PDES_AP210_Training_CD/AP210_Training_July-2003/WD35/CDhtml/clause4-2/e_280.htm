4.2.280 Design_specification

NOTE: The text description for this Application Object has not been fully updated to agree with the EXPRESS.
A Design_specification is a type of Ee_specification that establishes the design specifications of products. A Design_specification may be an Interface_specification. These specifications are not determined by other design or construction features or by referenced data.

NOTE: 
*)
ENTITY design_specification
  SUPERTYPE OF (interface_specification)
  SUBTYPE OF (ee_specification);
END_ENTITY; -- design_specification
