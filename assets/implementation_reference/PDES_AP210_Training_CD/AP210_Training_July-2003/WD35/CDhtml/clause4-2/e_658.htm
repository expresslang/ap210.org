4.2.658 Physical_feature_or_part_template

NOTE: The text description for this Application Object has not been fully updated to agree with the EXPRESS.
A Physical_feature_or_part_template is a type of Shape_element that is either on the physical boundary of a Physical_unit or is a Part_template. A Physical_feature_or_part_template is one of a Part_feature, a Physical_component_feature, a Physical_laminate_component, a Part_template, or a Stratum_feature. Additionally, a Physical_feature_or_part_template may either be a Datum_feature or a Datum_target. The Physical_feature_or_part_template is an intersection application object that supports assignemt of Geometric Dimensioning and Tolerance data to features or template definitions.

Informal constraints:

IP1: If the Physical_feature_or_part_template is not a Part_template it shall be on the boundary of the Physical_unit.

  SUBTYPE OF (shape_element);
