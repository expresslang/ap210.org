4.2.168 Concentricity_and_coaxiality_tolerance

NOTE: The text description for this Application Object has not been fully updated to agree with the EXPRESS.
A Concentricity_and_coaxiality_tolerance is a type of Geometric_tolerance_with_referenced_datum_system that specifies or partially specifies the design requirement on either the concentricity or the coaxiality of the toleranced Shape_element in relation to a Datum. Coaxiality is the quality or state of being coaxial.

NOTE: Concentricity and coaxiality tolerance is defined in terms of tolerance zone definitions in 14.11 of ISO 1101.

Formal constraints:

WR1: The Shape_element specified as the toleranced_shape_element shall be either a Centre_axis or a Centre_point.

NOTE: The attribute toleranced_shape_element is declared in the Geometric_tolerance entity of which this entity is a subtype.

WR2: There shall be exactly one Datum in the Datum_system the definition of which is specified as the datum_system_definition.

NOTE: The attribute datum_system_definition is declared in the Geometric_tolerance_with_referenced_datum_system entity of which this entity is a subtype.

WR3: If the Shape_element specified as the toleranced_shape_element is a Centre_point, the Datum of the Datum_system the definition of which is specified as the datum_system_definition shall be a Datum_point.

WR4: If the Shape_element specified as the toleranced_shape_element is a Centre_axis, the Datum of the Datum_system the definition of which is specified as the datum_system_definition shall be a Datum_axis.

WR5: The Concentricity_and_coaxiality_tolerance shall specify a Tolerance_zone_definition as its tolerance_zone_or_boundary_definition; and that Tolerance_zone_definition shall specify a Tolerance_zone_boundary_definition_with_specified_size as its boundary_definition; and that Tolerance_zone_boundary_definition_with_specified_size shall specify a Diametrical_boundary_size_characteristic as its specified_boundary_size.

Informal constraints:

IP1: The form of the toleranced Shape_element shall be consistent with the form of a shape element for which the application of a concentricity and coaxiality tolerance is sensible.
