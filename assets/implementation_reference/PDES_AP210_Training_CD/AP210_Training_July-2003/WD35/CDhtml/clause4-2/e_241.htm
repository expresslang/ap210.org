4.2.241 Datum_target_set

NOTE: The text description for this Application Object has not been fully updated to agree with the EXPRESS.
A Datum_target_set is a type of Datum_feature that is composed of one or more Datum_target.

The data associated with this application object are the following:

  • included_datum_targets

4.2.241.1 included_datum_targets

The included_datum_targets is an inverse relationship that specifies that the existence of the Datum_target_set is dependent on the existence of the Datum_target_usage_in_datum_target_set_relationship that specifies the Datum_target_set as its defined_datum_target_set. There shall be one or more Datum_target_usage_in_datum_target_set_relationship for the Datum_target_set.

Formal constraints:

WR1: A Datum_target_set shall not participate as a related_shape_element in a Shape_element_constituent_relationship. That is, a Datum_target_set shall not be a constituent of another Shape_element.
