4.2.245 Default_attachment_size_and_component_termination_passage_based_land_physical_template

A Default_attachment_size_and_component_termination_passage_based_land_physical_template is a type of Default_attachment_size_based_land_physical_template and of Default_component_termination_passage_based_land_physical_template. The shape of this template is dependent on both the requirements from the passage definition and the requirement for support of the interface between the instance of the Part_terminal intended to mate with an instance of the Land_physical_template.
