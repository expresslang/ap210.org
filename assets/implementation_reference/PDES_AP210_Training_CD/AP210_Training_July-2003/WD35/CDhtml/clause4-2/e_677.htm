4.2.677 Planar_projected_shape

NOTE: The text description for this Application Object has not been fully updated to agree with the EXPRESS.
A Planar_projected_shape is a type of Planar_shape. A Planar_projected_shape may be one of an Assembly_component_2d_shape, a Design_intent_modification_planar_shape, a Part_template_planar_shape, a Physical_unit_planar_shape, a Stratum_feature_planar_shape, or a Stratum_planar_shape. A Planar_projected_shape is also a type of Feature_shape_definition. A Planar_projected_shape is projected from a solid. A Planar_projected_shape that is an Assembly_component_2d_shape may also be either a Part_template_planar_shape or a Physical_unit_planar_shape.

NOTE: A Planar_shape may also be projected from a solid to handle the case where a Non_feature_shape_definition is needed that is not predefined in this standard. The predefined non feature shape projections included in this standard are Physical_unit_3d_keepout_shape and Physical_unit_planar_keepout_shape.

The data associated with this application object are the following:

  • curve_basis

4.2.677.1 curve_basis

The curve_basis is an inverse relationship that specifies Volume_shape_projection that specifies the Planar_projected_shape as its surface_projection. There shall be no more than one Volume_shape_projection for a Planar_projected_shape.

ENTITY planar_projected_shape
  SUPERTYPE OF(ONEOF(
  (assembly_component_2d_shape ANDOR(ONEOF(
  part_template_planar_shape,
  footprint_definition_shape,
  padstack_definition_shape,
  physical_unit_planar_shape))),
  design_intent_modification_planar_shape,
  stratum_feature_planar_shape,
  stratum_planar_shape))
  SUBTYPE OF (planar_shape, feature_shape_definition);
  INVERSE
    curve_basis_1 : SET [0:1] OF volume_shape_plane_projection FOR surface_projection_result;
    curve_basis_2 : SET [0:1] OF volume_shape_intersection FOR surface_intersection_result;
WHERE
 WR1: EXISTS (curve_basis_1) XOR EXISTS (curve_basis_2);
END_ENTITY; -- planar_projected_shape
