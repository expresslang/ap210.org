4.2.178 Conical_tolerance_zone_boundary

NOTE: The text description for this Application Object has not been fully updated to agree with the EXPRESS.
A Conical_tolerance_zone_boundary is a type of Tolerance_zone_boundary that is conical in form. The shape of the Conical_tolerance_zone_boundary is defined by a frustum the size of which is specified at the surfaces that define the ends of the frustum.

The data associated with this application object are the following:

  • boundary_and_surface_relationships

4.2.178.1 boundary_and_surface_relationships

The boundary_and_surface_relationships is an inverse relationship that specifies that the existence of the Conical_tolerance_zone_boundary is dependent on the existence of two Conical_tolerance_zone_boundary_and_surface_relationship that specify the Conical_tolerance_zone_boundary as their sized_tolerance_zone_boundary. There shall be exactly two Conical_tolerance_zone_boundary_and_surface_relationship for a Conical_tolerance_zone_boundary.
