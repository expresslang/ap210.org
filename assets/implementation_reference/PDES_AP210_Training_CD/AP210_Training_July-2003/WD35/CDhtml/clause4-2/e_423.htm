4.2.423 Geometric_tolerance_without_referenced_datum_system

NOTE: The text description for this Application Object has not been fully updated to agree with the EXPRESS.
A Geometric_tolerance_without_referenced_datum_system is a type of Geometric_tolerance that specifies or partially specifies the design requirement on one or more Datum_system independent geometric characteristics such as flatness of the Shape_element to which the Geometric_tolerance_without_referenced_datum_system is applied. A Geometric_tolerance_without_referenced_datum_system is one of a Circularity_tolerance, a Cylindricity_tolerance, a Flatness_tolerance, a Position_tolerance_without_referenced_datum_system, a Profile_of_any_line_tolerance_without_referenced_datum_system, a Profile_of_any_surface_tolerance_without_referenced_datum_system, or a Straightness_tolerance.
