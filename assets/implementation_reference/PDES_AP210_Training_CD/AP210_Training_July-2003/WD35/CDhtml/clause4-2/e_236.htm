4.2.236 Datum_system

NOTE: The text description for this Application Object has not been fully updated to agree with the EXPRESS.
A Datum_system is a type of Non_feature_shape_element that is a system of Datum that establish a Datum_reference_frame by which toleranced Shape_element may be located and oriented.

EXAMPLE: Figure df illustrates a Datum_system.

NOTE: The definition of datum-system is given in 3.2 of ISO 5459.

The data associated with this application object are the following:

  • datum_usages
  • defining_conditions
  • established_datum_reference_frame

4.2.236.1 datum_usages

The datum_usages is an inverse relationship that specifies that the existence of the Datum_system is dependent on the existence of the Datum_usage_in_datum_system_relationship that specifies the Datum_system as its established_datum_system. There shall be one, two, or three Datum_usage_in_datum_system_relationship for the Datum_system.

4.2.236.2 defining_conditions

The defining_conditions is an inverse relationship that specifies that the existence of the Datum_system is dependent on the existence of the Datum_system_defining_conditions that specifies the Datum_system as its conditions_of.

4.2.236.3 established_datum_reference_frame

The established_datum_reference_frame specifies the Datum_reference_frame that is established by the Datum_system.

Formal constraints:

WR1: Any particular Datum_feature shall be used no more than one time to establish the Datum_system.

WR2: Any particular Datum_target shall be used no more than one time to establish the Datum_system.

ENTITY datum_system
  SUBTYPE OF (non_feature_shape_element);
    established_datum_reference_frame : datum_reference_frame;
  INVERSE
    datum_usages        : SET [1:3] OF
                          datum_usage_in_datum_system_relationship FOR 
                          established_datum_system;
    defining_conditions : datum_system_defining_conditions FOR conditions_of;
END_ENTITY; -- datum_system
