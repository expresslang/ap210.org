4.2.292 Dimension_location_with_direction_vector

NOTE: The text description for this Application Object has not been fully updated to agree with the EXPRESS.
A Dimension_location_with_direction_vector is a type of Dimensional_location in which the characterization of the relationship between the two Shape_element is dependent on a vector that establishes a measurement direction.

The data associated with this application object are the following:

  • measurement_direction

4.2.292.1 measurement_direction

The measurement_direction either specifies the Datum_based_vector_orientation or the Axis_placement that establishes the measurement direction. If the measurement_direction specifies a Datum_based_vector_orientation, the measurement direction is established from Datum. If the measurement_direction specifies an Axis_placement, the measurement direction is based on the Axis_placement and the representation of the Shape_elements whose relationship is characterized by the Dimension_location_with_direction_vector.

Formal constraints:

UR1: The combination of located_elements inherited from Dimensional_location and measurement_direction shall be unique within a population of Dimension_location_with_direction_vector.

NOTE: The attribute located_elements is declared in the Dimensional_location entity of which this entity is a subtype.

WR1: The dimension_type of the Dimension_location_with_direction_vector shall be "linear".

NOTE: The attribute dimension_type is declared in the Dimensional_characteristic entity of which this entity is a subtype.

Informal constraints:

IP1: If the measurement_direction specifies an Axis_placement, the Axis_placement shall be in the same context as the representation of the two Shape_elements that are located relative to each other by the Dimension_location_with_direction_vector.
