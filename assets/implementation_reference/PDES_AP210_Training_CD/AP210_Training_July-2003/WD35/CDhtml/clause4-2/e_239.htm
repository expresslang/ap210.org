4.2.238 Datum_system_defining_conditions_with_material_conditions

NOTE: The text description for this Application Object has not been fully updated to agree with the EXPRESS.
A Datum_system_defining_conditions_with_material_conditions is a type of Datum_system_defining_conditions that specifies the conditions under which a Datum_system is established and in which either the "least_material_requirement" or the "maximum_material_principle" has been applied to usages of one or more Datum_feature in the Datum_system.

NOTE: If neither the "least_material_requirement" nor the "maximum_material_principle" is explicitly applied and the Datum_feature is a feature of size, the principle that is termed "regardless of feature size" in ASME Y14.5M is assumed to be in effect.

The data associated with this application object are the following:

  • applied_material_condition_properties

4.2.238.1 applied_material_condition_properties

The applied_material_condition_properties specify the Datum_feature_material_condition_property that define the material condition properties that are explicitly applied to the Datum_feature in the context of the Datum_system for the Datum_system_defining_conditions_with_material_conditions. There shall be one or more Datum_feature_material_condition_property for a Datum_system_defining_conditions_with_material_conditions.

EXAMPLE: The requirements may be such that the "maximum material principle" applies to Datum_feature A when it is used in one Datum_system. However, additional requirements may be such that neither the "maximum material principle" nor the "least material requirement" applies to Datum_feature A when it is used in a second Datum_system.

Formal constraints:

WR1: The conditions_of the Datum_system_defining_conditions_with_material_conditions shall be the same instance of Datum_system as specified by the established_datum_system attribute of the Datum_feature_usage_in_datum_system_relationship that is specified by the applied_to attribute of the Datum_feature_material_condition_property specified by the applied_material_condition_properties. That is, the Datum_system_defining_conditions_with_material_conditions and the specified Datum_feature_material_condition_propertys shall be associated with the same Datum_system.
