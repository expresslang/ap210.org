= About AP 210

ISO 10303-210 (AP 210) is the international standard for representing and exchanging electronic assembly and interconnect design data.

It covers the full scope of electronic design from component packaging through printed circuit assembly (PCA), including:

* Physical layout and interconnect design
* Component placement and connectivity
* Design rule constraints
* Functional requirements capture
* Library and catalogue definitions

AP 210 is developed and maintained by ISO TC 184/SC 4, the same committee responsible for the STEP family of standards (ISO 10303).
