= About AP 210

ISO 10303-210 (AP 210) is the international standard for representing and exchanging electronic assembly and interconnect design data. It is part of the STEP family of standards (ISO 10303) developed by ISO Technical Committee 184, Subcommittee 4 (TC 184/SC 4).

== What AP 210 Covers

AP 210 defines an information model for the domain of electronic assembly, interconnect, and packaging design. It specifies data structures for:

* **Product structure** — how components, assemblies, and interconnect substrates are organized
* **Physical design** — footprint geometry, board outlines, layer stacks, and placement
* **Connectivity** — nets, interconnects, and signal paths between components
* **Design rules and constraints** — manufacturing rules, electrical constraints, and physical requirements
* **Library definitions** — component catalogs, package definitions, and land patterns
* **Functional requirements** — design intent, parameters, and electrical properties

== Why It Matters

Electronic design data exchange is critical for the electronics industry. EDA (Electronic Design Automation) tools from different vendors must interoperate throughout the design-to-manufacturing chain. AP 210 provides a vendor-neutral, standards-based format for this exchange.

Key benefits:

* **Interoperability** — exchange design data between different EDA tools without loss
* **Long-term archival** — preserve design data in a standardized, technology-independent format
* **Supply chain communication** — share design intent and manufacturing data across organizations
* **Regulatory compliance** — meet industry requirements for design data documentation

== Development History

AP 210 has been developed through four editions, each expanding scope to address evolving industry needs:

* *Edition 1 (2004)* — Initial scope for printed circuit assembly design
* *Edition 2 (2011)* — Advanced packaging and interconnect modeling
* *Edition 3 (2016)* — Design rules, constraints, and layer stack modeling
* *Edition 4 (2021)* — Current edition with comprehensive coverage

See the link:/standard[Standards] page for details on each edition.

== Research and Implementation

AP 210 has been the subject of significant collaborative research between the National Institute of Standards and Technology (NIST) and PDES Inc. This research has produced:

* 36 validated test cases
* Reference implementations in Java (JSDAI)
* 80+ standardized queries for extracting AP210 data
* Visualization tools and conversion utilities
