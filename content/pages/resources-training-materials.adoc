= Training Materials

Original PDES AP210 Training CD presentation slides from the July 2003 training program. These slides formed the basis of the structured <<{/learn#,learning modules>> available on this site.

== Training Programs

The training was organized across multiple programs on two DVDs:

=== DVD 1, Program 2: AP 210 Overview

* `ap210_overview.ppt` — Introduction to AP 210 scope and purpose

=== DVD 1, Program 3: AP 210 Detailed Overview

* `ap210_overview.ppt` — Detailed overview of the AP 210 information model

=== DVD 2, Program 1: Wheatstone Bridge Test Case

* `ap210_overview.ppt` — AP 210 overview with Wheatstone Bridge focus
* `Wheatstone-Bridge_Intro.ppt` — Introduction to the Wheatstone Bridge circuit example
* `Wheatstone_Bridge_Testcase.ppt` — Detailed walk-through of the test case
* `May_2003_EM_PILOT_TAC_rev1.ppt` — EM Pilot TAC review presentation

=== DVD 2, Program 2: Product Structure and Classification

* `Product_classification.ppt` — How products are classified in AP 210
* `Functional_product_structure.ppt` — Functional decomposition and product hierarchy
* `Product_structure_graph.ppt` — Product structure graph representation
* `Design_rules.ppt` — Design rules and constraints
* `Wheatstone_Bridge_Testcase.ppt` — Wheatstone Bridge reference

=== DVD 2, Program 3: Physical Design and Connectivity

* `Physical_product_structure.ppt` — Physical hierarchy of components and assemblies
* `Layout_connectivity.ppt` — Connectivity modeling in the layout view
* `Layout_physical_design.ppt` — Physical design representation on the layout
* `Physical_connectivity.ppt` — Physical connectivity between components
* `Requirements.ppt` — Requirements capture and representation
* `mapping_between_design_usage.ppt` — Mapping between design and usage views

=== DVD 2, Program 4: Library and Design Definitions

* `Building_a_library_definition.ppt` — Creating library entries for components
* `Building_a_design_definition.ppt` — Building complete design definitions

== Additional Information

* `Navigating_ap210.ppt` — How to navigate the AP 210 entity structure

== STEP Introduction Materials

The training CD also included 22 STEP introduction presentations covering STEP overview, EXPRESS language fundamentals, constrained objects, information modeling, STEP and PDES Inc. organization, Application Protocols, engineering frameworks, and business context.
