= AP210 Concepts

This module covers the core concepts of AP 210's information model. You should complete the link:/learn/foundations[STEP & EXPRESS Foundations] module first.

The AP 210 model covers the full scope of electronic assembly design data, from product classification through physical design to design rules and constraints.
